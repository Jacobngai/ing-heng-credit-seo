### FAQ Content (For Landing Pages & Blog Posts)

#### English FAQ
1. **What is the typical financing term for a Komatsu PC350 excavator?**
   - Answer: Financing terms for medium to heavy excavators like the PC350 typically range from 3 to 7 years (36-84 months). The term depends on whether the equipment is new or used, your business financials, and down payment amount. At Ing Heng Credit, we offer flexible terms tailored to your quarry or construction project cash flow.

2. **Can I finance a used Komatsu PC350 excavator?**
   - Answer: Yes! We specialize in financing both new and used heavy equipment. For used Komatsu PC350 excavators, we typically finance equipment up to 15 years old, depending on condition, maintenance history, and operational hours. Used excavators may have slightly higher interest rates but much lower monthly payments due to lower principal amounts.

3. **What down payment is required for excavator financing in Malaysia?**
   - Answer: Down payment requirements vary by equipment condition and your credit profile. Generally, we require 10-30% down payment for new excavators and 20-40% for used equipment. For established clients with strong financials, we may offer reduced down payment options. The Komatsu PC350 typically requires RM60,000-150,000 down payment depending on the variant.

4. **How long does excavator financing approval take at Ing Heng Credit?**
   - Answer: Our streamlined approval process typically takes 48-72 hours for complete applications. We understand that construction and quarry projects have tight timelines, so we prioritize fast turnaround. Once approved, documentation and disbursement can be completed within 5-7 business days.

5. **What documents are needed to apply for Komatsu excavator financing?**
   - Answer: Required documents include:
     - Company registration (SSM) and business license
     - Directors' IC copies
     - 6 months business bank statements
     - Latest financial statements/tax returns
     - Equipment quotation or valuation (for used equipment)
     - Project contracts or proof of income (for contractors)
     - Existing hire purchase statements (if any)

6. **Can I finance multiple pieces of equipment for my quarry operation?**
   - Answer: Absolutely! We offer portfolio financing for quarry operators and contractors needing multiple equipment pieces. Financing your PC350 excavator, dump trucks, and crushers together can streamline approval, reduce administrative burden, and potentially secure better overall rates. Contact us for a customized portfolio financing package.

7. **What happens if my excavator needs major repairs during the financing period?**
   - Answer: While the equipment serves as collateral, you maintain ownership and operational control. You're responsible for maintenance and repairs. We recommend comprehensive insurance coverage (usually required) that includes breakdown coverage. For major unexpected repairs, we can discuss payment restructuring options for established clients in good standing.

8. **Is balloon payment available for excavator financing?**
   - Answer: Yes, we offer balloon payment structures where you make lower monthly payments with a larger final payment. This is popular for project-based businesses expecting a large payment upon project completion. Typical balloon structures are 20-30% of the equipment value as final payment. Discuss your cash flow pattern with our team to structure the optimal payment plan.
