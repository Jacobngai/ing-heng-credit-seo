A money keyword has these characteristics:
1. **Commercial Intent:** User is looking to purchase/finance equipment
2. **Specific Equipment:** Mentions specific machinery (excavator, forklift, lorry)
3. **Action Words:** "financing", "loan", "hire purchase", "pembiayaan", "融资"
4. **Location Modifiers:** "Malaysia", "Klang", "Selangor", "Kuala Lumpur"
5. **Urgency Indicators:** "fast approval", "urgent", "emergency", "24 hour"
6. **Decision Stage:** User knows what they want, ready to take action

**Money Keyword = High Intent + Low Competition + High Conversion Rate**

## PART 6: FACEBOOK ADS VS SEO - COMPARATIVE ANALYSIS

## The Fundamental Difference

### Facebook Ads Model

**How It Works:**
1. Target audience based on demographics, interests, behaviors
2. Show ads to people browsing Facebook (interruptive)
3. Hope they're interested in equipment financing (usually not)
4. Capture 1-2% of viewers as leads
5. Convert 10-25% of leads to customers

**The Numbers:**
- 10,000 people see ad
- 300 people click (3% CTR) @ RM 2-5 per click = RM 600-1,500
- 30 people submit inquiry (10% conversion)
- 3-5 people become customers (10-15% closing rate)
- Cost: RM 600-1,500 for 3-5 customers
- Cost Per Acquisition (CPA): RM 120-500 per customer

**The Problem:**
- Showing ads to people who aren't actively looking
- Wasting impressions on uninterested audience
- Continuous ad spend required
- No compounding effect (stop ads = stop leads)

### SEO Money Keyword Model

**How It Works:**
1. Target people actively searching for equipment financing
2. Capture 50-80% of searchers as visitors (high CTR)
3. Convert 25-50% of visitors to leads (high intent)
4. Convert 30-60% of leads to customers (warm leads)

**The Numbers:**
- 1,000 people search "excavator financing Malaysia"
- 500-800 people visit website (50-80% CTR) = RM 0 cost
- 125-400 people submit inquiry (25-50% conversion)
- 38-240 people become customers (30-60% closing rate)
- Cost: RM 0 (after initial SEO investment)
- Cost Per Acquisition (CPA): RM 0 (after SEO payoff period)

**The Advantage:**
- Only reaching people ACTIVELY SEARCHING
- 100% of audience has commercial intent
- Compounding ROI (traffic grows over time)
- Passive, long-term traffic (no ad spend)
- Higher conversion rates (already interested)
