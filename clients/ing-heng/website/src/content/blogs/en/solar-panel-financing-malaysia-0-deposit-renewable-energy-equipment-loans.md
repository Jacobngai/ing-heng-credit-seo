## Frequently Asked Questions About Solar Panel Financing

### System Performance and Technology

**Q: How long do solar panel systems last and what warranties are included?**
A: Quality solar panels come with 20-25 year power production warranties and typically operate efficiently for 30+ years. Inverters have 10-25 year warranties depending on type and manufacturer.

**Q: What happens during cloudy weather or monsoon seasons?**
A: Solar systems still generate power during cloudy conditions, though at reduced capacity. Grid-tied systems automatically draw additional power as needed, while battery systems provide backup during outages.

**Q: Do solar panels require regular maintenance?**
A: Solar panels require minimal maintenance - primarily periodic cleaning and annual inspections. Most systems include monitoring that alerts to any performance issues or maintenance needs.

**Q: Can solar systems be expanded after initial installation?**
A: Yes, most solar installations can be expanded by adding additional panels and equipment. We provide expansion financing for businesses with growing energy needs.

**Q: How does net metering work in Malaysia?**
A: Net Energy Metering allows you to export excess solar power to the grid and receive credits that offset your electricity consumption during periods when your system isn’t producing enough power.

### Financing and Investment Questions

**Q: What is the typical payback period for solar panel financing?**
A: Solar system payback periods typically range from 4-8 years depending on energy usage, system size, financing terms, and local electricity rates. After payback, systems generate pure savings.

**Q: Can I claim tax deductions for financed solar equipment?**
A: Solar equipment may qualify for capital allowances and green technology incentives. Consult with tax professionals for specific advice on your situation and applicable benefits.

**Q: What happens if I sell my property before the loan is paid off?**
A: Solar installations typically increase property value and can be transferred to new owners or paid off through sale proceeds. We can discuss options during the financing application process.

**Q: Do you finance solar installations for rental properties?**
A: Yes, we finance solar installations for rental and investment properties. Reduced operating costs improve property cash flow and tenant attraction in competitive markets.

**Q: Can businesses claim solar installations as business expenses?**
A: Solar installations are typically capital purchases eligible for depreciation and capital allowances rather than immediate expense deductions. Professional tax advice is recommended for optimal structuring.
