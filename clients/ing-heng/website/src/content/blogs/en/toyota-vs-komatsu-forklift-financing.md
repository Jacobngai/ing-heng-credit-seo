            Which Forklift Should You Choose?
          

          
            
              

#### Choose Toyota If:


              

- • You prioritize safety features and accident prevention
- • You operate high-frequency indoor warehouses (Shah Alam, Bukit Raja)
- • You plan long-term ownership (5+ years) and want higher resale value
- • You want to minimize downtime with reliable equipment
- • Budget allows 10-20% premium for lower long-term costs


            
            
              

#### Choose Komatsu If:


              

- • You need to minimize upfront capital expenditure
- • You operate mixed indoor/outdoor environments
- • You require heavy-duty lifting capacity
- • You have strong local Komatsu dealer support
- • You plan shorter ownership cycles (3-5 years)
