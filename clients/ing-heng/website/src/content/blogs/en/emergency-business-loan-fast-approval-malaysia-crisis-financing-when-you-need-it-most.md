## Why Banks Fail in Emergencies

### Traditional Bank Timeline:

• **Week 1:** Document submission and initial review

• **Week 2-3:** Credit committee evaluation

• **Week 4-5:** Legal documentation and approvals

• **Week 6+:** Fund disbursement

**By week 6, your emergency has become a business closure.**

### Bank Requirements That Waste Critical Time:

• **Audited financials** (many small businesses don’t have current audits)

• **Multiple guarantors** (finding guarantors takes weeks)

• **Collateral valuations** (property valuations take 2-3 weeks)

• **Business plan presentations** (emergency means no time for lengthy presentations)

• **Credit scoring delays** (automated systems reject first, ask questions later)
