## Professional Delivery Vehicle Inspection

Every delivery truck requires comprehensive inspection before approval:

### Delivery Vehicle Inspection Focus Areas

**Delivery Capability Assessment (40% inspection weighting):**

• Load capacity verification (payload ratings, suspension adequacy)

• Cargo area condition (floor, walls, securing points)

• Loading equipment (tailgate, ramps, accessibility)

• Cargo securing systems (tie-down points, safety equipment)

• Access and maneuverability (turning radius, parking capability)

**Operational Reliability Evaluation (35% inspection weighting):**

• Engine performance (power delivery, fuel efficiency)

• Transmission operation (smooth shifting, appropriate gearing)

• Braking system (stopping power, safety for loaded operation)

• Electrical system (battery capacity, alternator output)

• Cooling system (adequate for delivery stop-start operations)

**Commercial Vehicle Standards (25% inspection weighting):**

• Commercial vehicle compliance (safety standards, regulations)

• Driver visibility and safety (mirrors, lighting, visibility)

• Professional appearance (suitable for customer-facing delivery)

• Maintenance accessibility (service points, component access)

• Documentation completeness (registration, permits, compliance)
