## Types of Machinery Finance Companies in Malaysia

### Commercial Banks

Major banks like Maybank, CIMB, Public Bank, and RHB offer equipment financing through their SME banking divisions.

**Strengths:**
- Potentially lowest interest rates
- Established reputation
- Multi-product relationships
- Government guarantee programs access

**Weaknesses:**
- Strict equipment age limits (usually 5 years max)
- High down payment requirements (20-30%)
- Rigid credit scoring (CTOS 650+)
- Slow approval (3-6 weeks)
- Industry restrictions

**Best for:** Established businesses buying new equipment with strong credit profiles.

### Development Financial Institutions

Organizations like SME Bank, MIDF, and CGC provide targeted financing for specific sectors.

**Strengths:**
- Below-market interest rates
- Government backing
- Flexible for target sectors
- Technical advisory services

**Weaknesses:**
- Limited funds (often oversubscribed)
- Lengthy application process
- Sector restrictions
- Bureaucratic procedures

**Best for:** Businesses in priority sectors with time to navigate applications.

### Hire Purchase Companies

Private financing companies specializing in equipment purchase financing.

**Strengths:**
- More flexible than banks
- Faster approval process
- Accept older equipment
- Equipment expertise
- Lower down payment options

**Weaknesses:**
- Rates may be higher than banks
- Smaller lending capacity than major banks
- Vary significantly in quality

**Best for:** Used equipment, moderate credit situations, faster processing needs.

### Specialist Equipment Financiers (Ing Heng Credit)

**Strengths:**

**Weaknesses:**

## Questions to Ask Any Machinery Finance Company

Before committing, ask these questions:

### About Terms
1. What's the minimum and maximum financing amount?
2. What tenure options are available?
3. What's the down payment requirement?
4. Are there processing or documentation fees?
5. What's your policy on early settlement?

### About Process
1. What documents are required for application?
2. How long does approval typically take?
3. How do you assess equipment value?
4. What happens after approval (timeline to disbursement)?

### About Equipment
1. What equipment types do you finance?
2. Is there an equipment age limit?
3. Do you require equipment inspection?
4. What brands or types are preferred or restricted?

### About Experience
1. How long have you been financing equipment?
2. What's your experience in my industry?
3. How many businesses have you financed?
4. Can you share client references or testimonials?
