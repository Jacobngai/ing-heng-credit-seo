## Due Diligence Process Untuk Used Forklift

### 1. Engine Dan Powertrain Assessment

**Diesel engine inspection:**
- **Visual check:** Oil leaks, corrosion, mounting condition
- **Performance test:** Start-up behavior, idle quality, power delivery
- **Oil analysis:** Color, consistency, contamination level
- **Cooling system:** Radiator condition, coolant quality
- **Exhaust evaluation:** Smoke color, excessive emissions

**LPG system evaluation:**
- **Tank condition:** Inspection date, physical condition
- **Conversion quality:** Professional installation verification
- **Performance test:** Power delivery, fuel consumption
- **Safety check:** Gas leak testing, emergency shut-off

**Electric system analysis:**
- **Battery condition:** Capacity test, charge retention
- **Charging system:** Charger condition, cable integrity
- **Motor performance:** Smooth operation, no unusual noise
- **Controller function:** All speed ranges, regenerative braking

### 2. Hydraulic System Evaluation

**Critical inspection points:**
- **Fluid condition:** Color, contamination, proper viscosity
- **System pressure:** Test under load conditions
- **Cylinder inspection:** Seal condition, rod straightness, smooth operation
- **Hose condition:** Age, flexibility, pressure rating
- **Pump performance:** Consistent pressure delivery, no cavitation

**Lift mechanism assessment:**
- **Mast condition:** Straight, proper lubrication, smooth operation
- **Chain condition:** Wear, lubrication, proper tension
- **Fork condition:** Straightness, heel pin wear, proper spacing
- **Tilt function:** Smooth operation, proper speed control

### 3. Chassis Dan Running Gear

**Tire evaluation:**
- **Tread depth:** Remaining life assessment
- **Wear pattern:** Even wear indicates proper maintenance
- **Type suitability:** Solid, pneumatic, cushion untuk application
- **Rim condition:** Damage, proper mounting, bearing condition

**Chassis inspection:**
- **Frame integrity:** No cracks, proper alignment
- **Suspension:** Smooth operation, no excessive play
- **Steering system:** Responsive, no excessive play
- **Brake system:** Proper operation, adjustment, wear condition

### 4. Operator Environment

**Cabin assessment:**
- **Seat condition:** Comfort, adjustment range, safety features
- **Controls:** Smooth operation, proper response, clear marking
- **Visibility:** Clear sight lines, mirror condition, lighting
- **Safety equipment:** Seatbelt, ROPS/FOPS certification, warning systems

**Ergonomics evaluation:**
- **Control layout:** Logical, comfortable reach
- **Display readability:** Hour meter, fuel gauge, warning lights
- **Noise level:** Acceptable untuk long-term operation
- **Vibration:** Minimal transmission untuk operator comfort
