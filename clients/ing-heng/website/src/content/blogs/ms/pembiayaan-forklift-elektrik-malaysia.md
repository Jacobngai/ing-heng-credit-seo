## Electric Forklift Technology Dan Applications

### 1. Battery Technology Revolution

**Lead Acid Batteries (Traditional):**
- **Operating time:** 6-8 hours per charge
- **Charging time:** 8-12 hours full charge
- **Lifespan:** 1,200-1,500 cycles (3-5 years)
- **Cost:** Lower initial investment
- **Maintenance:** Regular watering, cleaning terminals

**Lithium-Ion Batteries (Advanced):**
- **Operating time:** 8-12 hours per charge
- **Charging time:** 1-3 hours full charge, opportunity charging
- **Lifespan:** 3,000-5,000 cycles (8-10 years)
- **Cost:** Higher initial, lower total cost of ownership
- **Maintenance:** Minimal, no watering required

**TCO comparison (5-year period):**
- **Lead acid total cost:** RM 85,000 (equipment + batteries + maintenance)
- **Lithium total cost:** RM 95,000 (equipment + batteries + maintenance)
- **Lithium advantages:** 10% cost premium, but 40% longer lifespan
