## Types Of Forklift Dan Applications

### 1. Diesel Forklift - Heavy Duty Workhorses

**Specifications dan applications:**
- **Load capacity:** 1.5 - 10 ton
- **Applications:** Outdoor operations, heavy cargo, container handling
- **Advantages:** High power, long operating hours, lower fuel cost
- **Industries:** Ports, construction materials, heavy manufacturing

**Popular models dalam Malaysia:**
- **Toyota 7FD:** Industry standard, excellent reliability
- **Mitsubishi FD:** Robust construction, good service network
- **Komatsu FD:** Fuel efficient, comfortable operator cabin
- **Chinese brands:** HELI, JAC, cost-effective alternatives

**Revenue potential:**
- **Container yards:** RM 120-180 per container
- **Construction sites:** RM 150-250 per hour
- **Manufacturing plants:** RM 1,200-2,000 daily rates
- **Port operations:** RM 200-300 per hour

### 2. LPG/Gas Forklift - Versatile Indoor/Outdoor

**Specifications dan applications:**
- **Load capacity:** 1.5 - 5 ton
- **Applications:** Warehouses, distribution centers, retail operations
- **Advantages:** Clean burning, indoor use, quick refueling
- **Industries:** E-commerce, retail distribution, food processing

**Market-leading models:**
- **Toyota 7FG:** Most popular, proven reliability
- **Mitsubishi FG:** Smooth operation, operator comfort
- **Nissan PL:** Value for money, good performance
- **TCM FG:** Japanese quality, competitive pricing

**Operating advantages:**
- **Fuel cost:** 30-40% lower than diesel
- **Indoor operation:** Clean emissions untuk warehouse use
- **Quick refueling:** 3-5 minutes vs hours for electric charging
- **Consistent power:** No battery degradation issues

### 3. Electric Forklift - Clean Dan Efficient

- **Productivity:** Quiet operation, better working environment
