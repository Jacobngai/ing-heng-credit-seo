Smart Financing Tips

Expert advice to maximize your equipment financing benefits

1

0% Deposit Available

We offer 0% deposit with 100% financing available* - no upfront payment needed. While larger deposits reduce monthly payments and interest, our 0% option ensures equipment is accessible to all businesses. (*Subject to approval and terms)

2

Choose the Right Term

Shorter terms (12-24 months) save on total interest but have higher monthly payments. Longer terms (48-60 months) offer lower monthly payments but higher total costs.

3

Consider Cash Flow

Match your loan term to your equipment's revenue-generating cycle. Construction equipment with seasonal work may benefit from flexible payment arrangements.

4

Tax Benefits

Equipment financing offers tax deductions on interest payments and potential capital allowances. Consult your accountant to maximize benefits.
