Professional Cold Chain Vehicle Inspection

Every refrigerated truck requires specialized inspection before approval:

Comprehensive Cold Chain Assessment Scope

Refrigeration System Evaluation (50% inspection weighting):

  • Compressor functionality (capacity, efficiency, noise levels)
  • Condenser and evaporator coil condition
  • Refrigerant levels and system leak testing
  • Temperature control accuracy (calibrated testing)
  • Defrost system operation (automatic and manual cycles)
  • Insulation integrity (thermal imaging inspection)
  • Door seals and gasket condition
  • Temperature monitoring system functionality

Vehicle Chassis Assessment (30% inspection weighting):

  • Engine condition (extra cooling requirements for refrigeration)
  • Electrical system (alternator capacity for refrigeration load)
  • Fuel system (additional capacity for refrigeration unit)
  • Suspension (handling refrigerated body weight)
  • Braking system (increased stopping distance considerations)
  • Tire condition (load rating appropriate for refrigerated cargo)

Cold Chain Body and Equipment (20% inspection weighting):

  • Insulated body construction (foam density, panel integrity)
  • Interior surfaces (food-grade materials, easy cleaning)
  • Drainage systems (condensation and cleaning water removal)
  • Loading equipment (hydraulic tailgate functionality)
  • Interior lighting (LED systems, battery impact)
  • Ventilation systems (air circulation for uniform temperature)
