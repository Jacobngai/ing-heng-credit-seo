Why Finance a Bulldozer Instead of Renting?

1. Stop Paying Rental Fees Forever

Rental costs add up fast:

  • Small dozer (D3): RM 3,000-5,000/month
  • Medium dozer (D5): RM 8,000-12,000/month
  • Large dozer (D7): RM 15,000-20,000/month

Example: 5-ton dozer rental

  • Monthly rental: RM 10,000
  • 3-year project: RM 360,000
  • Result: RM 360,000 spent, zero assets owned

Instead, finance with 0% deposit:

  • Dozer price: RM 400,000
  • Finance 100%: RM 400,000 loan
  • Monthly payment (60 months, 7% p.a.): RM 7,920
  • After 5 years: Own RM 400,000 dozer, save RM 115,000

2. Own the Asset from Day One

When you finance a bulldozer:

  • Build company assets
  • Depreciation benefits for tax
  • Use dozer for any project (not just one)
  • Sell or trade-in when upgrading
  • No daily rental charges eating profits

3. Win More Contracts

Many government and large-scale projects require:

  • Proof of owned equipment (not rental)
  • Financial stability (assets on balance sheet)
  • Long-term capability (not dependent on rental availability)

Owning your bulldozer makes you more competitive for major contracts.

4. Better Cash Flow Management

Spread the cost over 36-60 months:

  • Manage seasonal income fluctuations
  • Align payments with project revenue
  • Preserve working capital for operations
  • Pay from profits, not reserves

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need a deposit for bulldozer financing?

No deposit required for qualified applicants. We offer 0% down payment and finance up to 100% of bulldozer value. While banks require 20-30% down payment, we provide 0% deposit financing based on business strength and project contracts. This is our major competitive advantage.

Can I finance used bulldozers?

Yes! We specialize in used bulldozer financing up to 10 years old. Banks typically limit to 5 years. Used dozers cost 40-60% less than new, offer faster ROI, and provide proven reliability. Same 0% deposit option available for qualifying used equipment.

What bulldozer sizes can I finance?

We finance all sizes from mini dozers (50-90 HP) to large crawlers (300+ HP). Common brands include Caterpillar, Komatsu, Shantui, XCMG, and all major manufacturers. Financing range: RM 150,000 to RM 3,000,000+.

How long does bulldozer financing approval take?

Fast approval compared to traditional banks. We provide much quicker responses with streamlined processes. Banks take 2-4 weeks for approval. We understand contractors need equipment urgently for project deadlines.

What industries use bulldozer financing?

Construction companies, mining operators, land clearing contractors, plantation developers, infrastructure builders, and earthwork specialists. Any business requiring heavy earthmoving equipment for projects or operations.

Can new businesses apply for bulldozer financing?

Yes! We accept businesses from 1 year old with active projects and revenue. Banks typically require 3+ years operation. We understand contractors need equipment to grow their business and win larger contracts.

What interest rates do you offer for bulldozer financing?

Competitive rates starting from 6.5% per annum for qualified applicants. Final rate depends on credit profile, down payment amount, bulldozer age (new vs used), and financing tenure. Higher deposit = lower rate.
