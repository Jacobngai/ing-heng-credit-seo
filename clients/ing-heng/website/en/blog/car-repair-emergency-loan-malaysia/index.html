Frequently Asked Questions

General Emergency Loan Questions

Q: What makes this different from normal car loans?

A: Emergency car repair loans are processed urgently with relaxed documentation requirements. While normal car loans take 2-4 weeks, emergency loans are approved within hours. We prioritize genuine transportation emergencies over strict credit requirements because losing mobility affects your livelihood immediately.

Q: Can I apply if my car is very old (15+ years)?

A: Yes! We finance repairs for cars up to 20 years old if:

  • Repair cost is reasonable vs car value
  • Car has sentimental or practical value
  • You cannot afford replacement vehicle
  • Repair extends useful life significantly

Older cars often have lower repair costs and we understand not everyone can afford newer vehicles.

Q: What if I need to repair multiple things on my car?

A: Comprehensive repair financing is available for multiple simultaneous problems:

  • Engine and transmission issues together
  • Accident damage plus mechanical problems
  • Major service plus component replacement
  • Complete vehicle restoration

We can approve larger loans for comprehensive repairs that restore your car to reliable condition.

A: Yes! Many customers have existing car loans and need additional financing for unexpected repairs. We evaluate:

A: Yes, comprehensive car insurance is mandatory for all car repair financing because:

A: Additional repair financing is possible through:

Workshop and Repair Questions
