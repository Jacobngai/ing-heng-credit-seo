Types of Tow Truck Configurations

Light Duty Tow Trucks

Light duty models handle passenger vehicles, motorcycles, and small commercial vehicles. These trucks typically feature wheel-lift or boom systems with 4-6 ton capacities. Investment ranges from RM150,000 to RM300,000 depending on configuration and features.

Wheel-lift systems provide quick hookup capabilities ideal for repossession and short-distance towing. Compact designs enable operation in residential areas and confined parking situations.

Boom trucks offer versatile positioning capabilities for accident recovery and difficult access situations. Hydraulic boom systems provide precise vehicle positioning while protecting customer property.

Medium Duty Tow Trucks

Medium duty trucks handle SUVs, pickup trucks, and commercial vehicles up to 8-10 tons. These units typically feature flatbed configurations with hydraulic loading systems. Pricing ranges from RM250,000 to RM450,000.

Flatbed designs provide secure transport for luxury vehicles and prevent drivetrain damage during long-distance towing. Enclosed or open configurations accommodate various customer requirements.

Rollback systems enable loading vehicles without wheel contact, preventing further damage to disabled vehicles. These systems particularly benefit vehicles with transmission problems or tire damage.

Heavy Duty Recovery Trucks

Heavy duty units handle commercial trucks, buses, and specialty vehicles. Recovery capabilities include multiple winch systems, stabilization equipment, and heavy lifting attachments. Investment ranges from RM400,000 to RM1,200,000.

Commercial vehicle recovery requires specialized equipment and trained operators. Heavy duty units provide capabilities for upright recovery, rotation, and load transfers in challenging situations.

Frequently Asked Questions

Tow truck size depends on your target market and typical vehicle types. Light duty trucks handle passenger cars, while heavy duty units are needed for commercial vehicles and challenging recoveries.

Tow trucks range from RM150,000 for basic light duty units to RM1,200,000 for heavy duty recovery trucks. Complete packages including equipment and setup may exceed these ranges.
