Equipment Financing Comparison Malaysia 2025

Comprehensive comparison of equipment financing options in Malaysia. Compare banks vs credit companies, interest rates, terms, and find the best equipment financing solution.

By ING HENG Credit

Equipment Financing Comparison Malaysia: Complete Guide 2025

Choosing the right equipment financing provider significantly impacts your business costs, cash flow, and overall success. With multiple financing options available in Malaysia, understanding the differences between banks, credit companies, and manufacturer financing helps you select the optimal solution for your specific needs.

This comprehensive comparison guide analyzes all major equipment financing options in Malaysia, covering interest rates, terms, approval criteria, processing times, and total costs to help you make informed decisions.

Overview of Equipment Financing Providers

Types of Equipment Financing Providers

Traditional Banks:

  • Maybank, CIMB, Public Bank, RHB, Hong Leong Bank
  • Focus: Large corporations and established businesses
  • Strengths: Lower interest rates, large financing capacity
  • Challenges: Strict approval criteria, slow processing

Credit and Finance Companies:

  • Ing Heng Credit, Hong Leong Credit, Public Finance, others
  • Focus: SMEs, flexible financing solutions
  • Strengths: Fast approval, flexible terms, relationship-based
  • Considerations: Slightly higher rates, specialized focus

Manufacturer Captive Finance:

  • Caterpillar Financial, Komatsu Finance, Toyota Finance
  • Focus: Brand-specific equipment financing
  • Strengths: Competitive rates for new equipment
  • Limitations: Limited to specific brands and models

Alternative Lenders:

  • Peer-to-peer platforms, online lenders, specialized financiers
  • Focus: Niche markets and specific industries
  • Strengths: Innovation, speed, specialized services
  • Considerations: Limited track record, variable quality

Bank Interest Rate Ranges:

  • Maybank: 4.5-8.5% p.a. (excellent credit)
  • CIMB: 5.0-9.0% p.a. (strong businesses)
  • Public Bank: 4.8-8.8% p.a. (established customers)
  • RHB: 5.2-9.2% p.a. (relationship pricing)

  • Ing Heng Credit: 7.5-12% p.a. (risk-adjusted pricing)
  • Hong Leong Credit: 8.0-13% p.a. (flexible terms)
  • Public Finance: 7.8-12.5% p.a. (industry-focused)
  • Others: 8.5-15% p.a. (varies by provider)

Manufacturer Financing Rates:

  • Caterpillar Financial: 3.5-9.5% p.a. (new equipment only)
  • Komatsu Finance: 4.0-10% p.a. (promotional rates available)
  • Toyota Finance: 3.8-9.8% p.a. (brand loyalty benefits)

Total Cost Analysis

Example: RM500,000 Equipment Financing Over 60 Months

Bank Financing (6.5% p.a.):

  • Monthly Payment: RM9,830
  • Total Interest: RM89,800
  • Processing Fee: RM2,500
  • Total Cost: RM592,300

Credit Company Financing (9.0% p.a.):

  • Monthly Payment: RM10,390
  • Total Interest: RM123,400
  • Processing Fee: RM5,000
  • Total Cost: RM628,400

Cost Difference: RM36,100 (6% higher for credit company)

But Consider:

  • Bank approval uncertainty and time cost
  • Credit company flexibility and speed
  • Opportunity cost of delayed projects
  • Cash flow advantages of faster processing

Approval Criteria Comparison

Traditional Bank Requirements

Financial Criteria:

  • Minimum 3 years in business
  • Annual revenue: RM5 million+ typically
  • Debt service coverage ratio: 1.5x minimum
  • Current ratio: 1.2x minimum
  • Good credit score (CCRIS 1-2)

Documentation Requirements:

  • 3 years audited financial statements
  • Management accounts (current year)
  • Bank statements (12 months)
  • Directors‚Äô personal financials
  • Equipment quotations and specifications
  • Business plan and projections

Collateral Requirements:

  • Equipment as primary security
  • Additional collateral often required
  • Personal/corporate guarantees
  • Land mortgage for large amounts

Approval Process:

  • Credit committee review required
  • Multiple approval levels
  • Site visits and verification
  • Legal documentation review
  • 2-6 weeks processing time

Credit Company Requirements

Financial Criteria:

  • Minimum 6 months in business
  • Annual revenue: RM1 million+ typically
  • Focus on cash flow and business viability
  • Bad credit cases considered individually
  • Flexible evaluation approach

Documentation Requirements:

  • 6-12 months bank statements
  • Financial statements (if available)
  • Business registration documents
  • Equipment quotations
  • Business profile summary

Collateral Requirements:

  • Equipment as primary security
  • Minimal additional collateral
  • Corporate guarantee sufficient
  • Flexible security arrangements

Approval Process:

  • Streamlined evaluation
  • Relationship-based decisions
  • Fast track processing
  • 1-3 business days approval
  • Industry expertise applied

Processing Time Comparison

Bank Processing Timeline

Week 1-2: Application Review

  • Document collection and verification
  • Initial credit assessment
  • Collateral evaluation
  • Site visits (if required)

Week 2-4: Credit Analysis

  • Detailed financial analysis
  • Risk assessment and scoring
  • Credit committee preparation
  • Management review and approval

Week 4-6: Documentation

  • Legal documentation preparation
  • Collateral registration
  • Insurance arrangements
  • Final disbursement approval

Total Time: 4-6 weeks (can extend to 8 weeks for complex cases)

Credit Company Processing Timeline

Day 1: Application Submission

  • Initial application review
  • Preliminary assessment
  • Document completeness check
  • Fast track evaluation

Day 1-2: Credit Decision

  • Business and financial analysis
  • Equipment valuation
  • Risk assessment
  • Management approval

Day 2-3: Documentation

  • Agreement preparation
  • Signing arrangements
  • Insurance coordination
  • Disbursement processing

Total Time: 1-3 business days (same week disbursement possible)

Manufacturer Financing Timeline

Week 1: Application Processing

  • Brand-specific application forms
  • Credit assessment and verification
  • Equipment specification confirmation
  • Dealer coordination

Week 1-2: Approval Process

  • Captive finance company review
  • Credit scoring and assessment
  • Risk evaluation and pricing
  • Final approval and terms

Total Time: 1-2 weeks (varies by manufacturer and complexity)

Equipment Type and Age Comparison

New Equipment Financing

Banks:

  • Excellent terms for new equipment
  • Competitive rates and longer terms
  • Full equipment value financing
  • Warranty and insurance benefits

Credit Companies:

  • Competitive terms for new equipment
  • Flexible down payment options
  • Fast processing advantages
  • Strong dealer relationships

Manufacturer Financing:

  • Best rates for brand-specific new equipment
  • Promotional financing available
  • Integration with equipment purchase
  • Brand loyalty benefits and support

Used Equipment Financing

Banks:

  • Limited appetite for used equipment
  • Strict age restrictions (2-3 years maximum)
  • Higher down payment requirements
  • Detailed condition assessments required

Credit Companies:

  • Excellent used equipment financing
  • Equipment up to 10 years old
  • Flexible terms and conditions
  • Industry expertise in equipment valuation

Manufacturer Financing:

  • Limited used equipment programs
  • Certified pre-owned programs available
  • Brand-specific warranties and support
  • Higher rates than new equipment

Industry-Specific Comparison

Construction Equipment

Best Options:

  1. Credit Companies: Understanding of industry cycles, flexible terms
  2. Manufacturer Finance: Competitive rates for new equipment
  3. Banks: Good for large, established contractors

Key Considerations:

  • Seasonal payment flexibility
  • Project-based financing structures
  • Equipment durability and resale value
  • Service support and maintenance

Manufacturing Equipment

Best Options:

  1. Credit Companies: Fast processing for production needs
  2. Banks: Good rates for large equipment investments
  3. Manufacturer Finance: Technology-specific expertise

Key Considerations:

  • Technology obsolescence protection
  • Production integration requirements
  • Maintenance and service support
  • Tax benefits optimization

Transportation and Logistics

Best Options:

  1. Credit Companies: Fleet financing expertise
  2. Banks: Volume discounts for large fleets
  3. Manufacturer Finance: Brand-specific programs

Key Considerations:

  • Fleet management and coordination
  • Driver and operational requirements
  • Fuel efficiency and operating costs
  • Regulatory compliance support

Fee Structure Comparison

Bank Fees

Processing Fees:

  • 0.5-1% of financing amount
  • Minimum RM1,000-5,000
  • Non-refundable upon approval

Additional Fees:

  • Legal documentation: RM3,000-8,000
  • Valuation fees: RM500-2,000
  • Insurance arrangement: Variable
  • Early settlement: 1-3% penalty

Credit Company Fees

Processing Fees:

  • 0.5-2% of financing amount
  • Often negotiable or waived
  • Competitive total fee structure

Additional Fees:

  • Documentation: RM1,000-3,000
  • Insurance coordination: Often included
  • Early settlement: Usually no penalty
  • Flexible fee arrangements

Manufacturer Financing Fees

Processing Fees:

  • 0-1.5% depending on promotion
  • Often waived for new equipment
  • Brand loyalty discounts

Additional Fees:

  • Minimal additional charges
  • Integrated with equipment purchase
  • Dealer coordination included
  • Promotional benefits available

Approval Rate Analysis

Bank Approval Rates by Business Type

Large Corporations (RM50M+ revenue):

  • Approval rate: 70-85%
  • Competitive terms and rates
  • Relationship benefits

Medium Enterprises (RM5M-50M revenue):

  • Approval rate: 50-70%
  • Standard terms and conditions
  • Higher documentation requirements

Small Businesses (Under RM5M revenue):

  • Approval rate: 30-50%
  • Strict criteria and limited flexibility
  • Often requires additional collateral

Credit Company Approval Rates

Established Businesses (2+ years):

  • Approval rate: 90-95%
  • Competitive terms available
  • Relationship-focused evaluation

Growing Businesses (6 months-2 years):

  • Approval rate: 80-90%
  • Flexible terms and structures
  • Business potential evaluation

Startups (Under 6 months):

  • Approval rate: 70-85%
  • Higher deposits may be required
  • Strong business plan essential

Factors Affecting Approval Rates

Positive Factors:

  • Strong cash flow and profitability
  • Good credit history and payment record
  • Established business relationships
  • Essential equipment for operations
  • Experienced management team

Negative Factors:

  • Poor credit history or defaults
  • Inconsistent cash flow patterns
  • New business with limited track record
  • Non-essential or luxury equipment
  • Weak management or business plan

Challenges:

Credit Company Customer Service

  • Personal relationship management

Considerations:
