Maintenance and Service Considerations

Manual Pallet Jack Maintenance

Simple maintenance requirements:

Daily operations:

  • Visual inspection: Check wheels, forks, hydraulic system
  • Lubrication: Grease fittings as needed
  • Cleaning: Remove debris and moisture

Monthly service:

  • Hydraulic fluid check: Top up if needed
  • Wheel inspection: Check for wear and damage
  • General tightening: Nuts and bolts check

Annual maintenance:

  • Hydraulic system service: Fluid change, seal inspection
  • Complete inspection: All components and safety features
  • Cost: RM200-400 annually for professional service

Electric Pallet Jack Maintenance

Battery and electrical system care:

Daily routines:

  • Battery charging: Proper charging cycle management
  • Charge level monitoring: Avoid deep discharge
  • Visual inspection: Check for damage or wear

Weekly maintenance:

  • Battery water levels: Top up distilled water if needed
  • Terminal cleaning: Keep connections clean and tight
  • Performance check: Note any operational changes

Monthly service:

  • Battery equalization: Balance cell voltages
  • Motor and controller check: Performance evaluation
  • Safety system inspection: Emergency stops, alarms

Annual overhaul:

  • Comprehensive inspection: All electrical and mechanical systems
  • Battery condition assessment: Replace if needed (5-8 years)
  • Cost: RM800-1,500 annually including battery maintenance
