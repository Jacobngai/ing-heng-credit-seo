Maintenance Reality Check

Diesel Forklift Maintenance Schedule

  • Daily: Pre-operation inspections, fuel check
  • Weekly: Oil level, coolant, hydraulic fluid
  • Monthly: Air filter, fuel filter, transmission

Electric Forklift Maintenance Schedule

  • Daily: Battery water level (lead-acid only)
  • Weekly: Brake inspection, electrical connections
  • Monthly: Battery equalization (if needed)
  • Quarterly: Motor brush inspection
  • Annually: Electrical system check

Making the Right Choice for Your Operation
