Advanced Waste Sorting Technology Overview

Optical Sorting Systems

How they work: High-resolution cameras and sensors analyze material properties in real-time. Pneumatic systems precisely separate materials based on:

  • Color recognition: Sorts by visual appearance
  • Shape analysis: Identifies container types and sizes
  • Material density: Differentiates plastic types
  • Chemical composition: Uses near-infrared spectroscopy

Typical applications:

  • PET bottle sorting from mixed plastics
  • Aluminum can separation from steel cans
  • Paper grade sorting (newspaper vs magazines vs cardboard)
  • Glass color sorting (clear, brown, green)

Processing capacity: 1-15 tons per hour depending on system size Purity rates: 95-99% for most material streams Investment range: RM 600,000 - RM 3.5 million

Applications:
