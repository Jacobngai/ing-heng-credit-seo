Consultancies mainly have:

  • Intellectual property

Risk Assessment

1. Can I get financing for a consultancy business without physical assets?

Yes, ING HENG Credit understands that consultancy businesses rely on human capital rather than physical assets. We evaluate applications based on your professional expertise, market demand, and business model strength rather than requiring traditional collateral.

2. How much can I borrow to start my consultancy practice?

Financing amounts typically range from RM25,000 to RM500,000, depending on your business model, market opportunity, and financial capacity. We can finance office setup, technology needs, working capital, and initial marketing investments.

Generally, we look for at least 5 years of relevant industry experience, though exceptional candidates with strong credentials may qualify with less experience. Your track record, professional certifications, and client network potential are key factors in our assessment.

Our streamlined process typically takes 1-2 weeks for complete approval. We understand that consultancy businesses often need to move quickly to capitalize on opportunities, so we prioritize efficient processing while maintaining thorough evaluation.
