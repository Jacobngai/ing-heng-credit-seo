Understanding Calculator Results

Payment Calculation Methodology

How monthly payments are calculated:

Our calculator uses the reducing balance method - the same calculation method used by Malaysian banks and finance companies. This means interest is charged only on the remaining loan balance, not the original amount.

Formula breakdown:

  • P = Principal loan amount
  • R = Monthly interest rate (annual rate √∑ 12)
  • N = Number of months
  • Monthly Payment = P √ó [R(1+R)^N] √∑ [(1+R)^N-1]

Example calculation (RM80,000 loan at 9% for 48 months):

  • Principal: RM80,000
  • Monthly rate: 9% √∑ 12 = 0.75%
  • Months: 48
  • Monthly payment: RM1,955
