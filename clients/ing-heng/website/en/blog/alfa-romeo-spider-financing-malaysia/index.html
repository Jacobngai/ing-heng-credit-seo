Understanding Alfa Romeo Spider Values

Series 1 Duetto (1966-1969)

The holy grail of Spider collecting. Round tail design by Pininfarina represents pure Italian aesthetic. Matching numbers examples command premium prices, especially in Rosso Alfa red.

Investment Highlights:

  • Only 6,325 produced
  • Most valuable Spider variant
  • Bone stock examples extremely rare
  • Values: RM 180,000 - RM 450,000+

Series 2 (1970-1982)

The longest production run with refined engineering. Kamm tail design improved aerodynamics while maintaining classic proportions.

Investment Highlights:

  • Multiple engine options
  • Better parts availability
  • Solid investment fundamentals
  • Values: RM 85,000 - RM 250,000

Series 3 (1983-1989)

Modern conveniences meet classic styling. Improved reliability without sacrificing character.

Investment Highlights:

  • Electronic fuel injection
  • Better rust protection
  • Entry-level collector pricing
  • Values: RM 65,000 - RM 180,000

Series 4 (1990-1994)

Final evolution with modern safety features. Last of the truly analog sports cars.

Investment Highlights:

  • Most reliable variant
  • Modern conveniences
  • Appreciating rapidly
  • Values: RM 55,000 - RM 150,000
