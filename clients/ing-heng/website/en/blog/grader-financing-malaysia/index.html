No deposit required for qualified applicants. We offer 0% down payment and finance up to 100% of motor grader value. Requirements include good credit (680+), established business (2+ years), and strong project contracts. Most qualified applicants receive 0-10% deposit financing terms.

Yes! We specialize in used motor grader financing up to 10 years old. Banks typically limit to 5-7 years maximum. Used graders cost 40-50% less than new, offer faster ROI, and provide proven reliability. Same 0% deposit option available for qualifying used equipment.

What grader sizes can I finance?

We finance all sizes from compact graders (100 HP) to large motor graders (500+ HP). Popular brands include Caterpillar, Komatsu, John Deere, XCMG, and all major manufacturers. Financing range: RM 400,000 to RM 3,500,000+.

How long does grader financing approval take?

What projects need motor grader financing?
