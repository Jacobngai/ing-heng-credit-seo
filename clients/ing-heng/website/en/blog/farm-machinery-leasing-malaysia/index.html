What is Farm Machinery Leasing?

Farm machinery leasing is a financing arrangement where you use agricultural equipment without the full purchase price upfront. We purchase the equipment and lease it to you for a specified period with agreed monthly payments. At the lease end, you typically have options to purchase, return, or extend the lease.

Common Questions About Farm Machinery Leasing

Financial Considerations

Q: How do lease payments compare to loan payments?

A: Lease payments are typically lower than loan payments because you‚Äôre only paying for the equipment use, not the full ownership cost. However, total cost over time may be higher if you eventually purchase. The key advantage is cash flow management and risk reduction.

Example Comparison (RM200,000 tractor):

  • Purchase loan: RM4,200/month for 60 months = RM252,000 total
  • Operating lease: RM3,200/month for 60 months = RM192,000 (return equipment)
  • Finance lease: RM3,800/month for 60 months + RM20,000 purchase = RM248,000 (own equipment)

Q: Can I claim GST input tax on lease payments?

A: Yes, if your farm is GST-registered, you can typically claim input tax on lease payments. This provides additional cash flow benefits beyond the lease structure itself. Consult your tax advisor for specific circumstances.

Q: What happens if I want to end the lease early?

A: Early termination is possible but may involve penalties or remaining payment obligations. However, we often work with farmers to find solutions like lease transfers to other farmers or early purchase options. Circumstances like natural disasters or major market changes are considered individually.

Operational Concerns

Q: What if the leased equipment breaks down during critical farming periods?

A: This depends on your lease type:

  • Full-service leases: We provide replacement equipment or priority repairs
  • Basic leases: You handle repairs, but many manufacturers offer priority service for leased equipment
  • Insurance coverage: Can protect against lost income during equipment downtime

Q: Can I modify or customize leased equipment?

A: Modifications are typically restricted to protect equipment value. However, we can often approve reversible modifications or work with you to lease equipment that meets your specific needs from the start.

Q: How does equipment insurance work with leasing?

A: Comprehensive insurance is required on all leased equipment. You can arrange your own insurance or use our recommended providers. Insurance protects both parties and ensures continuity of operations.
