The Cash Flow Reality

New Lorry Cash Flow Impact:

  • Higher monthly payments
  • Maximum depreciation hit
  • Higher insurance premiums
  • Lower working capital available

Used Lorry Cash Flow Benefits:

  • Lower monthly payments
  • Minimal depreciation impact
  • Lower insurance costs
  • More working capital retained

Cash flow kills more logistics businesses than maintenance costs.

  • Used lorry benefits: Lower operating costs for competitive pricing

  • 3-5 year old lorries: Still modern technology, minimal efficiency difference
  • 8+ year old lorries: 20-25% higher fuel consumption

Technology Sweet Spot: 3-5 year old lorries offer modern efficiency without new vehicle pricing.

Making the Right Choice: Decision Framework

Choose New If:

  • Premium customers: Image crucial for high-end clients
  • Long-term plan: Keeping 10+ years
  • Warranty priority: Want maximum coverage
  • Latest tech: Need cutting-edge fuel efficiency

Choose Used (3-5 years) If:

  • Cost optimization: Maximize ROI priority
  • Business testing: First lorry or new routes
  • Cash flow focus: Need lower payments
  • Multiple units: Building fleet efficiently

Choose Used (6+ years) If:

  • Tight budget: Limited initial capital
  • Known routes: Established, predictable operations
  • Maintenance capability: In-house or trusted mechanic
  • High utilization: Multiple shifts justify older equipment
