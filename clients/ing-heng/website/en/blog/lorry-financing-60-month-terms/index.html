Understanding 60-Month Financing Structure

Eligibility for Extended Terms

Vehicle Qualifications:

  • Specialized equipment: Case-by-case assessment for complex vehicles

Business Requirements:

  • Established income: Demonstrated revenue stream for 12+ months
  • Stable operations: Consistent business activity and customer base
