## Executive Summary

Ing Heng Credit & Leasing is a Malaysian equipment financing company specializing in hire purchase, loan financing, and insurance solutions for construction, logistics, and industrial businesses. With 40 years of industry experience and operations centered in Klang, Selangor, the company provides fast-approval financing (2-hour turnaround) with a 95% approval rate, serving SMEs that require flexible, accessible equipment financing solutions.

**Core Value Proposition:** Fast, flexible equipment financing for Malaysian SMEs with minimal documentation requirements and high approval rates, enabling businesses to acquire essential machinery and vehicles without traditional banking barriers.

### Company History & Background

Ing Heng Credit was established in 1985 during Malaysia's industrial expansion period, initially serving the growing construction sector in Klang Valley. Over four decades, the company has evolved from traditional equipment leasing to comprehensive financing solutions, adapting to the needs of Malaysia's diverse SME landscape.

**Key Milestones:**
- **1985:** Founded in Klang, focusing on construction equipment financing
- **1990s:** Expanded to commercial vehicle financing for logistics sector
- **2000s:** Introduced flexible payment plans for seasonal businesses
- **2010s:** Implemented fast-approval systems (2-hour turnaround)
- **2020s:** Launched multi-language digital presence (EN/MS/ZH)
- **2025:** Comprehensive SEO strategy and digital transformation

**Location Advantage:**
Based in Klang, the company is strategically positioned near Port Klang (Malaysia's largest port), serving the dense concentration of logistics, warehousing, and industrial businesses in the Klang Valley region.

## Services Offered

### 1. Hire Purchase Financing

**Description:**
Hire purchase agreements allowing businesses to acquire equipment with ownership transfer upon completion of installment payments.

**Key Features:**
- **Financing Amount:** 70-90% of equipment value
- **Tenure:** 36-60 months (3-5 years)
- **Interest Rates:** Starting from 2.88% per annum (competitive rates)
- **Approval Time:** 2 hours
- **Down Payment:** 10-20% (flexible based on creditworthiness)

**Eligible Equipment:**
- Excavators (mini to large: 1.5-25 tons)
- Lorries (1-ton to 10-ton prime movers)
- Forklifts (electric and diesel)
- Cranes (mobile and tower cranes)
- Bulldozers and heavy earthmoving equipment
- Prime movers and trailers
- Refrigerated trucks
- Warehouse equipment

**Target Customers:**
- Construction contractors
- Logistics operators
- Manufacturing companies
- Warehouse operations

Business loans specifically designed for equipment acquisition, with funding provided within 3-5 days.

- **Funding Speed:** 3-5 days after approval
