**II. Understanding Each Option (900 words)**

**HIRE PURCHASE (Most Common in Malaysia)**

Definition:
- You buy the equipment with financing
- Finance company owns equipment until fully paid
- You own equipment after final payment
- Similar to car financing

How it works:
1. Choose equipment
2. Finance company buys it
3. You make monthly payments
4. After last payment, ownership transfers to you

Key features:
- Ownership: Yours after final payment
- Down payment: 0-30% typical
- Tenure: 3-9 years
- Interest rate: 6-10% p.a.
- Asset on balance sheet: Yes (after full payment)
- Tax deduction: Interest portion + depreciation
- Flexibility: Can sell/trade-in with settlement

Best for:
- Equipment you want to own long-term
- Excavators, lorries, forklifts, manufacturing equipment
- Building company asset base

---

**LEASING (Operating Lease)**

Definition:
- You rent equipment long-term
- Finance company remains owner forever
- You return equipment at lease end
- Like renting an apartment

How it works:
1. Choose equipment
2. Finance company buys it
3. You make monthly lease payments
4. At end, return equipment OR renew lease OR buy at residual value

Key features:
- Ownership: Never yours (unless you buy at end)
- Down payment: Usually lower (0-10%)
- Tenure: 2-5 years typical
- Monthly cost: Often lower than hire purchase
- Asset on balance sheet: No (off-balance sheet)
- Tax deduction: Full lease payment is expense
- Flexibility: Return/upgrade equipment easily

Best for:
- Technology that becomes obsolete quickly
- Equipment needed for specific project duration
- Businesses wanting to preserve capital
- IT equipment, specialized machinery

---

**EQUIPMENT LOAN (Less Common)**

Definition:
- Bank/finance company lends you money
- You own equipment from day one
- Loan is secured by equipment
- Similar to home loan

How it works:
1. You buy equipment (or get pre-approved)
2. Finance company lends purchase amount
3. Equipment is collateral for loan
4. You own equipment immediately

Key features:
- Ownership: Immediate
- Down payment: 20-40% typical
- Tenure: 5-10 years
- Interest rate: 5-9% p.a.
- Asset on balance sheet: Yes, immediately
- Tax deduction: Interest + depreciation
- Flexibility: Can sell equipment anytime (with loan settlement)

Best for:
- Established businesses with strong financials
- Large equipment purchases (RM 500K+)
- Long-term asset acquisition
