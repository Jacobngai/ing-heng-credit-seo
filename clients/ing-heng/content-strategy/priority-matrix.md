## ðŸ“Š PRIORITY SCORING METHODOLOGY

### Formula

**Priority Score = (Volume Score + Difficulty Score + Business Value) / 3**

### Volume Score (0-10)
- 0-20 volume = 2
- 21-50 volume = 4
- 51-100 volume = 6
- 101-200 volume = 7
- 201-500 volume = 8
- 501+ volume = 10

### Difficulty Score (0-10, INVERSE - lower difficulty = higher score)
- 0-10 difficulty = 10 (easiest to rank!)
- 11-20 difficulty = 9
- 21-30 difficulty = 8
- 31-40 difficulty = 7
- 41-50 difficulty = 6
- 51-60 difficulty = 5
- 61-70 difficulty = 4
- 71-80 difficulty = 3
- 81-90 difficulty = 2
- 91-100 difficulty = 1

### Business Value (0-10)
**Base Scores:**
- Transactional intent + high-priority persona = 10
- Commercial intent + high-priority persona = 8
- Informational intent + high-priority persona = 7
- Transactional intent + medium-priority persona = 9
- Commercial intent + medium-priority persona = 7
- Informational intent + medium-priority persona = 6

**Bonus Points (+1 each):**
- Mandarin language (42% revenue) = +1
- Malay language (40% revenue) = +1
- Long-tail with buying signals = +1
- Zero competition = +1
- Local targeting (Klang, Port Klang) = +1

### Priority Tiers
- **HIGH PRIORITY (Score 8-10):** Build first (Month 1-3)
- **MEDIUM PRIORITY (Score 6-7.9):** Build second (Month 4-6)
- **LOW PRIORITY (Score <6):** Build later (Month 7-12)
