## üèóÔ∏è ARCHITECTURE OVERVIEW

### Hub-and-Spoke Model

**HUB = Comprehensive Authority Page (3,000-5,000 words)**
- Targets broad keyword cluster
- Links to ALL related spokes
- Ultimate resource for a topic
- Updated quarterly

**SPOKE = Specific Deep-Dive Article (1,500-2,500 words)**
- Targets specific long-tail keyword
- Links back to hub (breadcrumb)
- Links to 3-5 related spokes
- Updated as needed

### Internal Linking Strategy

```
Homepage
    ‚Üì
3 Hub Pages (Equipment Financing Fundamentals, Equipment-Specific Guides, Industry Solutions)
    ‚Üì
36 Spoke Articles (12 spokes per hub)
    ‚Üì
Equipment Pages (Excavator, Lorry, Prime Mover, Forklift, etc.)
    ‚Üì
Service Pages (Hire Purchase, Loan Financing, Insurance)
    ‚Üì
Contact/Application Forms
```

**Cross-Linking Rules:**
- Hub ‚Üí ALL spokes (12 links minimum)
- Spoke ‚Üí Hub (1 link - breadcrumb)
- Spoke ‚Üí 3-5 related spokes (contextual)
- Equipment pages ‚Üí Related equipment (RelatedEquipment component)
- Every page ‚Üí Contact form
