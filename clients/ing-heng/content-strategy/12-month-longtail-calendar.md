## Content Production Guidelines

### Word Count Targets
- **Equipment guides:** 2,200-2,600 words
- **How-to articles:** 1,800-2,400 words
- **Comparison guides:** 2,000-2,400 words
- **Location pages:** 1,500-1,800 words
- **Problem-solution:** 1,800-2,200 words
- **Multi-language:** 1,500-1,900 words

### Content Structure (All Posts)
1. **Introduction** (150-200 words)
2. **Main Content** (1,200-2,000 words)
   - H2 sections with clear subheadings
   - Bullet points and numbered lists
   - Data/statistics where available
   - Customer pain points addressed
3. **FAQ Section** (300-400 words)
4. **Call-to-Action** (100-150 words)
5. **Related Equipment/Services** (Component)

### SEO Requirements (Every Post)
- ✅ Primary keyword in H1
- ✅ Keyword in first 100 words
- ✅ 2-3 H2 headings with keyword variations
- ✅ Meta description (155-160 chars)
- ✅ Internal links (minimum 5)
- ✅ External links (2-3 authoritative sources)
- ✅ Schema markup (Article, FAQ, Product where applicable)
- ✅ Image alt text with keywords
- ✅ URL slug with primary keyword

## Why This Strategy Works

### 1. Quick Wins First
- **Month 1-3:** Target only LOW difficulty keywords
- **Builds momentum** with early traffic and rankings
- **Proves ROI** quickly to stakeholders

### 2. Multi-Language Advantage
- **30% less competition** in Malay and Chinese searches
- **Faster ranking** (often within 1-2 weeks)
- **Higher conversion** (language-matched leads)

### 3. Long-Tail Focus
- **15-20% conversion rate** vs 2-3% for head terms
- **Easier to rank** (80% success rate vs 5% for head terms)
- **Captures buying intent** (specific = ready to buy)

### 4. Topical Authority
- **Comprehensive coverage** signals expertise to Google
- **Internal linking** between related posts boosts all rankings
- **Hub-and-spoke model** elevates service pages

### 5. Continuous Optimization
- **Track rankings weekly** (Google Search Console)
- **Update top performers** with new data quarterly
- **Build backlinks** to best-performing posts
- **Expand content** based on user questions (FAQs)
