Usage Guidelines

Do's ‚úÖ

  • Embrace white space aggressively (50%+ of page)
  • Use high-quality, unfiltered equipment photography
  • Maintain strict grid alignment
  • Keep typography hierarchy simple (max 3 sizes per page)
  • Use subtle Muted Bronze accents (less than 3% of page)
  • Prioritize readability and clarity

Don'ts ‚ùå

  • Don't use decorative elements (patterns, textures)
  • Don't use multiple colors per section
  • Don't add shadows or gradients
  • Don't use rounded corners (keep sharp edges)
  • Don't overcrowd content (less is more)
  • Don't use stock photos with models

Why Choose This Option?

Best For

  • Brands wanting to emphasize transparency and honesty
  • Markets where "no hidden fees" is a key selling point
  • Clients who appreciate design sophistication
  • Industries where quality speaks for itself (premium equipment)
  • B2B buyers who want facts, not fluff
‚úÖ Timeless aesthetic that won't age
‚úÖ Fast website loading (minimal assets)
‚úÖ Focuses attention on equipment and terms
‚úÖ Appeals to sophisticated, design-aware buyers
‚úÖ Lower development and maintenance costs
‚úÖ Excellent accessibility (high contrast)

Considerations

  • May feel "too simple" or "unfinished" to some clients
  • Requires excellent photography (design won't hide poor images)
  • Less emotional appeal (very rational approach)
  • May not stand out in crowded markets
