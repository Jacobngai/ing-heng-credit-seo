## CNY-Specific Challenges

### Challenge #1: Higher Wear & Tear

**Reality:**
- More guests per booking (families)
- More cooking (reunion dinners)
- More use of facilities
- Higher likelihood of accidents

**Mitigation:**
- Higher cleaning budget (add RM50-100 per stay)
- Clear house rules (cooking cleanup, guest limits)
- Security deposit (RM200-500)
- Frequent inspections between stays

---

### Challenge #2: Noise Complaints

**Reality:**
- Families gather, celebrate loudly
- Late-night mahjong sessions
- Children running around
- Fireworks (if allowed)

**Mitigation:**
- Clear quiet hours in house rules
- Inform neighbors about your CNY hosting
- Provide earplugs as courtesy
- Soundproofing if possible (rugs, curtains)

---

### Challenge #3: Parking Issues

**Reality:**
- More guests = more cars
- Heritage area has limited parking
- Congestion during CNY

**Mitigation:**
- Provide detailed parking instructions
- Reserve parking spots if possible
- Suggest alternative parking areas
- Consider paid parking options

---

### Challenge #4: Check-Out Delays

**Reality:**
- Guests want to extend CNY stay
- Late checkout requests
- Cleaning delays between stays

**Mitigation:**
- Strict checkout policy communicated early
- Late checkout fee (RM50-100)
- Buffer time between bookings (4-6 hours)
- Backup cleaning team on standby
