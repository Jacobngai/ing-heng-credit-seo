**Before/Work/After Work Juggling**
- 7:00 AM: Guest inquiry about early check-in

- 3:00 PM (in meeting): Water leak reported by neighbor

**This isn't occasional. It's daily.**

**Weekend Sacrifices**
- Friday evening: Drive to Melaka (2-3 hours from KL, 3-4 from Singapore)

- Saturday night: Rush back for work Monday
- Sunday: Too exhausted to enjoy personal time

**Emergency Response Delays**
When something breaks:
- You're at work in KL/Singapore
- Guest is frustrated waiting
- You call Melaka contractors (who don't know you)
- Emergency rates apply because it's urgent
- You pay premium prices for rushed work

**Quality Control Blindspots**
- Is the cleaning team actually doing thorough work?
- Are guests treating your property well?
- Is the maintenance person overcharging?
- Are there issues you're not seeing until guest complaints?

**Local Knowledge Gaps**
- Which contractors are reliable?
- What are fair prices for services?
- Which local events impact bookings?
- What are guests really looking for in Melaka?

**Opportunity Costs**
- Time spent on Airbnb management
- Stress of constant availability
- Missed opportunities for pricing optimization
- Lower occupancy from slow guest communication
