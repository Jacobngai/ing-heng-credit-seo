## Heritage Zone Restrictions (What You Need to Know)

### Good News: Airbnb is Allowed

Despite being a UNESCO heritage site, **short-term rentals are permitted** for:
- Condos within the zone
- Strata-titled properties
- Properties with commercial access

### Potential Challenges

1. **Parking Restrictions**
   - Limited street parking
   - Some condos charge guest parking fees
   - Solution: Choose condos with free parking

2. **Noise Regulations**
   - Quiet hours enforced (10 PM - 7 AM)
   - No loud music/parties
   - Solution: Clear house rules + guest screening

3. **Renovation Rules**
   - External alterations restricted
   - Interior renovations allowed
   - Solution: Focus on interior design for appeal

When we manage your property, we:
- Verify all heritage zone rules
- Obtain necessary permissions
