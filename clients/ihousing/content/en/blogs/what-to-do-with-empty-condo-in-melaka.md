## Why Your Condo Sits Empty: Common Challenges Property Owners Face

Understanding WHY your condo isn't renting is the first step to solving the problem. We work with hundreds of Melaka property owners, and these are the most common reasons condos stay vacant:

### 1. Can't Find Reliable Long-Term Tenants

The Melaka rental market can be challenging:
- Local tenants often prefer landed properties over condos
- Corporate tenants want fully furnished units (you may have an empty unit)
- Students (Melaka has several universities) want cheaper options
- Creditworthy tenants are hard to find and vet

### 2. Tenant Damage Concerns

You've heard horror stories from other landlords:
- Walls damaged and need repainting
- Appliances broken beyond repair
- Floors scratched and stained
- Unauthorized renovations or modifications
- When tenants finally leave, you're left with a RM10,000-30,000 renovation bill

### 3. Legal Hassle of Evicting Non-Paying Tenants

This is every landlord's nightmare:
- Tenant stops paying rent but refuses to leave
- Legal eviction process takes **12-18 months** in Malaysia
- Lawyer fees: RM3,000-10,000+
- Lost rent during the process: RM18,000-45,000
- Even after winning court cases, enforcement can take additional months

### 4. Don't Know How to Manage Rentals

You're busy with your career, family, or other investments:
- Showing the property to prospective tenants takes time
- Handling maintenance requests interrupts your schedule
- Collecting rent and chasing late payments is stressful
- Dealing with tenant complaints drains your energy

### 5. Property Needs Renovation Before Renting

Your condo might be in original condition:
- Bare walls need painting
- Old fixtures need updating
- Furniture and appliances missing
- No air conditioning (essential in Melaka's climate)
- You're looking at RM15,000-50,000 just to make it rent-ready

**Sound familiar?** These challenges are exactly why many Melaka condo owners are turning to Airbnb short-term rentals as an alternative solution.
