## 2025 Guest Expectations: What Changed?

### Post-Pandemic Standards

Guests in 2025 have higher expectations than ever:

**Hygiene is non-negotiable:**
- Contactless check-in preferred
- Hand sanitizer in every room
- Antibacterial cleaning products used
- High-touch surfaces sanitized
- Extra cleaning supplies visible

**Work-from-anywhere is normal:**
- High-speed WiFi is essential (not nice-to-have)
- Dedicated workspace (table + comfortable chair)
- Multiple power outlets
- Good lighting for video calls

**Long-term stays are increasing:**
- Extended stays (7+ days) are 40% more common than 2019
- Guests need laundry facilities
- Kitchen essentials for cooking
- Extra storage space

**Smart home features expected:**
- Smart TV with streaming
- Keyless entry (digital locks)
- USB charging ports
- Air conditioning with remote control

## Avoid These Amenity Mistakes

### Common Errors

**1. Providing Cheap Bedding**
- Mistake: Low-quality mattress, thin pillows
- Result: Poor sleep = bad review
- Fix: Spend RM1,500-2,500 on quality mattress per bedroom

**2. Insufficient WiFi**
- Mistake: Basic 30 Mbps package
- Result: Slow streaming, poor video calls
- Fix: Minimum 100 Mbps, test regularly

**3. Missing Kitchen Essentials**
- Mistake: Fridge + microwave only
- Result: Can't cook simple meals
- Fix: Add rice cooker, kettle, basic cookware

**4. Hiding Cleaning Supplies**
- Mistake: All supplies stored away
- Result: Guests question cleanliness
- Fix: Keep sanitizer, disinfectant visible

**5. No Backup Supplies**
- Mistake: Exactly 1 roll of toilet paper
- Result: Guests run out, poor experience
- Fix: Always provide 2-3 backups

**6. Poor Lighting**
- Mistake: Single ceiling light
- Result: Dark, uninviting space
- Fix: Multiple light sources (lamps, ambient)

**7. Inadequate Power Outlets**
- Mistake: 1-2 outlets per room
- Result: Can't charge multiple devices
- Fix: Power strips with 4-6 outlets
