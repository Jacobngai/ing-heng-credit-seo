## Common Transition Questions (Answered)

### Q: Will my existing guests be affected?

**A:** No. All confirmed reservations are honored by your current manager until the official handover date. After handover, we take over all future bookings. Your guests experience zero disruption.

### Q: What if my current manager penalizes me for switching?

**A:** Most management contracts have standard termination clauses (typically 30 days notice). We help you review your contract and ensure compliant termination. We've never seen a platform penalize an owner for switching managers - it's your property, your choice.

### Q: How long does the full transition take?

**A:** Typically 3-4 weeks from initial consultation to full handover. The actual "downtime" where we're actively transferring access is 48-72 hours. During this window, your property remains bookable, and all existing reservations are protected.

### Q: Do I need to tell my guests anything?

**A:** No. Guests don't need to know management changed behind the scenes. They book through platforms (Airbnb, Booking.com) and receive consistent communication regardless of which manager handles your property.

### Q: What if I have long-term guests (30+ day stays)?

**A:** We coordinate with long-term guests separately. Their agreements remain valid, and we take over management communication for the remainder of their stay. No guest disruption, seamless internal handover.

### Q: Will I lose my Superhost status?

**A:** No. Superhost status is based on your performance metrics, not your manager. Our professional management typically improves Superhost qualification (4.8+ rating, 80%+ response rate, <1% cancellation rate).
