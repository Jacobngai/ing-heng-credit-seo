## How to Evaluate Any Property Manager (Checklist)

### Before Signing: Ask These Questions

**Revenue & Performance:**
- [ ] What is your average occupancy rate?
- [ ] What do similar units earn annually?
- [ ] Can you show actual performance data?
- [ ] How do you optimize pricing?
- [ ] Which platforms do you list on?

**Fees & Costs:**
- [ ] What is your EXACT management fee?
- [ ] Are there ANY other fees (admin, platform, markup)?
- [ ] How much do you charge per cleaning?
- [ ] Do you markup maintenance costs?
- [ ] Are platform fees charged to me or included?

**Services:**
- [ ] What's included in management fee?
- [ ] How do you handle guest communication?
- [ ] What's your response time?
- [ ] Do you provide professional photography?
- [ ] How do you handle cleaning and maintenance?

**Contract & Exit:**
- [ ] What is the contract length?
- [ ] Can I terminate early? Under what terms?
- [ ] What happens to my bookings if I switch?
- [ ] Is there a penalty for underperformance?

**References:**
- [ ] Can I speak to 2-3 existing owners?
- [ ] Can I visit a managed property?
- [ ] How long have you been operating?
- [ ] How many properties do you manage?
