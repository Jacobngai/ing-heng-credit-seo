## ROI Calculation Formula

**Monthly Net Income = Monthly Airbnb Income - iHousing Fee**

**Annual Net Income = Monthly Net Income √ó 12**

**ROI % = (Annual Net Income / Down Payment) √ó 100**

**Example (Type B with 70% occupancy):**
- Monthly Income: RM5,400
- iHousing Fee: RM250
- Monthly Net: RM5,150
- Annual Net: RM61,800
- If Down Payment: RM200,000
- **ROI: (RM61,800 / RM200,000) √ó 100 = 30.9% annual ROI**
