## SEARCH BEHAVIOR INSIGHTS (Chinese Speakers)

### Baidu vs Google Malaysia
- **Google Malaysia:** Chinese speakers in Malaysia use Google.com.my
- **Search Terms:** Mix of Chinese + English (e.g., "Airbnb 马六甲")
- **Simplified Chinese:** Used in Malaysia (not Traditional)

### Keyword Length
- **Shorter queries** compared to English (2-4 characters common)
- **More direct** (e.g., "民宿投资" vs "how to invest in homestay")

### Cultural Search Patterns
- **Price-conscious:** Will search "费用" (fees) more than English speakers
- **Trust-focused:** "靠谱" (reliable), "信誉" (reputation) matter
- **Comparison-heavy:** "vs" searches common
