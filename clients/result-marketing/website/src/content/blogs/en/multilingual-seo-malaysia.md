## The Technical Nightmare (And Why Agencies Charge RM 10k+)

If multilingual SEO is so great, why doesn't everyone do it?

Because it's hard.

To do it right, you can't just use Google Translate plugin. That's a disaster for SEO (and makes your brand look cheap). You need:

1.  **Hreflang Tags:** Code that tells Google "This is the BM version of this English page." mess this up, and Google ignores your pages.
2.  **Separate URLs:** `domain.com/en/`, `domain.com/bm/`, `domain.com/zh/`.
3.  **Cultural Nuance:** Direct translation fails. "Cheap renovation" translates to "Renovasi murah", but maybe the market actually searches for "Bajet renovasi".

Most agencies charge **RM 3,000 - RM 5,000 PER LANGUAGE** because of this complexity. They treat a 3-language site as 3 separate projects.

Suddenly, your RM 3,000 SEO budget balloons to RM 9,000. So businesses cut the non-English languages and go back to the "English-only" trap.
