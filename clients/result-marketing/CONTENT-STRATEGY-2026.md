## Quality Control System

### Before Production (Pre-flight Checklist)
- [ ] Keyword research validates search volume (>100/month)
- [ ] Intent alignment (commercial/transactional)
- [ ] No duplicate topics (check content calendar)
- [ ] Template assigned correctly

### During Production (Writer Checklist)
- [ ] Follows template structure
- [ ] Specific numbers (RM amounts, days, percentages)
- [ ] Includes relevant case study or example
- [ ] Has clear CTA with WhatsApp number
- [ ] Word count: 600-1200 words
- [ ] Summary box at top (key points)
- [ ] Table of contents for long posts
- [ ] At least 3 CTAs throughout
- [ ] Schema.org JSON-LD included

### After Production (QA Checklist)
- [ ] SEO meta description (150-160 chars)
- [ ] Title includes target keyword
- [ ] Mobile formatting (short paragraphs, bullets)
- [ ] Tables render correctly
- [ ] WhatsApp link works
- [ ] No broken formulas/calculators
- [ ] Translation accuracy (ZH/MS)
- [ ] Meets BLOG-STANDARD.md requirements

## Risk Management

### Content Quality Risks
**Risk:** High volume = low quality
**Mitigation:**
- Mandatory template usage
- QA bottleneck (nothing publishes without review)
- Weekly quality audits
- Sample testing (random spot checks)

### Translation Accuracy Risks
**Risk:** AI loses technical meaning
**Mitigation:**
- Technical terminology glossary
- Human review of 10% random sample
- Industry expert consultation monthly

### SEO Risks
**Risk:** Duplicate content, keyword cannibalization
**Mitigation:**
- Content calendar tracking (no duplicate topics)
- Canonical tags for similar content
- Monthly SEO audit

### Burnout Risks
**Risk:** Team exhaustion at this scale
**Mitigation:**
- Realistic daily targets (10-15, not 50)
- Template-driven approach (less creative fatigue)
- Weekend breaks (no publishing Sun)
- Quarterly template refresh
