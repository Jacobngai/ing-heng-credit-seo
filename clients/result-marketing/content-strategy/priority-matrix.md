# Result Marketing - Content Priority Matrix

**Date Created:** 2025-11-10
**Scoring System:** Multi-factor analysis (Search Volume + Difficulty + Business Value + Strategic Fit)
**Total Keywords:** 113 keywords analyzed

---

## Executive Summary

This priority matrix ranks all Result Marketing keywords by a **composite opportunity score** that considers:
1. **Search Volume** (0-10 points) - Higher volume = more traffic potential
2. **Keyword Difficulty** (0-10 points, inverted) - Lower difficulty = easier to rank
3. **Business Value** (0-10 points) - Alignment with Result Marketing's services
4. **Strategic Fit** (0-10 points) - Unique positioning opportunity

**Maximum Score:** 40 points
**Priority Tiers:**
- **TIER 1 (30-40 points):** Build IMMEDIATELY (Weeks 1-4)
- **TIER 2 (20-29 points):** Build NEXT (Months 2-3)
- **TIER 3 (10-19 points):** Build LATER (Months 4-6)
- **TIER 4 (0-9 points):** SKIP or low priority

---

## Scoring Methodology

### 1. Search Volume Score (0-10)
- **10 points:** 1,000+ monthly searches
- **8 points:** 500-999 monthly searches
- **6 points:** 100-499 monthly searches
- **4 points:** 50-99 monthly searches
- **2 points:** 10-49 monthly searches
- **5 points (NEW):** N/A (untracked) but strategic/emerging keyword
- **0 points:** <10 monthly searches or irrelevant

### 2. Keyword Difficulty Score (0-10, inverted)
- **10 points:** 0-20 difficulty (very easy)
- **8 points:** 21-40 difficulty (easy)
- **6 points:** 41-60 difficulty (medium)
- **4 points:** 61-80 difficulty (hard)
- **2 points:** 81-100 difficulty (very hard)
- **6 points (NEW):** N/A (untracked) - assume medium difficulty for new keywords

### 3. Business Value Score (0-10)
- **10 points:** Direct service keyword (transactional, high intent)
- **8 points:** Vertical-specific keyword (target persona)
- **6 points:** Problem/solution keyword (high conversion potential)
- **4 points:** Educational keyword (moderate conversion)
- **2 points:** General keyword (low conversion)
- **0 points:** Low relevance to business

### 4. Strategic Fit Score (0-10)
- **10 points:** Unique positioning (AI SEO, long-tail, 100 keywords/month)
- **8 points:** Competitive differentiation (vs. traditional agencies)
- **6 points:** High-priority vertical (SaaS, legal, finance)
- **4 points:** Standard SEO topic (table stakes)
- **2 points:** Generic marketing topic
- **0 points:** Off-topic or low relevance
