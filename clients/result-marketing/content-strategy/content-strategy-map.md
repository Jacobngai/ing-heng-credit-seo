## Intent Classification Framework

### The 4 Search Intents (SEO-pro.md Chapter 2)

**1. Informational Intent**
- **User Goal:** Learn, understand, research
- **Query Types:** How to, what is, why, guide, tutorial
- **Content Format:** Blog posts, guides, tutorials, explainers
- **Funnel Stage:** TOFU (Top of Funnel) - Awareness
- **Conversion Goal:** Email signup, follow on social, bookmark site

**2. Commercial Investigation Intent**
- **User Goal:** Compare options, evaluate solutions
- **Query Types:** Best X, X vs Y, reviews, alternatives, comparison
- **Content Format:** Comparison pages, reviews, case studies, roundups
- **Funnel Stage:** MOFU (Middle of Funnel) - Consideration
- **Conversion Goal:** Request quote, download case study, schedule consultation

**3. Transactional Intent**
- **User Goal:** Buy, sign up, hire, purchase
- **Query Types:** [Service] agency, [service] for [business type], pricing, packages
- **Content Format:** Service pages, pricing pages, landing pages
- **Funnel Stage:** BOFU (Bottom of Funnel) - Decision
- **Conversion Goal:** Contact form submission, book consultation, start project

**4. Local Intent**
- **User Goal:** Find nearby business/service
- **Query Types:** [Service] near me, [service] in [city], [service] [location]
- **Content Format:** Location pages, local landing pages
- **Funnel Stage:** BOFU (localized decision-making)
- **Conversion Goal:** Call, visit office, local consultation

## Customer Journey Mapping

### TOFU → MOFU → BOFU Flow

**Stage 1: Awareness (TOFU - Informational)**

**User Journey:**
1. User has problem: "My website isn't getting traffic"
2. Searches: "why is my website not getting traffic"
3. Lands on Result Marketing blog post (informational content)
4. Reads article, learns about long-tail SEO and AI search optimization
5. **Conversion:** Downloads lead magnet ("Free SEO Audit Checklist")
6. Enters email list, receives nurture sequence

**Key TOFU Pages:**
- Problem-focused content (traffic, leads, costs)
- AI SEO educational content
- Long-tail keyword strategy guides
- Vertical-specific educational content

**CTA Strategy:**
- Low-friction CTAs (download, subscribe, follow)
- Lead magnets (checklists, templates, calculators)
- "Learn more" links to MOFU content

---

**Stage 2: Consideration (MOFU - Commercial Investigation)**

**User Journey:**
1. User understands their problem and knows SEO is a solution
2. Searches: "AI SEO agency Malaysia" or "best alternative to traditional SEO agencies"
3. Lands on comparison page or vertical solution page
4. Compares Result Marketing to competitors
5. Reads case studies, reviews pricing, evaluates fit
6. **Conversion:** Books free consultation or requests custom quote

**Key MOFU Pages:**
- Vertical solution pages (SEO for SaaS, legal, finance, etc.)
- Comparison pages (AI vs. manual, Result Marketing vs. competitors)
- Pricing and package pages
- Case studies and testimonials

**CTA Strategy:**
- Medium-friction CTAs (book consultation, request quote, get proposal)
- Lead magnets (industry-specific keyword research, ROI calculators)
- "Schedule a call" or "Get custom quote" buttons
- Chat widget for immediate questions

---

**Stage 3: Decision (BOFU - Transactional)**

**User Journey:**
1. User is ready to hire an SEO agency
2. Searches: "SEO services Malaysia" or "SEO agency for small business Malaysia"
3. Lands on core service page or homepage
4. Reviews services, pricing, testimonials
5. Checks FAQ, reads guarantees, watches testimonial videos
6. **Conversion:** Fills contact form, calls phone number, books discovery call

**Key BOFU Pages:**
- Core service pages (AI SEO Malaysia, SEO services)
- Homepage (overview + conversion-focused)
- Pricing page (transparent packages)
- About page (team, credentials, trust signals)
- Contact page (multiple contact methods)

**CTA Strategy:**
- High-friction CTAs (contact us, start project, get started)
- Multiple CTA placements (hero, mid-page, bottom, sticky)
- Prominent phone number and email
- Live chat for immediate conversion assistance
- "What happens next" clarification (reduce anxiety)

---

### Content Interlinking Strategy

**TOFU → MOFU Linking:**
Every informational (TOFU) article should link to 2-3 relevant MOFU pages:
- **Example:** "Why Is My Website Not Getting Traffic" (TOFU)
  - Links to: "AI SEO Agency vs. Manual" (MOFU comparison)
  - Links to: "SEO for Small Business Malaysia" (MOFU vertical solution)
  - Links to: "Fast SEO Results" (MOFU results page)

**MOFU → BOFU Linking:**
Every commercial investigation (MOFU) page should link prominently to BOFU service pages:
- **Example:** "SEO for SaaS Companies Malaysia" (MOFU)
  - Primary CTA: "Get SaaS SEO Package Quote" (BOFU service page)
  - Secondary CTA: "Book Free SaaS SEO Audit" (BOFU lead form)
  - Footer CTA: "View All Services" (BOFU service overview)

**BOFU → TOFU Linking:**
Service pages should link to relevant educational content (builds trust):
- **Example:** "AI SEO Malaysia" (BOFU service page)
  - Links to: "How to Optimize for ChatGPT Search" (TOFU guide)
  - Links to: "AI Search vs. Google Search" (TOFU comparison)
  - Links to: "Case Study: How We Ranked 100 Keywords in 90 Days" (social proof)
