## **Google Business Profile Optimization**

### 1. Claim and Verify Your Profile

**Steps:**
1. Go to business.google.com
2. Click "Manage now"
3. Enter business name
4. Verify ownership (postcard, video, or phone)

**Critical:** Must verify to access all features.

### 2. Complete All Fields

**Required information:**
- Business name (consistent with signage)
- Address (actual location, not PO Box)
- Phone number (local area code)
- Website URL
- Business hours (accurate)
- Business categories (primary + secondary)

**Pro tip:** Use exact business name everywhere (website, directories, social media).

### 3. Upload Photos

**Minimum:**
- 10 photos minimum
- Types: Interior, exterior, team, products/services, at work

**Best practices:**
- High resolution (1000x1000px minimum)
- Good lighting
- Professional quality
- Regular uploads (weekly)

**AI engines use photos to validate business legitimacy.**

### 4. Collect Reviews

**Target:**
- 50+ reviews minimum
- 4.5+ average rating
- Mix of 4 and 5 star reviews

**How to get reviews:**
- Ask every satisfied customer
- Make it easy (QR code, direct link)
- Offer incentive (discount on next service)
- Respond to every review

### 5. Post Regular Updates

**Post types:**
- Offers and promotions
- New services
- Events
- Tips and advice
- Before/after photos

**Frequency:** 1-2 posts per week

**AI engines recognize active profiles as more authoritative.**
