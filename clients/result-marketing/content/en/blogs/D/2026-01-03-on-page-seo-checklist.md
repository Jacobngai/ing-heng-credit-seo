## Title Tag Optimization

**Most important on-page SEO element.**

### Best Practices:

**Length:** 50-60 characters

**Format:**
`Primary Keyword | Secondary Keyword | Brand`

**Example:**
`Crane Rental in Cheras, KL | 20-50 Ton | [Company Name]`

**Rules:**
- Include target keyword (preferably at start)
- Unique for each page
- Compelling (makes people want to click)
- Include location if local business
- Don't keyword stuff

**Good Examples:**
- `Emergency Plumber Kuala Lumpur | 24/7 Service | Ahmad Plumbing`
- `Excavator Rental Selangor | RM1200/day | [Company]`
- `Car Service Petaling Jaya | From RM80 | [Workshop Name]`

**Bad Examples:**
- `Home` (too generic)
- `Crane Crane Rental Crane Hire` (keyword stuffing)
- `Welcome to our website` (no keywords)
- Page title that's 150 characters (too long)

## Meta Description Best Practices

**Doesn't affect ranking directly, but affects CTR (click-through rate).**

### Best Practices:

**Length:** 150-160 characters

**Format:**
`[Benefit] + [Keyword] + [CTA]`

**Example:**
`Need crane rental in Cheras? 20-50 ton mobile cranes available. Same-day delivery. RM500-1500/day. Call +60123456789 for free quote.`

**Rules:**
- Include keyword naturally
- Compelling benefit
- Clear call-to-action
- Unique per page
- Accurate (don't mislead)

**Good Examples:**
- `Emergency plumbing in Kuala Lumpur? 30-minute response, 24/7. From RM150. Call now: 012-3456789.`
- `Rent excavators in Selangor from RM1200/day. 20-50 ton available. Operator included. Free quote: +60123456789`
- `Professional car service in Petaling Jaya. From RM80. Pickup & delivery available. Book now.`

**Bad Examples:**
- `We are the best company in the world for everything` (vague, no benefit)
- Description that's 200 characters (truncated in search results)
- Clickbait that doesn't match page content

## Header Tag Structure

**Organizes content for users and Google.**

### Best Practices:

**H1 (One per page):**
- Most important heading
- Include primary keyword
- Describes page content clearly

**Example:**
`<h1>Crane Rental Services in Cheras, Kuala Lumpur</h1>`

**H2 (Main sections):**
- Break up content into sections
- Include related keywords
- Logical organization

**Examples:**
```html
<h2>Our Crane Fleet</h2>
<h2>Crane Rental Pricing</h2>
<h2>Service Areas</h2>
```

**H3 (Subsections):**
- Details within H2 sections
- Supporting keywords

**Examples:**
```html
<h2>Our Crane Fleet</h2>
  <h3>20 Ton Mobile Crane</h3>
  <h3>50 Ton Crawler Crane</h3>
  <h3>100 Ton All Terrain Crane</h3>
```

**Rules:**
- One H1 per page (never more)
- Logical hierarchy (H1 â†’ H2 â†’ H3)
- Don't skip levels (H1 â†’ H3 â†’ H2 = wrong)
- Include keywords naturally (don't force)

## Content Optimization

### Content Length

**Minimum:** 300 words (very thin content)

**Good:** 1,000-1,500 words (optimal for rankings)

**Long-form:** 2,000+ words (comprehensive guides)

**For service pages:** 500-1,000 words

**For blog posts:** 1,000-2,000 words

### Keyword Placement

**Primary keyword:**
- In title tag
- In first 100 words of content
- In H1
- In one or two H2s
- Naturally throughout (1-2% density)

**Secondary keywords:**
- In H2s and H3s
- Naturally in content
- Image alt text

### Content Quality

**Google E-E-A-T (Experience, Expertise, Authoritativeness, Trustworthiness):**

**Demonstrate experience:**
- Case studies
- Project photos
- Years in business

**Show expertise:**
- Technical details
- Industry knowledge
- Specifications

**Build authority:**
- Stats and data
- Awards and certifications
- Media mentions

**Establish trust:**
- Reviews and testimonials
- Contact information prominent
- Professional design

### Content Structure

**Make it scannable:**
- Short paragraphs (2-3 sentences)
- Bullet points
- Numbered lists
- Bold key information
- Images with captions

**Example structure:**
```html
<h1>Crane Rental in Cheras</h1>

<p>Opening paragraph with keyword and main benefit.</p>

<h2>Our Crane Fleet</h2>
<p>Details about fleet...</p>

<h2>Rental Pricing</h2>
<ul>
<li>20 ton: RM500/day</li>
<li>50 ton: RM1000/day</li>
</ul>

<h2>Why Choose Us</h2>
<p>Benefits and USP...</p>

<h2>Contact Us</h2>
<p>Call to action...</p>
```

## Other On-Page Elements

### URL Structure

**Good:**
- `domain.com/crane-rental-cheras`
- `domain.com/services/excavator-rental`

**Bad:**
- `domain.com/page?id=123`
- `domain.com/post-2847392`

### Image Optimization

**File name:** Descriptive, with keywords
- `good: mobile-crane-rental.jpg`
- `bad: IMG_1234.jpg`

**Alt text:** Describe image, include keyword if relevant
- `good: 50 ton mobile crane on construction site`
- `bad: image.jpg`

**File size:** Compress for speed
- Under 200KB per image
- Use WebP format if possible

### Internal Linking

**Link to:**
- Related service pages
- Relevant blog posts
- Contact page (with CTA)

**Anchor text:** Descriptive, includes keywords
- `good: Learn more about our excavator rental services`
- `bad: click here`

### External Linking

**Link to:**
- Authoritative sources (government sites, industry associations)
- Manufacturers (equipment specs)
- Local business directories

**Shows Google you're part of ecosystem.**

## FAQ

**Q: How many keywords should I target per page?**
A: One primary keyword, 2-5 secondary keywords. Don't over-optimize.

**Q: Does keyword density still matter?**
A: Yes, but natural is best. 1-2% density. Don't force it.

**Q: Can I have multiple H1 tags?**
A: No. One H1 per page. Use H2s for other main headings.
