## Why Month 1-2 Lose Money

**Three reasons AEO takes 60+ days:**

### 1. Google's Re-Indexing Cycle

**The reality:**
- Google doesn't instantly index schema changes
- Re-indexing takes 2-6 weeks
- AI engines need time to discover and trust new structured data

**What's happening:**
```
Day 1-14: Google crawls your schema
Day 15-30: Google processes and validates schema
Day 31-45: AI engines start testing your content in answers
Day 46-60: First AI Overview appearances
```

---

### 2. Content Optimization Iterations

**The process:**
- Month 1: Publish foundational content, schema, FAQs
- Month 2: Analyze what's working, optimize underperforming pages
- Month 3: Double down on winning topics, expand reach

**Most businesses underestimate content optimization.** You need 3-4 iterations to find the winning formula.

---

### 3. AI Engine Trust Building

**AI engines don't trust new brands immediately.**

They need to see:
- Consistent schema markup across all pages
- Regular content updates (not just set-and-forget)
- Brand mentions across the web (digital PR, backlinks)
- Positive user signals (click-through rates, time on site)

**This trust takes 6-8 weeks to build.**

## **FAQ: AEO ROI Timeline**

### **Can I speed up the AEO timeline?**

Partially. You can accelerate by: 1) Investing more in content production (RM2,000-3,000/month instead of RM1,200), 2) Running Google Ads simultaneously (capture immediate traffic while building AEO), 3) Aggressive digital PR (get brand mentions faster). However, Google's indexing cycle (2-6 weeks) is fixed. You can't cheat that.

### **What if I see NO results by Month 4?**

Something is wrong. Common causes: 1) Wrong keywords (low search volume), 2) Poor schema implementation (Google can't read your markup), 3) Weak content (AI engines don't cite low-quality content), 4) Technical issues (page speed, mobile errors). Request an audit from your AEO agency or switch providers.

### **Is Month 3 too late to start AEO?**

No. AEO is still emerging. Most businesses don't understand it yet. Starting in Month 3 of 2026 means you're ahead of 90% of competitors. The AI search revolution is just beginning. Early adopters now will dominate AI answers for years.

### **Do seasonal businesses have different timelines?**

Yes. Seasonal businesses (e.g., aircond servicing peaks in hot months) should start AEO 2-3 months BEFORE peak season. If your peak is May, start AEO in February. By May, you'll be in Month 3-4 and ready to capture peak demand.

### **Should I stop AEO in slow months?**

No. AEO builds cumulative authority. Stopping for 1-2 months loses momentum. AI engines may deprioritize your content if they see inconsistent updates. Better to reduce scope (e.g., RM800/month instead of RM1,200) than stop completely.
