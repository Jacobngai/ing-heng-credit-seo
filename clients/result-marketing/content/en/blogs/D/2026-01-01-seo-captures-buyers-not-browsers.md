## Why Social Media Targets Browsers

Facebook, Instagram, TikTok = interruption marketing.

**How it works:**
1. User scrolling for entertainment
2. Your ad interrupts their scroll
3. User wasn't looking for your service
4. 95% ignore your ad
5. 5% click out of curiosity
6. 2-5% of those convert

**The funnel:**
- 10,000 impressions
- 500 clicks (5% CTR)
- 25 leads (5% conversion)
- **Cost: RM50-150 per lead**

You're paying to interrupt people who didn't ask for your service.

### The Ad Blindness Problem

**Malaysian social media usage stats:**
- Average user sees 5,000 ads/month
- 76% ignore sponsored posts
- Ad recall drops after 3rd exposure
- Click-through rates declining 15% yearly

Your "RM50 Facebook ad lead"? They saw your ad 5+ times before clicking.

That's RM250 in impressions for ONE low-intent lead.

## How to Target Buyer Intent Keywords

### Step 1: Identify Transactional Keywords

**High-intent keyword patterns:**
- "[service] near me"
- "[service] in [location]"
- "best [service] [location]"
- "[service] price"
- "cheap [service] [location]"
- "emergency [service]"

**Examples:**
- "crane rental near me kuala lumpur"
- "lorry rental selangor price"
- "emergency plumber klang valley"
- "aircond service petaling jaya"

### Step 2: Create Location-Specific Pages

**Don't create one page targeting all of Malaysia.**

**Wrong approach:**
- Page: "Crane rental services Malaysia"
- Targets: Entire country
- Competition: National competitors
- Conversion: Low (too broad)

**Right approach:**
- Page: "Crane rental in Cheras, Kuala Lumpur"
- Targets: Cheras area
- Competition: Local competitors
- Conversion: High (specific location)

Create separate pages for each service area you cover.

### Step 3: Include Commercial Intent Signals

**On your SEO landing pages, include:**
- Pricing (or "from RM X")
- Response time ("within 2 hours")
- Service area ("serving Cheras, KL")
- Phone number prominent
- WhatsApp click-to-chat
- Reviews/testimonials
- "Book now" or "Get quote" CTA

**Why these matter:**
Searchers with commercial intent are evaluating providers. They compare:
- Price
- Speed
- Location
- Reviews

Make these visible instantly.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: What's the difference between informational and transactional intent?**
A: Informational = learning ("how to rent a crane"). Transactional = buying ("crane rental near me"). Focus on transactional for lead generation.

**Q: Can I target both informational and transactional keywords?**
A: Yes. Use blog posts for informational (builds authority). Use service pages for transactional (generates leads).

**Q: How do I find buyer intent keywords for my industry?**
A: Use Google Keyword Planner, filter by "commercial" and "transactional" intent. Look for "near me", "price", "best", "emergency" modifiers.

**Q: What's a good conversion rate for buyer intent traffic?**
A: 20-40% is normal for service businesses. If you're getting less than 10%, your landing page needs work (slow load, no phone number, poor design).

**Q: Should I use Google Ads for buyer intent keywords?**
A: You can (CPC RM5-15). But SEO is cheaper long-term. Use Google Ads for the first 3-6 months while SEO builds, then scale back.
