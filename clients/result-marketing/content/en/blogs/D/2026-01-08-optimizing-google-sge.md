## **Understanding Google SGE**

### What is Google SGE?

**SGE** = **Search Generative Experience**, now called **AI Overviews**.

**How it works:**
- User searches query
- Google's AI (Gemini) generates answer
- AI cites 3-5 authoritative sources
- User sees answer + citations at top of results

**Impact:**
- AI Overviews appear in 80% of searches (vs 0% in 2022)
- Cited sources see 40% CTR (vs 2-4% traditional)
- Zero-click searches decrease 25% (users click cited sources)

**Global:**
- 2023: SGE announced (beta)

- 2025: 50+ countries
- 2026: Global rollout (100%)

## **Content Optimization for SGE**

### 1. Answer-First Format

**Structure every page:**

**[H1] Main Question**
"Direct answer in 1-2 sentences."

**[H2] Detailed Explanation**
Comprehensive coverage with examples.

**[H2] Supporting Data**
Statistics, case studies, research.

**[H2] Conclusion**
Summary and CTA.

**Example:**
"How much does SEO cost in Malaysia?

SEO costs RM1,200-3,000 per month in Malaysia (2026). Pricing depends on keyword difficulty, competition level, and service scope. Most reputable agencies require 6-12 month commitments.

[Detailed explanation...]"

### 2. Target Question Keywords

**High-opportunity keywords:**
- "How much does [service] cost?"
- "How long does [service] take?"
- "What's the best [product/service]?"
- "How do I [action]?"
- "Why [problem] happens?"

**Create dedicated FAQ pages** for each question.

### 3. Use Structured Formatting

**Best practices:**
- Short paragraphs (2-3 sentences)
- Bulleted lists for readability
- Bold key information
- Numbered lists for steps
- Clear section headers

**Why:** AI engines extract structured content easily.

### 4. Include Examples and Data

**Add to every page:**
- Real case studies
- Specific numbers (not "many" or "lots")
- Before/after metrics
- Client testimonials
- Industry statistics

**Why:** Data builds trust and authority.
