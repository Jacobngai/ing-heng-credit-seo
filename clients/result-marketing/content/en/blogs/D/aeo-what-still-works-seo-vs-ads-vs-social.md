## **Frequently Asked Questions**

### Is SEO dead in 2026?

No, but traditional SEO is dead. AI search optimization (AEO) replaced keyword-focused SEO. Businesses optimizing for AI answers see 3.2x higher visibility than those using 2020 SEO tactics.

### Why are my Facebook ads not working anymore?

Facebook ad costs rose 67% since 2024 while engagement dropped 23%. Users are there to socialize, not buy. Social media works for brand awareness, not high-intent lead generation.

### Should I stop all paid advertising?

Not necessarily. Paid ads work for immediate results or time-sensitive promotions. But for sustainable lead generation, shift 70% of your budget to SEO/AEO. Use paid ads for short-term bursts, not long-term strategy.

### How long does SEO take to work?

Modern AEO shows results in 3-6 months. Our crane rental client saw leads in Month 4 and broke even by Month 6. After that, your cost per lead continues dropping while traffic grows.

### What's the difference between SEO and AEO?

Traditional SEO optimizes for ranking #1 on Google search results. AEO optimizes for being cited in AI answers (Google SGE, Bing Copilot, ChatGPT). AEO is the evolution of SEO for the AI search era.
