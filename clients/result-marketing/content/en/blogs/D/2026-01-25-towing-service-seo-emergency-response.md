## **Converting Emergency Calls**

### Mobile Optimization is CRITICAL

**95% of towing searches happen on mobile** during emergencies. Someone stranded on the highway isn't opening a laptop.

**Your mobile site must have:**
1. **Click-to-call button** HUGE and above the fold
2. **24/7 availability** prominently displayed
3. **Service areas** clearly listed (highways covered)
4. **Response time** ("Within 45 minutes")
5. **Pricing estimate** (transparency reduces abandonment)
6. **WhatsApp button** (backup option)

### Speed is Everything

**Emergency callers will call 2-3 companies.** The fastest to answer wins.

**Conversion Strategy:**
- **Auto-answer or pick up within 2 rings**
- **Ask location immediately** ("Where are you now?")
- **Give ETA** ("We'll be there in 30 minutes")
- **Confirm price** ("RM300 for this distance")
- **Text driver details** (name, phone, truck plate)

**Response Time Impact:**
- Answer in 2 rings: 80% conversion
- Answer in 5 rings: 40% conversion
- Goes to voicemail: 5% conversion

### Pricing Transparency

**Towing customers hate hidden fees.** Show pricing to reduce abandonment.

**Example Pricing Table:**
```
Standard Towing:
- RM300 base (first 10km)
- RM3-6/km after 10km
- RM50 after-hours surcharge (10pm-6am)

Examples:
- KL to Petaling Jaya: RM250-350
- KL to Shah Alam: RM350-450
- KL to Klang: RM400-500
- KL to Seremban: RM600-800

Highway Towing:
- PLUS highway coverage: RM350-500 (depending on location)
```
