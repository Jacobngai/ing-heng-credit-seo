## **The 6 DIY AEO Requirements**

### Requirement 1: Technical Skills (HTML/CSS/JavaScript)

**You'll need to:**
- Edit schema markup in JSON-LD format
- Implement structured data without breaking your site
- Fix crawl errors and indexing issues
- Optimize page speed (Core Web Vitals)
- Handle canonical tags and redirects

**Skill level needed:** Intermediate web development

**Reality check:**
- Can you read and edit HTML? YES/NO
- Can you debug JavaScript errors? YES/NO
- Can you implement JSON-LD schema? YES/NO

**If you answered NO to any:** DIY AEO will be extremely difficult.

### Requirement 2: Content Creation Ability

**You'll need to produce:**
- 4 blog posts/month (1,000-1,500 words each)
- Service page optimization (10-15 pages)
- FAQ content for schema markup
- Local business descriptions
- Competitor comparison content

**Quality standard:**
- Must be better than competitors' content
- Must include original data, insights, examples
- Must be optimized for AI Overviews (clear, direct answers)
- Must demonstrate E-E-A-T (experience, expertise, authority, trust)

**Reality check:**
- Can you write 4 high-quality blog posts/month? YES/NO
- Do you have deep industry knowledge to share? YES/NO
- Can you write in a clear, direct style? YES/NO

**If you answered NO:** You'll need to hire a content writer (RM800-2,000/month).

### Requirement 3: SEO Knowledge

**You'll need to understand:**
- Keyword research and intent analysis
- On-page optimization (title tags, meta descriptions, headers)
- Internal linking strategy
- AI Overview optimization techniques
- Schema markup types and implementation
- Technical SEO (crawling, indexing, site architecture)

**Learning curve:** 40-60 hours of study + 6 months practice

**Resources to consume:**
- Google's Search Central documentation
- Ahrefs/SEMrush learning academies
- Schema.org documentation
- AI search engine guidelines (Bing, Google, ChatGPT)

**Reality check:**
- Do you have 40+ hours to learn SEO basics? YES/NO
- Can you explain the difference between title and H1 tags? YES/NO
- Do you know what JSON-LD is? YES/NO

**If you answered NO:** Expect 3-6 months of learning before executing effectively.

### Requirement 4: Consistency & Discipline

**AEO requires:**
- Weekly content publishing (every single week)
- Monthly technical audits
- Quarterly strategy adjustments
- Ongoing competitor monitoring
- Schema markup updates

**Time commitment breakdown:**
- Keyword research: 3 hours/week
- Content writing: 8 hours/week
- Technical optimization: 2 hours/week
- Performance tracking: 1 hour/week
- Learning/staying updated: 1 hour/week
- **Total:** 15 hours/week

**Reality check:**
- Can you commit 15 hours/week for 12 months? YES/NO
- Will you still be consistent after 3 months of zero results? YES/NO
- Can you maintain this schedule during busy periods? YES/NO

**If you answered NO:** You'll quit before seeing results.

### Requirement 5: Analytical Mindset

**You'll need to:**

- Track AI Overview appearances

- Calculate ROI from AEO efforts
- A/B test different strategies
- Pivot when something isn't working

**Metrics to track:**

**Reality check:**

- Can you connect search rankings to revenue? YES/NO

**If you answered NO:** You'll optimize the wrong things and waste time.

## **When DIY Makes Sense (And When It Doesn't)**

### DIY AEO Makes Sense IF:

**Scenario 1: You're Building an AEO Agency**
- You want to offer AEO services
- DIY learning = investment in future business
- Timeline: 6-12 months learning, then monetize
- **Verdict:** DIY is the right path

**Scenario 2: You Have Zero Budget and Abundant Time**
- Startup with RM0 marketing budget
- You're working ON the business full-time
- Can invest 20 hours/week for 12 months
- **Verdict:** DIY is the only option

**Scenario 3: You Have Technical Skills and Love Learning**
- Web developer or marketer by trade
- Enjoyed technical SEO challenges
- Already know HTML/CSS/JavaScript
- **Verdict:** DIY could work

**Scenario 4: Your Business is Low-Competition Niche**
- Tiny market with weak competitors
- 10 searches/month for target keywords
- Not worth hiring agency (won't get ROI)
- **Verdict:** DIY is sufficient

### DIY AEO Does NOT Make Sense IF:

**Scenario 1: You're Running a Profitable Business**
- Revenue: RM20,000+/month
- Time is worth RM100+/hour
- Can afford RM1,200/month
- **Verdict:** Hire an agency. 79% cheaper in true cost.

**Scenario 2: You Need Results in 6 Months**
- Launching new product/service
- Competitive market (construction, logistics, etc.)
- Can't wait 12+ months for DIY to work
- **Verdict:** Hire an agency. 6 months faster results.

**Scenario 3: You Hate Technical Work**
- Not interested in learning HTML/schema
- Get frustrated by analytics
- Prefer focusing on core business
- **Verdict:** Hire an agency. Protect your sanity.

**Scenario 4: You've Tried DIY Before and Failed**
- Previous SEO attempts produced zero results
- You started strong but quit after 2 months
- Content is outdated/incomplete
- **Verdict:** Hire an agency. Break the failure cycle.
