## **Getting Started with Tourism AEO**

### **Step 1: Audit Your Current Presence**

**Check if you appear in AI Overviews:**
1. Search your top 10 keywords
2. Note AI Overview citations
3. Identify which competitors appear
4. Spot content gaps (queries you should rank for)

**Tool:** Manual search (free) or Semrush Position Tracking (paid)

### **Step 2: Create Answer-Optimized Content**

**Content formula for tourism AEO:**
1. **Direct answer block** (50-75 words at top)
2. **Comparison tables** (hotels, packages, prices)
3. **FAQ sections** (with schema markup)
4. **Itinerary guides** (step-by-step formats)
5. **Location-based lists** (Top 10, Best 5, etc.)

**Example structure:**
```markdown
## Best Family Resorts Penang 2026

[Direct answer: 3-5 top resorts with pricing]

### Quick Comparison

| Resort | Price/Night | Best For | Rating |
|--------|-------------|----------|---------|
| Resort A | RM450 | Families with kids | 4.8 |
| Resort B | RM380 | Budget-conscious | 4.6 |

[Detailed reviews, FAQ, booking info]
```

### **Step 3: Add Schema Markup**

**Essential schemas for tourism:**
- **FAQPage** (for common questions)
- **Review** (builds trust with AI engines)
- **LocalBusiness** (location-based queries)
- **Article** (guide content)

**Implementation:** Hire a developer or use AEO agency (recommended for non-technical tourism operators)
