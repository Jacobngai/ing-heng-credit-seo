## **Google Business Profile for Local AI**

### Complete Your Profile

**Essential elements:**
1. **Business name** (consistent everywhere)
2. **Address** (actual location, not PO Box)
3. **Phone** (local area code)
4. **Website** (URL to your site)
5. **Hours** (accurate, updated)
6. **Services** (detailed descriptions)
7. **Service areas** (cities/regions)
8. **Photos** (50+ minimum)
9. **Posts** (weekly updates)
10. **Reviews** (100+, 4.8+ rating)

**Why:** AI engines scan Google Business Profile data for local recommendations.
