## **Service-Specific AIEO Strategy**

### 1. Google Business Profile Optimization

**Critical for local service businesses:**

**Complete all fields:**
- Business name (consistent)
- Address (actual location)
- Phone number (local area code)
- Website URL
- Business hours (accurate)
- Services (detailed descriptions)
- Service areas (cities/regions covered)

**Upload photos:**
- 20+ photos minimum
- Types: Team, office, work examples, equipment
- High quality, professional

**Collect reviews:**
- Target: 100+ reviews
- Rating: 4.8+ minimum
- Respond to every review

### 2. FAQ Pages for Services

**Create comprehensive FAQ pages:**

**Service-specific questions:**
- "How much does [service] cost?"
- "How long does [service] take?"
- "Do you offer emergency [service]?"
- "What areas do you serve?"
- "Are you licensed and insured?"
- "Do you offer guarantees?"

**Implementation:**
- Dedicate page to FAQs
- Add FAQPage schema markup
- Direct answers first
- Detailed explanations follow

### 3. LocalBusiness Schema

**Essential for service businesses:**

```json
{
  "@type": "LocalBusiness",
  "name": "Your Service Business",
  "address": {...},
  "telephone": "+60175032281",
  "openingHours": "Mo-Su 00:00-23:59",
  "priceRange": "$$",
  "areaServed": ["Kuala Lumpur", "Petaling Jaya", "Subang Jaya"]
}
```

### 4. Service Schema

**Describe your services:**

```json
{
  "@type": "Service",
  "name": "Emergency Plumbing Service",
  "description": "24/7 emergency plumbing repairs in Kuala Lumpur",
  "provider": {
    "@type": "LocalBusiness",
    "name": "ABC Plumbing"
  },
  "areaServed": "Kuala Lumpur",
  "hasOfferCatalog": {
    "@type": "OfferCatalog",
    "itemListElement": [
      {
        "@type": "Offer",
        "itemOffered": "Emergency Repair",
        "price": "150",
        "priceCurrency": "MYR"
      }
    ]
  }
}
```

## **Content Strategy for Services**

### 1. Problem-Solution Content

**Address customer pain points:**

**Format:**
**Problem:** Describe issue customers face
**Solution:** Explain how your service solves it
**Proof:** Show case studies, testimonials
**CTA:** Contact for service

**Example:**
"Burst pipe in Kuala Lumpur? We provide 24/7 emergency plumbing services. Average response time: 30 minutes. Call now for immediate assistance."

### 2. How-To Guides

**Demonstrate expertise:**

**Topics:**
- "How to Choose a [Service Provider]"
- "How to Prepare for [Service]"
- "What to Expect During [Service]"
- "DIY vs Professional [Service]"

**Why:** Builds trust, demonstrates expertise, gets cited by AI.

### 3. Pricing Transparency

**Be upfront about costs:**

**Include:**
- Starting prices
- What affects pricing
- Payment options
- Guarantees

**Example:**
"Emergency plumbing services start at RM150. Final cost depends on:
- Time of day (emergency surcharge after hours)
- Parts needed
- Complexity of repair
- We provide free estimates before starting work."

### 4. Social Proof

**Showcase results:**

**Types:**
- Before/after photos
- Video testimonials
- Case studies with metrics
- Review highlights
- Awards and certifications

**Where:** Home page, service pages, dedicated testimonials page.
