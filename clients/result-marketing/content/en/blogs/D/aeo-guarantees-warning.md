**No.** No agency, consultant, or tool can guarantee your website will appear in AI overviews or achieve specific rankings. Google's AI algorithms constantly change, and placement depends on countless factors including content quality, authority, relevance, and competitor activity.

## **Why AEO Guarantees Are Impossible**

### Reason #1: Google Controls the Algorithm

You don't own Google. You don't control their AI. You can't guarantee what Google's algorithm will do tomorrow.

**Google's AI Changes Constantly:**
- 2024: Gemini overhaul
- 2025: AI Overview expansion to 100+ countries
- 2026: Multiple algorithm updates (SpamBrain, MUM, BERT iterations)

What worked last month might not work today. No one‚Äîabsolutely no one‚Äîcan predict or guarantee AI placement.

### Reason #2: Competitors Keep Changing

You might have the best content today, but tomorrow a competitor publishes something better. Your ranking drops.

**Example:**
You're the #1 source for "crane rental pricing in Malaysia." Next week, a competitor publishes original research with 500 data points. They get cited; you don't.

Did your agency fail? No. The landscape changed.

### Reason #3: User Intent Evolves

User queries shift over time. Questions change. New topics emerge.

**2025 Query:** "How much does crane rental cost?"
**2026 Query:** "What's the total cost of crane rental including operator and transport?"

If your content doesn't evolve, you lose AI citations‚Äîeven if you were previously ranking.

## **What "Guaranteed" Agencies Actually Do**

### Black-Hat Tactic #1: Schema Spam

They generate hundreds of FAQ pages with AI, add schema markup for questions not actually answered, and hope Google doesn't notice.

**Result:** Temporary AI citations, followed by manual action penalty.

### Black-Hat Tactic #2: Fake Authority Building

They buy links from "AI authority" sites, PBNs, or content farms claiming to boost your AI visibility.

**Result:** Wasted money, zero real authority, potential unnatural links penalty.

### Black-Hat Tactic #3: Doorway Pages

They create multiple pages targeting every possible keyword variation with identical content.

**Result:** Algorithmic suppression for thin content, no meaningful traffic.

### Black-Hat Tactic #4: The "Money-Back Guarantee" Trick

They promise "guaranteed results or your money back." When you don't see results, they blame you:
- "You didn't approve our recommendations fast enough"

**Result:** They keep your money, you keep nothing.

## **FAQ**

### Why can't agencies guarantee AEO results?

AI placement depends on Google's proprietary algorithms, which constantly change. Agencies don't control Google, competitor actions, or user behavior. Ethical agencies can improve probability of success but never guarantee outcomes.

### Are there any legitimate AEO guarantees?

The only legitimate "guarantee" in AEO is satisfaction guarantees on agency work (e.g., "If you're not satisfied with our strategy after 30 days, we'll refund your money"). No ethical agency guarantees specific rankings or AI citations.

### What should I do if an agency promises guaranteed AI rankings?

Run away. Agencies promising guaranteed results are either lying about their capabilities (to get your money) or planning to use black-hat tactics (which will trigger penalties). Both are bad for your business.

### How do I know if my current AEO agency is using black-hat tactics?

Warning signs: guaranteed results, secret techniques, one-time payment for lifetime results, automated schema generation, lack of transparency about process, refusal to explain specific tactics.

### What's a realistic expectation for AEO investment?

Expect to invest RM12,000-24,000/year for ethical AEO services. Results typically begin appearing in 3-6 months. Most businesses see positive ROI within 9-12 months through increased organic traffic and leads.
