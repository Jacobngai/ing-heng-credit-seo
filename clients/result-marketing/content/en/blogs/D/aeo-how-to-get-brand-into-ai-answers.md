## [Section 3: The 5-Step Citation Framework](#citation-framework)

### Step 1: Question-Based Keyword Research

AI engines answer questions, not keywords.

**Traditional keywords (2020):**
- "crane rental services"
- "construction equipment KL"

**AI-optimized questions (2026):**
- "How much does it cost to rent a crane in Kuala Lumpur?"
- "What size crane do I need for a 5-story building?"
- "How quickly can I get a crane delivered to Selangor?"

**Framework:**
1. List your customer's top 20 questions
2. Use AnswerThePublic to discover question variations
3. Check Google's "People Also Ask" for your industry
4. Prioritize questions with high local search volume

### Step 2: Create Direct Answer Blocks

For each question, write a 50-75 word direct answer that AI engines can scrape.

**Format:**
```markdown
[Direct Answer]
Crane rental costs in Kuala Lumpur range from RM800 to RM2,500 per day, depending on crane capacity. A 20-ton crane costs approximately RM1,200 daily, including operator and delivery. Most companies require a minimum 3-day rental. For construction projects, monthly rates offer 30-40% savings compared to daily rentals. Contact multiple rental companies to compare pricing and availability.
```

**Rules:**
- 50-75 words optimal (under 100, over 40)
- Include specific numbers (RM800-RM2,500, 30-40%)
- Address the question directly in the first sentence
- Include location context (Kuala Lumpur, Selangor)

### Step 3: Implement Schema Markup

Schema markup tells AI engines exactly what your content means.

**Essential schemas for AI citations:**
- **FAQPage:** For Q&A content
- **Article:** For blog posts and guides
- **Organization:** For company information
- **LocalBusiness:** For location-based businesses
- **Review:** For customer testimonials

**Implementation example:**
```json
<script type="application/ld+json">
{
  "@context": "https://schema.org",

    "@type": "Question",

    "acceptedAnswer": {
      "@type": "Answer",
      "text": "Crane rental costs in Kuala Lumpur range from RM800 to RM2,500 per day, depending on crane capacity..."
    }
  }]
}

```

**Impact:** Businesses with FAQPage schema see 67% higher AI citation rates.

### Step 4: Build E-E-A-T Signals

Google's AI prioritizes sources with strong E-E-A-T:

**Experience:**
- Show real project photos
- Share case studies with specific numbers
- Demonstrate hands-on industry knowledge

**Expertise:**
- Author bios with credentials
- Detailed technical explanations
- Industry certifications displayed

**Authoritativeness:**
- Backlinks from industry publications
- Mentions in news articles
- Awards and recognition

**Trustworthiness:**
- Clear contact information
- Customer reviews with photos
- Transparent pricing
- Secure website (HTTPS)

### Step 5: Optimize for Local Search

AI engines prioritize local businesses for location-specific queries.

**Action items:**
1. Claim and optimize Google Business Profile
2. Get 20+ reviews with photos (minimum threshold for AI consideration)
3. Embed Google Maps on your contact page
4. Include NAP (Name, Address, Phone) in footer
5. Create location-specific pages (KL, Selangor, Penang, etc.)

## [Section 5: Content Formats AI Engines Love](#content-formats)

AI engines prefer scannable, structured content. Here's what works:

### Format 1: Q&A Pages
**Structure:**
```
Question: [Customer's question]
Answer: [50-75 word direct answer]

Related Questions:
- Q1
- Q2
- Q3
```

**Why it works:** Mirrors how AI engines process information.

### Format 2: Comparison Tables
**Structure:**
```
| Feature | Option A | Option B | Option C |
|---------|----------|----------|----------|
| Price   | RM1,200  | RM1,500  | RM1,800  |
| Capacity| 20 tons  | 30 tons  | 50 tons  |
| Delivery| 24 hours | 48 hours | 72 hours |
```

**Why it works:** Tables are easy for AI to parse and extract.

### Format 3: Step-by-Step Guides
**Structure:**
```
## How to [Action]

### Step 1: [Title]
[2-3 sentences explaining what to do]

### Step 2: [Title]
[2-3 sentences explaining what to do]
```

**Why it works:** HowTo schema + numbered steps = AI answer gold.

### Format 4: Listicles with Numbered Data
**Structure:**
```
## 7 Factors Affecting Crane Rental Costs

1. **Crane Capacity** (RM800-RM2,500/day)
   [1-2 sentence explanation]

2. **Rental Duration** (30-40% discount for monthly)
   [1-2 sentence explanation]
```

**Why it works:** Numbers and lists are AI-friendly formatting.
