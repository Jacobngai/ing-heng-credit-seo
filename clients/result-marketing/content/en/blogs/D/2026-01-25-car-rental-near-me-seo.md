**Critical for "near me" searches.** Google Maps appears above organic results.

**Setup checklist:**
- [ ] Verify ownership (postcard or video verification)
- [ ] Accurate NAP (Name, Address, Phone) - MUST match website
- [ ] Primary category: "Car rental agency"
- [ ] Secondary category: "Van rental agency" (if applicable)
- [ ] Upload 20+ photos (fleet, office, team, happy customers)
- [ ] Get 30+ reviews (target 4.8+ stars)
- [ ] Enable messaging (WhatsApp for quick quotes)
- [ ] Add services (vehicle types, with/without driver)
- [ ] Set service areas (specific radius or cities)
- [ ] Add business hours (24/7 if applicable)
