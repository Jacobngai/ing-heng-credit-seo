## Factors That Speed Up or Slow Down SEO

### Speeds Up SEO:

**Low Competition Keywords**
- "Crane rental in Cheras" (faster)
- vs. "Crane rental Kuala Lumpur" (slower)

**Existing Authority**
- Old domain (5+ years) = faster
- New domain = slower

**Technical Excellence**
- Fast website = faster
- Mobile-friendly = faster
- Good structure = faster

**Content Quality**
- Comprehensive content = faster
- Thin content = slower

**High Activity**
- Weekly updates = faster
- Monthly updates = slower

### Slows Down SEO:

**High Competition**
- Major cities (KL, Penang) = slower
- Niche keywords = faster

**New Website**
- 0-6 months old = slow start
- 12+ months old = faster

**Technical Issues**
- Slow site = slower
- Not mobile-friendly = slower
- Broken links = slower

**Low Authority**
- No backlinks = slower
- New domain = slower

**Inconsistent Work**
- Pause SEO = lose momentum
- Monthly updates = steady growth
