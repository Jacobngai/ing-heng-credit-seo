## Inventory Pages That Rank

Vehicle dealers have a unique SEO advantage: **inventory pages**.

### Why Inventory Pages Rank Well

**Google loves vehicle inventory pages because:**
- Regularly updated (new vehicles added)
- Structured data (make, model, year, price, mileage)
- High user engagement (buyers spend time browsing)
- Natural internal linking (related vehicles)

**The strategy:**
Create SEO-optimized pages for each vehicle category + individual vehicle pages.

### Category Pages (High Traffic)

**Examples:**
- "Used Proton Cars for Sale in Kuala Lumpur"
- "Second Hand Perodua Below RM20,000"
- "Toyota Vios for Sale Selangor"
- "MPV Cars for Sale Malaysia"
- "Luxury Cars Under RM100,000"

**Each category page should include:**
- Category in title tag and H1
- Price range clearly stated
- Year range (e.g., "2015-2022 models")
- Grid of current inventory (10-20 vehicles)
- Filters (price, year, mileage, location)
- Popular searches in this category
- Buying guide for this vehicle type

### Individual Vehicle Pages (High Conversion)

**URL structure:**
`domain.com/used-toyota-vios-2019-kuala-lumpur`

**Title tag:**
"2019 Toyota Vios 1.5 G for Sale in Kuala Lumpur | RM65,000 | 50,000km"

**Page content:**
- Vehicle specifications (year, make, model, variant)
- Mileage, condition, service history
- High-quality photos (20-30 photos, interior + exterior)
- Price prominently displayed
- Comparison (market price vs your price)
- Vehicle highlights (low mileage, 1 owner, accident-free)
- Financing calculator
- WhatsApp click-to-chat
- Test drive booking form
- Similar vehicles for sale

### Schema Markup for Vehicles

**Implement Vehicle Schema:**

```json
{
  "@type": "Car",
  "name": "2019 Toyota Vios 1.5 G",
  "vehicleConfiguration": "Sedan",
  "modelDate": "2019",
  "mileageFromOdometer": {
    "@type": "QuantitativeValue",
    "value": "50000",
    "unitCode": "KMT"
  },
  "offers": {
    "@type": "Offer",
    "price": "65000",
    "priceCurrency": "MYR",
    "availability": "https://schema.org/InStock"
  }
}
```

**Result:**
Your inventory shows up in Google's rich results with:
- Image
- Price
- Mileage
- Year
- Dealer name

**Click-through rate increase: 30-50%**

## Lead Capture for Vehicle Buyers

Vehicle buyers have high intent but need nurturing.

### Multi-Stage Lead Capture

**Stage 1: Quick Inquiry (Low Friction)**
- WhatsApp click-to-chat button (prominent)
- Pre-filled message: "Hi, I'm interested in the [vehicle name]. Is it still available?"
- Response time: < 30 minutes
- Goal: Answer basic questions, build trust

**Stage 2: Test Drive Booking (Medium Friction)**
- Simple form: Name, phone, preferred date/time
- Offer instant confirmation
- Send reminder SMS/WhatsApp
- Goal: Get them to the showroom

**Stage 3: Financing Application (High Friction)**
- Detailed form (only after test drive)
- Income, employment, address
- Pre-approval decision
- Goal: Close the sale

### Speed-to-Lead Matters

**Vehicle buyers contact multiple dealers.**

**Statistics:**
- 78% buy from the **first** dealer to respond
- Average buyer contacts 3-5 dealers
- Response time < 30 minutes = 60% conversion
- Response time > 2 hours = 20% conversion

**Your advantage with SEO:**
- You own the lead (not paying marketplace commission)
- You get the lead first (they found you directly)
- No bidding against other dealers

### WhatsApp Integration (Critical)

**Malaysian car buyers prefer WhatsApp.**

**Implement:**
1. Click-to-chat button on every vehicle page
2. Auto-reply with vehicle details
3. Send photos/videos via WhatsApp
4. Schedule test drives via WhatsApp
5. Send location/map to showroom
6. Follow up automatically

**Example WhatsApp flow:**
