## Why CPC Varies by Industry

### Factor 1: Customer Lifetime Value

Google's auction considers how much a customer is worth.

**High LTV industries = higher CPC:**
- Construction equipment rental (RM50,000-200,000 per contract)
- Emergency services (RM500-5,000 per job)
- Vehicle rental (RM10,000-100,000 per contract)

**Lower LTV industries = lower CPC:**
- Cleaning services (RM200-800 per job)
- Basic home repairs (RM300-1,500 per job)

### Factor 2: Urgency

Urgent needs = higher CPC.

**Emergency keywords ("emergency", "24 hour"):**
- Immediate need
- Less price-sensitive
- Higher conversion (20-40%)
- **Higher CPC: RM15-25**

**Non-emergency keywords:**
- Planned purchase
- Price-sensitive
- Lower conversion (5-15%)
- **Lower CPC: RM5-12**

### Factor 3: Competition

More advertisers bidding = higher CPC.

**High competition industries:**
- Construction equipment (lots of rental companies)
- Emergency services (many service providers)
- Vehicle services (established market)

**Lower competition industries:**
- Specialized services (niche markets)
- New service types (emerging markets)
