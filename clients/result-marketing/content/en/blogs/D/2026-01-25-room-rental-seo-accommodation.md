## **Room Listing SEO Optimization**

**Each room listing needs:**

### 1. Optimized Title
- Include room type, location, key feature
- Example: "Master Room for Rent Subang Jaya - Aircond, WiFi, Near LRT"
- Length: 50-60 characters

### 2. Professional Photos (15+ images)
- Room (bed, window, built-in cabinet)
- Bathroom (attached or shared)
- Common areas (living room, kitchen)
- Facilities (washing machine, fridge, WiFi)

### 3. Detailed Description
- Room details (size, furnished, aircond)
- Facilities included (WiFi, water, electricity)
- Location highlights (near MRT/LRT, malls)
- Housemate profile (who else lives there)
- Rental terms (deposit, preferred tenant)

### 4. Clear Rental Terms
- Monthly rent
- Deposit amount
- Utilities included or separate
- Preferred tenant (gender, profession, student)
- House rules (cooking, visitors, pets)

- Pre-filled message: "Hi, I'm interested in your master room for rent in Subang Jaya"
