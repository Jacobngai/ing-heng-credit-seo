## The Framework: Industry AEO

### 1. Target Industry Questions
- "How much does [industry service] cost?"
- "Best [industry] company in [location]"
- "What to look for in [industry] provider"
- "[Industry] vs [alternative] comparison"

### 2. Create Industry Content
- Case studies from your sector
- Industry statistics and data
- Comparison guides
- How-to guides specific to industry

### 3. Build Industry Authority
- Get featured in industry publications
- Collect industry-specific reviews
- Display certifications and credentials
- Show team experience

### 4. Use Industry Schema
- Organization schema with industry details
- Product/Service schema for offerings
- Review schema for client testimonials
- FAQ schema for common questions
