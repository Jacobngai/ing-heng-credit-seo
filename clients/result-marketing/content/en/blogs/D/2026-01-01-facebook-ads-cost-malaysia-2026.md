## Why Facebook Ads Keep Getting Expensive

### Reason 1: Auction-Based Pricing

Facebook Ads work on a real-time auction system.

**How it works:**
1. Advertiser sets maximum bid per click
2. Facebook runs auction every time an ad loads
3. Highest bidder + best ad quality wins the impression
4. More bidders = higher prices

**The problem:**
- More Malaysian businesses advertising every year
- Same audience size = more competition
- Auction prices keep rising

**Simple supply and demand:**
- Supply: Fixed (Malaysian Facebook users ~30 million)
- Demand: Increasing (more businesses advertising)
- Result: Higher prices

### Reason 2: Ad Saturation

Malaysians see too many ads.

**Statistics:**
- Average Facebook user sees 5,000 ads/month
- 76% of users ignore sponsored posts
- Ad recall drops 50% after 3rd exposure
- Click-through rates declining 15% year-over-year

**What this means:**
To get the same results, you must:
- Bid higher (costs more)
- Create better ads (takes more time)
- Accept lower conversion (wastes money)

### Reason 3: iOS Privacy Changes

Apple's 2021 privacy update crippled Facebook's tracking.

**Impact:**
- Facebook can't track as much user behavior
- Less targeting data = less precise targeting
- Less precise = lower conversion rates
- Lower conversion = higher effective cost per lead

**Before iOS update:**
- Could track users across websites/apps
- Highly targeted custom audiences
- 5-10% conversion rates

**After iOS update:**
- Limited tracking
- Broader targeting
- 2-5% conversion rates

Same spend. Half the results.
