## **Claude's Ranking Factors**

### 1. Authority and Trust (Critical)

**Claude prioritizes:**
- Established brands with track records
- Educational and government sources
- Industry publications and research
- Sites with clear credentials

**How to build:**
- Showcase team credentials
- Display certifications and awards
- Publish case studies with data
- Get featured in reputable publications

### 2. Content Accuracy

**Claude requires:**
- Factual, verifiable information
- Citations to primary sources
- Up-to-date data (2026, not 2019)
- Transparent sourcing

**How to demonstrate:**
- Link to primary sources
- Update content regularly
- Include statistics with sources
- Avoid speculation or exaggeration

### 3. Content Clarity

**Claude prefers:**
- Direct, concise answers
- Structured formatting (H1, H2, H3)
- Bullet points for readability
- Clear explanations without jargon

**Example structure:**
"How much does SEO cost in Malaysia?

SEO costs RM1,200-3,000 per month in Malaysia (2026). Pricing depends on keyword difficulty, competition level, and service scope.

**Breakdown:**
- Basic SEO: RM1,200-1,800/month
- Advanced SEO: RM2,000-3,000/month
- Enterprise SEO: RM3,500+/month

**Factors affecting pricing:**
- Keyword competition
- Current website authority
- Content creation needs
- Link building requirements"

### 4. Ethical Business Practices

**Claude's safety focus means:**
- Prefers businesses with ethical practices
- Avoids recommending controversial brands
- Values transparency and honesty
- Penalizes deceptive tactics

**How to align:**
- Transparent pricing
- Clear terms and conditions
- Genuine reviews (no fake reviews)
- Honest marketing claims

## **Optimization Strategies**

### 1. Create Authoritative Content

**Comprehensive guides:**
- 3,000+ word pillar pages
- Cover topics in-depth
- Include case studies and data
- Update regularly

**Example topics:**
- "Complete SEO Guide for Construction Businesses"
- "Equipment Rental SEO: From Strategy to Results"
- "Local SEO Malaysia: 2026 Guide"

### 2. Demonstrate Expertise

**Show credentials:**
- Author bios with qualifications
- Team profiles with experience
- Client case studies with results
- Industry awards and recognition

**Example author bio:**
"John Smith has 12 years experience in SEO, specializing in construction equipment and vehicle rental industries. Certified Google Analytics expert. Helped 50+ businesses achieve #1 rankings."

### 3. Use Structured Data

**Schema markup helps Claude understand:**

**FAQPage:**
```json
{
  "@type": "FAQPage",
  "mainEntity": [{
    "@type": "Question",
    "name": "How much does SEO cost?",
    "acceptedAnswer": {
      "@type": "Answer",
      "text": "RM1,200-3,000 per month in Malaysia."
    }
  }]
}
```

**Article:**
```json
{
  "@type": "Article",
  "headline": "SEO Pricing Guide Malaysia 2026",
  "author": {
    "@type": "Person",
    "name": "John Smith",
    "credentials": "Google Certified"
  },
  "datePublished": "2026-01-08"
}
```
