## **Direct Answer: Does LocalBusiness Schema Help You Show Up in Local AI Search?**

Yes. When users ask AI engines "Where's the best [service] in [city]?" or "Who offers [product] near me?", the systems prioritize businesses with LocalBusiness Schema. This markup provides structured location data (address, phone, service area, hours) that AI engines use to generate local recommendations. Local businesses with schema are 5x more likely to be cited in location-based AI answers compared to those without.
