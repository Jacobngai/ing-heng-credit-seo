## **Direct Answer: How Do I Know if an AEO Agency is Legitimate?**

**A good AEO agency demonstrates 4 non-negotiables:** (1) Live AI Overview case studies with before/after proof, (2) Deep technical knowledge of schema markup and structured data, (3) Realistic timelines (60-90 days), not promises of 2-week results, and (4) Client retention above 80%. If they lack any of these, they're either inexperienced or rebranding traditional SEO as AEO.

## **Sign #2: They Speak AEO, Not SEO Language**

**Good AEO language:**
- "AI Overview citations"
- "Answer Engine Optimization"
- "Schema markup for AI search"
- "Structured data optimization"
- "Content formatting for AI engines"

**SEO language (red flag):**
- "Backlinks"
- "Domain authority"
- "Keyword density"
- "PageRank"
- "Link building"

**The difference:** AEO is about earning citations in AI answers. SEO is about ranking in traditional blue links. Good agencies understand this distinction.

## **Sign #3: Realistic Timelines (60-90 Days Minimum)**

**Good agencies say:**
- "60-90 days for initial AI Overview citations"
- "4-6 months for consistent visibility"
- "Results depend on competition and content quality"

**Bad agencies promise:**
- "2 weeks to AI Overviews!" (Lying)
- "Guaranteed citations in 30 days or money back" (Unrealistic)
- "We've cracked the AI search code" (Scam language)

**Why it matters:** AI search engines need time to discover, index, and test content. 60-90 days is the minimum realistic timeline for most industries.

## **Sign #4: Transparent Pricing (RM1,500-3,000/month)**

**Fair pricing ranges:**

- Technical specialists (RM4,000-7,000/month salary)
- Tools and software (RM500-1,000/month)

Agencies charging RM800/month can't afford quality team members.

## **FAQ: Evaluating AEO Agencies**

### **What if an agency has good case studies but low retention?**

**Answer:** Investigate why. Good results but low retention might mean poor communication, pricing issues, or scope creep. Ask to speak to former clients to understand why they left.

### **Can a good agency be new (less than 1 year old)?**

**Answer:** Possible but risky. New agencies might lack experience, even if they're talented. Look for: (1) Previous SEO experience they're adapting to AEO, (2) Lower pricing to offset risk, (3) Initial results they can show. If they have none of these, wait until they build a track record.

### **Should I choose an agency based on price?**

**Answer:** No. Choose based on proof and process first, then compare pricing among qualified agencies. RM1,500-3,000/month is fair. Below RM1,200 is suspicious. Above RM5,000 should be justified with dedicated teams and comprehensive services.

### **Do good agencies always have industry-specific experience?**

**Answer:** Preferred but not required. What matters more: (1) Understanding of AEO technical requirements, (2) Proven results in any industry, (3) Ability to learn your industry quickly. A great generalist AEO agency beats a mediocre specialist.

### **How do I verify AI Overview case studies are real?**

**Answer:** (1) Check the URLs exist and are live, (2) Search the queries yourself to see if they still appear, (3) Ask for client contact to verify, (4) Look for dates (recent is better than old), (5) Request before/after screenshots showing improvement.
