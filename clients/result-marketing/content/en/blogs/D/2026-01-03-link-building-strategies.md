## What Are Backlinks?

**Definition:** Links from other websites to your website.

**Why they matter:**
- Google's #1 ranking factor (after content)
- Each link = "vote of confidence" in your site
- More quality links = higher rankings

**Not all links equal:**
- Link from New York Times = 100x more powerful than link from spam site
- Relevant links (same industry) more valuable than random links
- Local links (Malaysian sites) most valuable for Malaysian businesses

**Link quality factors:**
- Domain authority (higher = better)
- Relevance (same industry = better)
- Anchor text (descriptive = better)
- Placement (in content = better than sidebar/footer)

## Link Building Strategies

### 1. Create Linkable Assets

**What are linkable assets?**
- Content so valuable people naturally link to it

**Examples:**
- Industry research/studies with original data
- Comprehensive guides (5,000+ words)
- Tools/calculators
- Infographics with unique data
- Case studies with real results

**Example:**
- Crane rental company creates "Malaysia Construction Equipment Rental Price Guide 2026"
- Other sites link to it as reference
- Earns 50+ quality links naturally

### 2. Guest Posting

**Write articles for other sites in exchange for link back to your site.**

**Process:**
1. Find industry blogs/websites
2. Pitch article ideas (valuable to their audience)
3. Write high-quality article
4. Include link in author bio or content

**Good guest post:**
- Relevant to your industry
- High-quality site (domain authority 40+)
- Natural link placement
- Valuable content (not just self-promotion)

**Example:**
- Excavator rental company guest posts on "Construction Malaysia" blog
- Article: "10 Tips for Choosing the Right Excavator for Your Project"
- Link in author bio: "By [Name], founder of [Company] - excavator rental in KL"

### 3. Digital PR

**Get press coverage = earn links.**

**Strategies:**
- Sponsor local events
- Conduct surveys and share results
- Create shareable data
- Comment on industry news (journalist requests expert quotes)

**Example:**
- Lorry rental company sponsors "Klang Valley Logistics Expo"
- Press release covered by logistics news sites
- Earns 10+ quality links

### 4. Partner Linking

**Get links from business partners.**

**Opportunities:**
- Manufacturers/distributors (list as authorized dealer)
- Suppliers (vendor directory)
- Clients (case studies, testimonials)
- Industry associations (member directory)

**Example:**
- Forklift rental company gets link from Toyota Forklift Malaysia
- Listed as "authorized rental partner"
- High-value link (relevant, authoritative)

## FAQ

**Q: How many backlinks do I need?**
A: Depends on competition. Low competition: 10-20 links. High competition: 50-200 links.

**Q: Should I buy links?**
A: No. Violates Google guidelines. Can get your site penalized. Not worth the risk.

**Q: What's anchor text?**
A: The clickable text in a link. Best practice: mix of branded (your company name), exact match (keywords), and natural (click here).

**Q: How long before new links help rankings?**
A: 1-3 months. Google discovers link, re-evaluates your site, rankings improve gradually.
