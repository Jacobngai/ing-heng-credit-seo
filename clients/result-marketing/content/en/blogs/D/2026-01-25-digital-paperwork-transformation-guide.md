## **What is Digital Paperwork?**

**Digital paperwork = Replace physical documents with digital alternatives.**

### Before (Manual Paperwork)
1. Customer fills form (paper)
2. Staff types data into computer
3. Document printed, signed, filed
4. Courier sends to another location
5. Recipient re-enters data into their system
6. **Time: 3-10 days. Error rate: 3-5%.**

### After (Digital Paperwork)
1. Customer fills form (mobile/web)
2. Data auto-saves to cloud database
3. Digital signature captured
4. Instant transmission to all parties
5. Auto-populates all systems
6. **Time: Instant. Error rate: <0.5%.**

**Benefits:**
- **Speed:** Instant vs days/weeks
- **Accuracy:** Auto-entry vs manual typing errors
- **Cost:** RM1,500/month vs RM10,000/month staff
- **Accessibility:** Search any document in seconds
- **Backup:** Cloud = disaster-proof
