## Answer Engine Optimization Framework

**The Core Principles:**

1. **Direct Answers**
   - Clear, concise responses
   - Q&A format preferred
   - Numbered lists and tables
   - Specific data and statistics

2. **Schema Markup**
   - Article schema for content
   - FAQPage for questions
   - HowTo for guides
   - Organization for brand

3. **E-E-A-T Signals**
   - Experience: Real case studies
   - Expertise: Author credentials
   - Authoritativeness: Backlinks
   - Trust: Reviews and testimonials

4. **Top 3 Rankings**
   - 80% citation rate for #1
   - 65% citation rate for #2
   - 50% citation rate for #3
