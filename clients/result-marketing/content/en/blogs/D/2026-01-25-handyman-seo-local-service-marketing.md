### Service List (Critical for Handymen)

**Customers need to KNOW what you do.** List all services clearly.

**Essential Service List:**
- **Plumbing repairs:** Leaky taps, unclog drains, fix running toilets
- **Electrical repairs:** Replace outlets, fix light switches, install fixtures
- **Carpentry:** Door repair, window repair, shelf installation
- **Painting:** Touch ups, room painting, wall repair
- **Furniture assembly:** IKEA furniture, cabinets, wardrobes
- **Hanging items:** TVs, mirrors, curtains, pictures
- **General repairs:** Drywall, caulking, weatherstripping

### Trust Signals for Handymen

**You're entering people's homes.** Trust is everything.

**Essential Trust Elements:**
- **Background-checked** (if applicable)
- **Own tools + transport** (reliability)
- **Before/after photos** of repairs
- **Uniform/apron** (professionalism)
- **Punctuality guarantee** ("on time or you pay less")
- **Satisfaction guarantee** ("free fix if not happy")
- **Real reviews** with customer photos
- **Police clearance** (optional but builds trust)

## **Building Repeat Business**

### Repeat Customer Strategy

**One-time jobs become recurring when you:**

**1. Do exceptional work**
- Clean up after yourself (customers LOVE this)
- Arrive on time (or text if running late)
- Wear shoe covers (protect their home)
- Explain what you're doing (builds trust)
- Follow-up call (ensure satisfaction)

**2. Make recurring offers**
- "Annual home maintenance plan: RM600/year"
- "4 visits/year = inspections + minor repairs"

**3. Stay top of mind**

### How long does handyman SEO take to work?

**2-3 months** for local keywords like "handyman [your area]". Specific repair keywords (furniture assembly, door repair) rank faster in **1-2 months** (lower competition).

### Should I list all my services on my website?

**Absolutely.** Customers need to know what you do. List every service you offer - even small ones. You never know what someone is searching for. "TV mounting" might be your most profitable service.

### Can I target handyman keywords in multiple languages?
