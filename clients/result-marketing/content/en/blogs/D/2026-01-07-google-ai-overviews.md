## **What Are Google AI Overviews?**

**Google AI Overviews** (formerly **Search Generative Experience** or **SGE**) are AI-generated answers that appear at the top of Google search results.

**Key characteristics:**

- AI-generated summary answering the user's query
- Cites 3-5 authoritative sources
- Appears above traditional blue links
- Conversational, easy-to-understand format
- Includes follow-up question suggestions

## **How Google Selects Sources**

### Selection Criteria

Google's AI evaluates sources based on:

**1. E-E-A-T (Experience, Expertise, Authoritativeness, Trustworthiness)**
- Demonstrated real-world experience
- Credentials and qualifications
- Industry recognition and awards
- Transparent business information
- Genuine customer reviews

**2. Content Quality**
- Clear, direct answers to questions
- Comprehensive coverage of topics
- Accurate, up-to-date information
- Well-structured formatting (headings, lists)
- Proper grammar and spelling

**3. Technical Signals**
- Schema markup (Article, FAQ, HowTo)
- Page speed (Core Web Vitals)
- Mobile-friendliness
- Secure connection (HTTPS)
- Proper internal linking

**4. User Behavior**
- High click-through rate
- Low bounce rate
- Long dwell time
- Positive user engagement
- Social shares and backlinks

### The Citation Process

**Google's AI follows this process:**

1. **Analyze Query:** Understand user intent (informational, commercial, local)
2. **Retrieve Candidates:** Find 50-100 potential sources
3. **Evaluate Quality:** Score based on E-E-A-T, technical signals
4. **Verify Accuracy:** Cross-reference information across sources
5. **Generate Summary:** Create conversational answer
6. **Select Citations:** Choose 3-5 best sources to cite

**Only the top 3-5 make the cut.**

## **Optimization Strategies**

### 1. Create "Answer-First" Content

**Structure your content to directly answer questions:**

**Format:**
```markdown
## [Question]

[Direct answer in 1-2 sentences]

[Detailed explanation with supporting information]

[Examples, data, case studies]

[Conclusion]
```

**Example:**
```markdown
## How Much Does SEO Cost in Malaysia?

SEO costs RM1,200-3,000 per month in Malaysia (2026). Pricing depends on keyword difficulty, competition level, and service scope. Most reputable agencies require 6-12 month commitments.

### Detailed Pricing Breakdown
[...content...]
```

**Google's AI can easily extract and cite this direct answer.**

### 2. Implement Schema Markup

**Critical schema types for AI Overviews:**

**FAQPage Schema:**
```json
{
  "@type": "FAQPage",
  "mainEntity": [{
    "@type": "Question",
    "name": "How much does SEO cost?",
    "acceptedAnswer": {
      "@type": "Answer",
      "text": "RM1,200-3,000 per month in Malaysia."
    }
  }]
}
```

**HowTo Schema:**
```json
{
  "@type": "HowTo",
  "name": "How to Choose an SEO Agency",
  "step": [{
    "@type": "HowToStep",
    "text": "Check their case studies and client results"
  }]
}
```

**Article Schema:**
```json
{
  "@type": "Article",
  "headline": "SEO Pricing Guide Malaysia 2026",
  "author": {
    "@type": "Organization",
    "name": "Result Marketing"
  },
  "datePublished": "2026-01-07"
}
```
