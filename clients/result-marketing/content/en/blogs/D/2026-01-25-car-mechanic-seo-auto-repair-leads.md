## **Converting Repair Leads**

### Service List (Critical for Workshops)

**Customers need to KNOW what you fix.** List all services clearly.

**Essential Service List:**
- **Regular maintenance:** Oil change, filter replacement, tire rotation
- **Brake services:** Pad replacement, disc resurfacing, fluid flush
- **Aircond repair:** Gas top up, compressor replacement, leak repair
- **Engine repair:** Diagnostics, overhaul, replacement
- **Electrical:** Battery, alternator, starter motor
- **Suspension:** Shock absorbers, springs, alignment
- **Transmission:** Fluid change, repair, overhaul
- **Body work:** Scratch repair, dent removal, painting

### Trust Signals for Mechanics

**Car owners are protective of their vehicles.** Build trust upfront.

**Essential Trust Elements:**
- **Qualified mechanics** - show certifications
- **Spare parts warranty** - 6-12 months warranty
- **Transparent pricing** - itemized quotes
- **Before/after photos** - prove quality work
- **Professional equipment** - show your workshop
- **Timely delivery** - "done when promised"
- **Real reviews** with car photos
- **Free inspection** (optional - attracts customers)

### Pricing Transparency Strategy

**Car owners hate surprise bills.** Show pricing ranges to increase inquiries 3x.

**Example Pricing Table:**
```

- Oil change (synthetic): RM200-350
- Oil filter: RM30-80

- Brake pads (front): RM200-400
```

## **Building Loyal Customers**

### Customer Retention Strategy

**Turn one-time repairs into loyal customers:**

**1. Deliver exceptional service**
- On-time delivery (or early)
- Clean car before returning (customers LOVE this)
- Explain repairs clearly (don't confuse them)
- Show old parts (prove work was done)
- Follow-up call (ensure satisfaction)
- Send service reminder (next due date)

## **Frequently Asked Questions**

### How long does car mechanic SEO take to work?

**2-4 months** for local keywords like "car workshop [your area]". Specific repair keywords (brake repair, aircond repair) rank faster in **1-2 months** (lower competition, high intent).

### Should I show exact pricing on my website?

**Use ranges.** Every car is different. Show ranges (e.g., "RM200-400 for oil change") to set expectations. You can add "exact quote after inspection" for complex repairs.

### Can I target car keywords in multiple languages?

**Absolutely.** 40% of Malaysians prefer searching in Malay or Chinese. We create content in EN/ZH/MS. You can use translation apps or hire part-time help for non-English inquiries.

### Do I need photos of my workshop on my website?

**Critical.** Car owners want to see:
- Your workshop (is it clean/professional?)
- Your equipment (do you have modern tools?)
- Your mechanics (are they qualified?)
- Your work (before/after repairs)
