## **Direct Answer: Can You Do AEO Yourself?**

Yes, you can do AEO yourself with 4-8 hours per month and RM200-600 in tools. The core tasks (FAQ schema, question-targeted content, citation monitoring) are learnable for business owners with basic technical skills. DIY works best for local businesses in low-competition niches. Expect 60-70% of agency results in 6-12 months (agencies achieve faster due to experience and tools). Hire when you have multiple locations, compete in aggressive markets, or simply lack time for consistent execution.

## [DIY AEO Framework](#diy-aeo-framework)

### Phase 1: Foundation (Month 1)

**Technical Setup (3-4 hours):**
1. Install schema plugin (RankMath, Yoast, or Schema Pro)
2. Add organization schema to homepage
3. Add FAQ schema to key service pages
4. Set up Google Search Console
5. Install GA4 (if not already done)

**Content Audit (2-3 hours):**
1. List your top 20 customer questions
2. Identify which pages answer them
3. Note gaps (questions without answers)
4. Prioritize by search volume (use Google Trends, free)

**Baseline Metrics (1 hour):**
1. Current organic traffic (GA4)
2. Current AI citations (manual check of 20 queries)
3. Branded search volume (GSC)
4. Competitor citation count

---

### Phase 2: Content Creation (Months 2-4)

**Question-Targeted Content:**
- **Week 1:** Write 2 FAQ pages (50+ questions each)
- **Week 2:** Create 3 "how to" articles
- **Week 3:** Write 2 comparison articles ("X vs Y")
- **Week 4:** Update 2 existing pages with FAQ schema

**Content Templates That Work:**

**FAQ Page Template:**
```markdown
# [Service] FAQ: [Number] Questions Answered

## General Questions
- Q1: [Question]
  - A: [Direct answer, 50-75 words]

## Pricing Questions
- Q: How much does [service] cost?
  - A: [Price range with context]

## Process Questions
- Q: How long does [service] take?
  - A: [Timeline with stages]

[Add FAQ schema to each Q&A pair]
```

**"How To" Article Template:**
```markdown
# How to [Achieve Outcome] in [Timeframe]

## Direct Answer
[50-75 word summary answering the question directly]

## Steps to [Achieve Outcome]
1. [Step 1]
2. [Step 2]
3. [Step 3]

## What You'll Need
- [Requirement 1]
- [Requirement 2]

## Common Mistakes to Avoid
- [Mistake 1]
- [Mistake 2]

[Add Article schema]
```

### Phase 3: Citation Building (Months 3-6)

**Weekly Tasks (1-2 hours):**
1. Check 50 core queries for AI Overviews
2. Note who's cited (you or competitors)
3. Identify why competitors are cited
4. Adjust your content to match citation patterns

**Monthly Tasks (2-3 hours):**
1. Build 5-10 new citations (directory listings, guest posts)
2. Update 2-3 existing articles with fresh data
3. Respond to all reviews (if applicable)
4. Request reviews from happy customers

---

### Phase 4: Maintenance (Month 6+)

**Ongoing Tasks (4-6 hours/month):**
1. Create 2-4 new pieces of content
2. Update 2-3 existing articles
3. Monitor citations weekly
4. Respond to reviews
5. Review GA4/GSC monthly

## [When to Hire Instead](#when-to-hire-instead)

### Hire An Agency If...

**Multiple Locations:**
- You have 3+ locations
- Each location needs individual citations
- Managing 50+ queries per location = overwhelming
- **Hire:** RM2,500-4,000/month handles all locations

**Zero Time:**
- You're already working 60+ hours/week
- AEO consistently deprioritized
- 3+ months of "I'll do it next week"
- **Hire:** RM1,200-2,500/month buys consistency

**High Competition:**
- Competitors have 1,000+ citations
- They're investing in AEO aggressively
- You're losing market share
- **Hire:** Agencies have tools/experience to catch up faster

**B2B with Long Sales Cycles:**
- High-value deals (RM40,000+)
- Complex technical content required
- Multiple decision makers
- **Hire:** Professional content creation worth the investment

**Technical Limitations:**
- Schema markup feels overwhelming
- CMS issues/blocking
- Website speed problems
- **Hire:** Technical expertise included

---

### Stay DIY If...

**Single Location:**
- One city/region to target
- 50-100 core queries only
- Manageable workload

**Low Competition:**
- Few competitors in AI Overviews
- Local market not saturated
- DIY can compete effectively

**Tight Budget:**
- RM1,200/month agency fee is prohibitive
- RM200-400/month in tools is feasible
- Time investment is available

**Learning Interest:**
- You enjoy learning new skills
- Want to understand digital marketing
- Willing to invest 6-10 hours/month

**Niche Market:**
- Specialized services
- Limited queries to target
- Deep subject matter expertise (you're the expert)

## **FAQ: DIY AEO**

### **Is DIY AEO really effective?**

Yes. DIY AEO achieves 60-70% of agency results when done consistently. The gap is speed (agencies execute faster due to experience and tools), not capability. Dedicated business owners can reach 400-800 AI citations within 6-8 months DIY.

### **What's the hardest part of DIY AEO?**

Schema markup and content velocity. Learning structured data requires technical patience. Creating 4-8 pieces of content monthly requires discipline. Most DIY failures come from inconsistency, not inability.

### **Can I switch from DIY to agency later?**

Absolutely. Many businesses start DIY to learn, then hire agencies once they understand the value. Your DIY foundation (schema, content) transfers seamlessly. Agencies accelerate from there.

### **How long until DIY AEO shows results?**

3-4 months for first AI citations. 6-8 months for significant traction (400+ citations). 12+ months for full optimization. Agencies typically achieve in 3-4 months what takes DIY 6-8 months.

### **What's the minimum viable DIY AEO commitment?**

4 hours/month + RM200 in tools (RankMath Pro). Below this, you won't build citations fast enough to compete. 6-10 hours/month + RM400 in tools is recommended for meaningful results.
