## **Month 1: Foundation Phase**

### Week 1: Setup & Research (8 hours)

**Task 1: Install tools (2 hours)**
- Create ChatGPT Plus account (RM70/month)
- Sign up for Surfer SEO (RM200/month)
- Install Schema Pro plugin (RM150/year)
- Set up Google Search Console
- Set up Google Analytics 4

**Task 2: Keyword research (3 hours)**
- Use Google Keyword Planner (free)
- Search your main service + "how", "what", "cost"
- Example: "crane rental" â†’ "how much crane rental cost"
- Export 30 question-based keywords
- Filter by: 50-500 searches/month, low competition

**Task 3: Competitor analysis (3 hours)**
- Search your top 10 keywords
- Open top 3 ranking pages for each
- Note: What questions do they answer?
- Note: What's missing (gaps)?
- Note: Their content structure

**Deliverable:** Spreadsheet with 30 target keywords + competitor insights

### Week 2: Content Production (10 hours)

**Task 1: Create first AI-optimized post (4 hours)**
- Pick one keyword from Week 1 research
- Use ChatGPT to generate outline
- Write 1,200-word article with this structure:
  - Direct answer block (50-75 words at top)
  - Clear sections with H2/H3 headers
  - FAQ section (5 questions with direct answers)
  - Specific numbers and examples
  - Comparison table if relevant

**Task 2: Optimize with Surfer SEO (2 hours)**
- Paste content into Surfer
- Aim for score 80+
- Add suggested keywords
- Adjust word count if needed
- Fix readability issues

**Task 3: Add FAQ schema (2 hours)**
- Identify 5 FAQs from your content
- Add FAQ schema via Schema Pro
- Test with Google Rich Results Test
- Fix any errors

**Task 4: Publish and track (2 hours)**
- Publish on your website
- Submit to Google Search Console
- Add to tracking spreadsheet
- Note: Date published, target keyword, current ranking

**Deliverable:** 1 published, optimized blog post with schema

### Week 3: Content Production (10 hours)

**Repeat Week 2 process with different keyword**

**Focus:** Answer a different question angle

**Example for crane rental:**
- Week 2: "How much does crane rental cost?"
- Week 3: "What is the minimum rental period?"

**Deliverable:** 2nd published post with schema

### Week 4: Review & Adjust (10 hours)

**Task 1: Month 1 review (2 hours)**
- Check Google Search Console
- Any pages indexed?
- Any impressions for target keywords?
- Any clicks?

**Task 2: Competitor check (2 hours)**
- Search your target keywords
- Where do you rank? (probably page 5-10)
- That's normal for Month 1
- Note: Who's ranking? What are they doing?

**Task 3: Create 2 more posts (6 hours)**
- Follow Week 2 process
- Target 2 new keywords

**Month 1 total:** 4 blog posts published with FAQ schema

**Expected results:** Zero. That's normal.

## **Month 2: Optimization Phase**

### Week 5-8: Scale What Works

**Weekly routine (10 hours each week):**

**Task 1: Create 1 new post (6 hours)**
- Follow Month 1 process
- Target new keyword
- Optimize with Surfer (80+ score)
- Add FAQ schema

**Task 2: Update 1 old post (2 hours)**
- Add internal links to new post
- Update any outdated information
- Add more examples/data
- Improve meta description

**Task 3: Track progress (2 hours)**
- Check Google Search Console
- Note: Which keywords getting impressions?
- Note: Which pages getting clicks?
- Adjust strategy: double down on what's working

**Month 2 total:** 4 more blog posts (8 total)

**Expected results:** First impressions in Search Console, maybe first clicks

### Week 8: Add More Schema Types

**Task 1: Organization schema (2 hours)**
- Add via Schema Pro
- Include: Business name, logo, URL, social links
- Test with Rich Results Test

**Task 2: Article schema (2 hours)**
- Add to your 8 blog posts
- Include: Author, publish date, headline
- Test each page

**Deliverable:** All pages have multiple schema types

**Why this matters:**
AI engines use schema to understand content. More schema = better understanding = higher citation chances.

**Month 3 total:** 4 more blog posts (12 total)

## **What to Track (3 Metrics Only)**

Don't track everything. Track what matters:

### Metric 1: AI Overview Citations
**Definition:** How often AI engines cite your content

**How to track:**
- Manually search target keywords weekly
- Note: "Cited in AI Overview: YES/NO"
- Track in spreadsheet

**Goal:** 5 citations by Month 3

### Metric 2: Keyword Rankings
**Definition:** Position in Google search results

**How to track:**
- Use Search Console (free)
- Check "Average position" for target keywords
- Note: Position 1-10 (page 1), 11-20 (page 2), etc.

**Goal:** 3 keywords in top 20 by Month 3

### Metric 3: Organic Leads
**Definition:** Contact form submissions, calls, emails from search traffic

**How to track:**
- Ask every new lead: "How did you find us?"
- Note: "Google search" responses
- Compare to Month 1 baseline

**Goal:** 5-10 organic leads/month by Month 3
