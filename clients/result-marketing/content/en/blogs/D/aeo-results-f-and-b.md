## **How AEO Works for Restaurants**

### **Step 1: AI Citations Drive Direct Reservations**

When someone searches "best halal restaurant in KL," Google's AI Overview cites 2-3 trusted options. Those restaurants get the direct calls, WhatsApp messages, and walk-ins.

**No commission. No platform fees. Direct customers.**

### **Step 2: Schema Markup = AI Understanding**

AI engines need structured data to understand your restaurant:
- **Restaurant schema:** Cuisine type, price range, menu items
- **LocalBusiness schema:** Location, hours, contact info
- **Review schema:** Food ratings (critical for hungry customers)
- **Menu schema:** Dishes, prices, dietary options

### **Step 3: Content That Answers Food Queries**

Create content that answers specific dining questions:
- "Best halal Italian restaurants in Bangsar for families"
- "Chinese restaurants in PJ with private rooms for events"
- "Cafes in KL with WiFi suitable for working"

AI scrapes this content. When customers ask food questions, your restaurant gets recommended.
