**What we built:**

**Automated review workflow:**

**Step 1: Guest check-out detection**
- System detects when guest checks out
- Triggers review request sequence

**Step 2: Timing optimization**
- Wait 24 hours (guest settled back home)

- Higher response rate (guest not in rush)

**Step 3: Smart routing**
- 5-star reviews: Direct guest to Airbnb, Booking.com

- Addresses issues privately

**Step 4: Automated follow-up**
