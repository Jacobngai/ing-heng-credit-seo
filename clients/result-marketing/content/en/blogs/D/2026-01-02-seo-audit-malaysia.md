## Technical SEO Checklist

### 1. Site Speed

**Check your speed:**
- Go to PageSpeed Insights (pagespeed.web.dev)
- Enter your website URL
- Check both Mobile and Desktop scores

**Passing score:**
- Mobile: 70+ (Good), 85+ (Excellent)
- Desktop: 80+ (Good), 90+ (Excellent)

**Failing?**
- Optimize images (compress, WebP format)
- Minify CSS/JS
- Enable caching
- Use CDN

### 2. Mobile-Friendliness

**Check:**
- Google Search Console â†’ Mobile Usability
- Or test on your phone

**Pass if:**
- Text readable without zooming
- Buttons tap-friendly (not too small)
- Content fits screen width (no horizontal scroll)
- No Flash or pop-ups blocking content

### 3. Indexing

**Check:**
- Google Search Console â†’ Coverage
- Look for "Errors"

**Should see:**
- Valid: Most pages
- Errors: 0 pages

**If errors exist:**
- Fix critical errors (server errors, not found)
- Submit for re-indexing

### 4. Sitemap

**Check:**
- `yourdomain.com/sitemap.xml`

**Should exist:**
- XML sitemap with all pages
- Submitted to Google Search Console

### 5. Robots.txt

**Check:**
- `yourdomain.com/robots.txt`

**Should NOT block:**
- Important pages
- CSS files
- JS files

## On-Page SEO Checklist

### 1. Title Tags

**Check:**
- Right-click â†’ View Page Source
- Search for `<title>`

**Should be:**
- 50-60 characters
- Include target keyword
- Unique for each page
- Compelling (makes people want to click)

**Example:**
`Crane Rental in Cheras, Kuala Lumpur | RM500/day | [Company Name]`

### 2. Meta Descriptions

**Check:**
- View page source
- Search for `name="description"`

**Should be:**
- 150-160 characters
- Include keyword
- Compelling call-to-action
- Unique per page

**Example:**
`Need crane rental in Cheras? 20-50 ton mobile cranes available. Same-day delivery. RM500-1500/day. Call +60123456789 for free quote.`

### 3. Header Tags

**Check:**
- View page
- Look at headings (H1, H2, H3)

**Should be:**
- One H1 per page (includes main keyword)
- H2s for main sections
- H3s for subsections
- Logical hierarchy

### 4. Content Quality

**Check:**
- Word count (300+ minimum, 1000+ ideal)
- Keyword in first 100 words
- Content covers topic thoroughly
- Not keyword stuffing (natural flow)

**Pass if:**
- Comprehensive coverage
- Answers user questions
- Easy to read (short paragraphs, bullet points)
- Includes images

### 5. URL Structure

**Check:**
- Page URLs

**Should be:**
- Short and descriptive
- Include keyword
- Hyphens between words
- Lowercase

**Good:**
`domain.com/crane-rental-cheras`

**Bad:**
`domain.com/page?id=12345`

## Off-Page SEO Checklist

### 1. Backlinks

**Check:**
- Use free tools (Ahrefs free backlink checker)
- Enter your domain

**Look for:**
- Total backlinks (more = better, generally)
- Referring domains (unique sites linking to you)
- Quality of links (edu, my, gov = good)

**Red flags:**
- Spammy sites linking
- Sudden link drops
- Unnatural anchor text (100% exact match keywords)

### 2. Domain Authority

**Check:**
- Moz Domain Authority checker (free)
- Enter your domain

**Score:**
- 0-100 scale
- 20-40: New site
- 40-60: Growing
- 60+: Strong authority

### 3. Social Signals

**Check:**
- Search your business on Google
- Look at social media results

**Should have:**
- Facebook Business Page
- Google Business Profile
- Industry-relevant platforms (LinkedIn for B2B)

## Local SEO Checklist

### 1. Google Business Profile

**Check:**
- Search your business on Google Maps
- Click on your listing

**Should have:**
- Business name accurate
- Address correct
- Phone number prominent
- Website link
- Business hours
- 10+ photos
- 10+ reviews (4.0+ rating)
- Posts in last month

### 2. NAP Consistency

**Check:**
- Search your business name
- Check all directories

**Should match exactly:**
- Name (same on all sites)
- Address (same format)
- Phone (same number)

**Inconsistent NAP = lower rankings.**

### 3. Reviews

**Check:**
- Google Business Profile
- Facebook
- Industry directories

**Should have:**
- 20+ reviews (minimum for competitive industries)
- 4.0+ average rating
- Recent reviews (within last month)
- Responses to reviews (80%+ response rate)
