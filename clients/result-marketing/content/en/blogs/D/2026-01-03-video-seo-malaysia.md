## Why Video Matters for SEO

**Direct SEO benefits:**

**1. Increased Dwell Time**
- Users stay longer on pages with video
- Google uses dwell time as ranking factor
- Longer dwell = better rankings

**2. Video in Search Results**
- Google shows video carousels for many searches
- Video stands out (eye-catching thumbnail)
- Higher click-through rate

**3. Backlinks**
- People link to/share videos
- Video embeds on other sites = backlinks
- YouTube links count as backlinks

**4. YouTube SEO**
- YouTube = search engine
- Rank on YouTube = traffic
- YouTube appears in Google results

**Indirect benefits:**

**5. Higher Conversion**
- Video explains products/services better
- Builds trust (see real people, products)
- Increases leads/sales

**6. Social Shares**
- Video shared more than text
- Social signals = SEO benefit
- Viral potential

## YouTube SEO Optimization

### 1. Keyword Research

**Find video keywords:**
- YouTube search suggestions
- Google "video" search
- Answer The Public (questions)

**Example:**
- Search: "how to rent a crane"
- YouTube suggests:
  - "how to rent a crane for construction"
  - "crane rental cost"
  - "mobile crane rental process"

**Target these video keywords.**

### 2. Optimize Video Title

**Formula:** Keyword + Hook

**Examples:**
- "Crane Rental in KL | Complete Guide (2026)"
- "How Much Does Excavator Rental Cost? [Price Guide]"
- "Emergency Plumber Kuala Lumpur | 24/7 Service"

**Best practices:**
- Include target keyword
- 60 characters max (before truncation)
- Compelling (makes people want to click)
- Year (shows freshness)

### 3. Optimize Description

**First 2 lines (most important):**
- Include keyword naturally
- Explain what video covers
- Include CTA (call to action)

**Example:**
```
Need crane rental in Kuala Lumpur? This complete guide covers types,
pricing, and how to choose the right crane for your project.

ðŸ“ž Call +60123456789 for free quote

0:00 - Types of cranes
1:30 - Rental pricing
3:00 - How to choose
5:00 - Contact us

#crane #rental #construction
```

**Best practices:**
- 200-500 words (longer descriptions rank better)
- Include keyword 2-3 times naturally
- Add timestamps (for long videos)
- Include contact info
- Add hashtags (3-5 relevant)

### 4. Tags

**What are tags?**
- Keywords that help YouTube understand your video

**Strategy:**
- 5-10 tags
- Mix of broad and specific

**Example:**
```
crane rental, mobile crane, excavator rental,
construction equipment, kuala lumpur, crane services
```

### 5. Thumbnail

**Most important factor for click-through rate.**

**Best practices:**
- 1280x720 resolution
- High contrast (stands out)
- Text overlay (compelling title)
- Human face (builds trust)
- Professional quality

**Avoid:**
- Low resolution
- Cluttered (too much text)
- Misleading (doesn't match video)

## Video on Your Website

### Where to Place Video

**1. Homepage**
- Above the fold (visible without scrolling)
- Short (60-90 seconds)
- Overview video: who you are, what you do

**2. Service Pages**
- Explain specific services
- Show products in action
- Build trust

**Example:**
- Crane rental page: Video showing crane in use
- Excavator rental page: Video of excavator operating

**3. About Page**
- Team introduction
- Facility tour
- Company story

**4. Testimonials/Case Studies**
- Client video testimonials
- Project case studies
- Before/after videos

### Technical Optimization

**Hosting options:**

**YouTube (recommended):**
- Free hosting
- Built-in SEO
- Unlimited bandwidth
- BUT: Shows related videos (including competitors)

**Vimeo:**
- No ads
- No competitor videos
- Professional appearance
- BUT: Paid (after free tier)

**Wistia/Hosted:**
- Full control
- Analytics
- BUT: Expensive, no YouTube SEO benefit

**Best strategy:**
- Upload to YouTube (SEO benefit)
- Embed on website (conversions)
- Use YouTube embed with "hide related videos" parameter

## Video Types That Work

### 1. How-To Videos

**Format:** Step-by-step instructions

**Example:** "How to Choose the Right Crane for Your Project"

**Length:** 5-10 minutes

**Why works:**
- Educational value
- Builds authority
- Generates leads (CTA at end)

### 2. Product/Service Demos

**Format:** Show product/service in action

**Example:** "20-Ton Mobile Crane Demo - Kuala Lumpur"

**Length:** 2-5 minutes

**Why works:**
- Shows capabilities
- Builds trust
- Helps buyers visualize

### 3. Testimonials

**Format:** Client interviews

**Example:** "Client Story: How [Company] Saved RM50,000 with Rental"

**Length:** 2-3 minutes

**Why works:**
- Social proof
- Builds credibility
- Emotional connection

### 4. Facility/Tour Videos

**Format:** Behind-the-scenes

**Example:** "Our Crane Fleet Tour - [Company Name]"

**Length:** 3-5 minutes

**Why works:**
- Shows professionalism
- Builds trust
- Demonstrates scale

### 5. FAQ Videos

**Format:** Answer common questions

**Example:** "Crane Rental FAQ - Top 10 Questions Answered"

**Length:** 5-10 minutes

**Why works:**
- Addresses objections
- Saves time (answers questions upfront)
- SEO value (question keywords)
