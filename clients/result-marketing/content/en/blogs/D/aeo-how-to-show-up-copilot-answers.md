## What Is AEO?

**AEO = Answer Engine Optimization**

AEO is the practice of optimizing your content to appear as cited sources in AI-generated search answers (Google AI Overviews, Microsoft Copilot, ChatGPT, etc.).

**The Goal:**
- Get cited in AI answers
- Build brand authority
- Drive zero-click impressions
- Capture follow-through clicks

**The Difference:**
- SEO: Optimize for organic rankings (#1-10 positions)
- AEO: Optimize for AI answer citations (3-5 sources)

---

## AEO vs SEO: What's the Difference?

**SEO (Search Engine Optimization):**
- Goal: Rank #1-10 in organic results
- Metrics: Rankings, traffic, clicks
- Content: Keywords, backlinks, on-page SEO
- Timeline: 3-12 months for results

**AEO (Answer Engine Optimization):**
- Goal: Get cited in AI answers
- Metrics: Citations, impressions, brand visibility
- Content: Q&A, schema, E-E-A-T
- Timeline: 1-3 months for citations

**Why You Need Both:**
- AI cites top-ranking pages (80% from top 3)
- You need SEO rankings to get AEO citations
- Combined: 3.2x more visibility than SEO alone

## How to Get Started with AEO

### Step 1: Target Answer-Intent Keywords
**Good Targets:**
- "How much does [service] cost?"
- "Best [service] in [location]"
- "How to choose [service] provider"

**Bad Targets:**
- Brand name (already ranking)
- "Buy [product]" (transactional)

### Step 2: Create Direct-Answer Content
**Format:**
```markdown
## Question Heading?

**Direct Answer:** Clear, specific response.

**Supporting Details:**
- Point 1
- Point 2
- Point 3
```

### Step 3: Add Schema Markup
**Required Schema:**
- Article schema (blog posts)
- FAQPage schema (Q&A)
- HowTo schema (guides)
- Organization schema (brand)

### Step 4: Build E-E-A-T
- Experience: Case studies, examples
- Expertise: Author bios, credentials
- Authoritativeness: Backlinks, mentions
- Trust: Reviews, testimonials

## Schema Markup for AEO

Schema is the language AI engines understand.

**Priority Schema Types:**
1. **Article Schema** - 40% more citations
2. **FAQPage Schema** - 65% more citations for questions
3. **HowTo Schema** - 52% more for guides
4. **Review Schema** - 38% more brand citations
5. **Organization Schema** - 25% more local citations

**Implementation:**
- Use JSON-LD format
- Include all required fields
- Validate with Google Rich Results Test
- Update regularly
