**80% of pest control searches happen on mobile**. Someone seeing cockroaches in their kitchen isn't opening a laptop.

**Your mobile site must have:**
1. **Click-to-call button** prominent above the fold
2. **WhatsApp button** (preferred by 65% of Malaysians)
3. **Pests treated** clearly listed (termites, cockroaches, rats, etc.)
4. **Service areas** (suburbs you cover)
5. **Emergency service badge** (if available)
6. **Pricing ranges** (builds trust)

### Trust Signals for Pest Control

Homeowners and businesses are **hiring you to spray chemicals in their property**. Trust is essential.

**Essential Trust Elements:**
- **License/certification** (Pest Control Association, etc.)
- **Safe chemicals used** (highlight eco-friendly options)

- **Warranty on treatments** (e.g., "30-day cockroach guarantee")
