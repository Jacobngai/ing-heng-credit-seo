## **How AEO Works for Retail**

### **Step 1: AI Citations Drive Foot Traffic**

When someone searches "baby shop near me," Google's AI Overview cites 2-3 trusted local businesses. Those businesses get the phone calls, the WhatsApp messages, the foot traffic.

**AEO makes your retail business one of those cited sources.**

### **Step 2: Schema Markup = AI Understanding**

AI engines need structured data to understand your retail business:
- **LocalBusiness schema:** Your location, hours, products
- **Product schema:** Your inventory, prices, availability
- **Review schema:** Customer ratings (critical for trust)
- **FAQ schema:** Common questions customers ask

### **Step 3: Content That Answers Questions**

Create content that answers specific retail queries:
- "Best baby shop for newborn essentials in KL"
- "Hardware store with weekend hours in Petaling Jaya"
- "Affordable fashion boutiques in Bangsar"

AI scrapes this content. When customers ask questions, your retail business gets recommended.

## **FAQ**

### **How fast can retail stores see AEO results?**

Most retail businesses see initial traffic increases within 60-90 days. Significant AI Overview citations typically appear by month 3-4. The timeline depends on your competition level, location authority, and content quality. High-demand retail categories like baby products and hardware often see faster results than low-competition niches.

### **Does AEO work for online-only retail stores?**

Yes, but with a different focus. Online retailers should prioritize Product schema, shipping/return FAQ schema, and category guides rather than local "near me" optimization. E-commerce AEO focuses on product-specific queries like "best baby stroller for newborns" rather than location-based searches.
