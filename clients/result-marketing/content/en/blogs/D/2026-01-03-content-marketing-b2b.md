## Content Types That Work

### 1. How-To Guides

**Format:** Step-by-step instructions

**Example:** "How to Choose the Right Crane for Your Construction Project"

**Why works:**
- Answers specific question
- Demonstrates expertise
- Builds trust

**Length:** 1,500-2,500 words

### 2. Problem-Solution Articles

**Format:** Identify problem, explain solutions

**Example:** "5 Common Logistics Problems (and How to Solve Them)"

**Why works:**
- Addresses pain points
- Positions you as solution provider
- Generates leads (CTA: "Need help with X? Contact us")

**Length:** 1,000-1,500 words

### 3. Comparison Guides

**Format:** Compare options (equipment, methods, services)

**Example:** "Excavator Rental vs. Purchase: Cost Comparison 2026"

**Why works:**
- Buyers in evaluation stage
- Helps decision-making
- Positions you as advisor

**Length:** 1,200-2,000 words

**Why works:**

### 5. Industry Insights

**Format:** Trends, predictions, analysis

**Example:** "Malaysia Construction Industry Outlook 2026"

**Why works:**
- Demonstrates industry knowledge
- Attracts backlinks (others cite your data)

## Writing SEO-Friendly Content

### 1. Keyword Research First

**Find keywords:**
- 100-1,000 searches/month (sweet spot)
- Low-medium difficulty
- Clear intent (informational or commercial)

**Tools:**
- Google Keyword Planner (free)
- Answer The Public (questions people ask)
- Google "People Also Ask"

**Example:**
- Keyword: "excavator rental vs purchase"
- Search volume: 300/month
- Difficulty: Low
- Intent: Commercial investigation

### 2. Structure for Scannability

**B2B readers are busy. Make content scannable:**

**Use:**
- Short paragraphs (2-3 sentences)
- Bullet points
- Numbered lists
- Bold key information
- Subheadings every 200-300 words
- Tables/charts for data

**Example structure:**
```markdown
## Introduction
(What this article covers)

## What is Excavator Rental?
(Explanation)

## Benefits of Renting
* Benefit 1
* Benefit 2
* Benefit 3

## When to Buy vs. Rent
(Table comparing scenarios)

## Cost Comparison
(ROI calculation)

## Conclusion
(Recap + CTA)
```

### 3. Include Data and Examples

**B2B buyers want proof.**

**Include:**
- Statistics (with sources)
- Real examples
- Case studies
- Calculations (ROI)

**Example:**
- "According to Master Builders Association Malaysia..."
- "Client X saved RM50,000 by renting instead of buying..."
- "ROI calculation: Rental RM10,000/month vs. Purchase RM500,000..."

### 4. Add Visuals

**Break up text with:**
- Charts/graphs (data)
- Infographics (processes)
- Screenshots (examples)
- Photos (real projects)

**Optimize images:**
- Descriptive file names
- Alt text with keywords
- Compress for speed

### 5. Optimize Meta Elements

**Title tag:** 50-60 characters, include keyword
**Example:** "Excavator Rental vs Purchase | Cost Comparison 2026"

**Meta description:** 150-160 characters, compelling CTA
**Example:** "Renting or buying excavator? Compare costs, pros, and cons. Make the right decision for your business. Free consultation: +60123456789"

**Send to:**

- Prospects (your email list)

**Subject line:** Short, compelling
