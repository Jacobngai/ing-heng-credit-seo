## What is AEO ROI?

**AEO ROI (Answer Engine Optimization Return on Investment)** measures how much revenue you generate from appearing in AI answers (Google AI Overviews, ChatGPT, Copilot, Perplexity) compared to your investment.

**Simple definition:**
- **Investment:** RM1,200/month (typical AEO retainer)
- **Revenue:** Leads/calls from people who saw you in AI answers
- **ROI:** Percentage return on that RM1,200

**The difference from traditional SEO:**
- Traditional SEO tracks clicks (people visiting your site)
- AEO tracks **impressions + conversions** (people seeing you in AI answers without clicking)

**Why this matters in 2026:**
Google AI Overviews now appear in 45% of search results. Zero-click searches don't mean zero revenue. You're getting brand visibility and leads that never touch your website.

## **FAQ: AEO ROI Calculation**

### **How long does it take to see positive AEO ROI?**

Most businesses see positive ROI by Month 3-4. The first 2 months are investment-heavy (schema setup, content creation, earning citations). By Month 6, typical ROI is 300-600%. The key is tracking leads from AI answers accurately.

### **What's a good AEO ROI benchmark?**

**Healthy benchmarks by industry:**
- Emergency services (locksmith, plumber): 200-400% ROI by Month 3
- High-ticket services (construction, equipment rental): 400-800% ROI by Month 6
- Local service businesses (aircond, cleaning): 150-300% ROI by Month 4

Anything below 100% ROI by Month 6 means your AEO strategy needs adjustment (wrong keywords, poor conversion rate, weak schema).

### **Do I need expensive tools to calculate AEO ROI?**

No. You need:
1. Google Search Console (free) - track AI Overview impressions
2. A simple lead tracking spreadsheet (Excel/Google Sheets)

Most expensive ROI tools overcomplicate it. Focus on revenue, not vanity metrics.

### **What if my AEO ROI is negative after 6 months?**

**Common causes:**
1. **Wrong keywords:** Targeting low-intent searches
2. **Poor conversion:** Your business isn't closing leads well
3. **Weak schema:** Your content isn't optimized for AI answers
4. **Low search volume:** Your niche doesn't have enough AI search traffic

**Solution:** Audit your AEO strategy or switch to Google Ads (higher cost, faster results).

### **How does AEO ROI compare to Facebook Ads ROI?**

**Typical comparison (Malaysian SME data):**
- **Facebook Ads:** 120-180% ROI (but costs increase every year)
- **Google Ads:** 150-250% ROI (high competition in 2026)
- **AEO:** 300-800% ROI (compounds over 12+ months)

The key difference: AEO is an **asset** (you own the rankings), ads are **rent** (you disappear when you stop paying).
