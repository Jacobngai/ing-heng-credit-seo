## **Technical SEO Foundation**

### 1. HTTPS Enabled (1 point)

**Check:** Does your URL start with `https://`?

**Why it matters:**
AI engines won't cite non-secure sites. HTTPS is mandatory for trust and security.

**How to fix:**
- Purchase SSL certificate (RM50-200/year)
- Install via hosting provider
- Force HTTPS redirect

**Test:** Visit your site. If browser shows "Not Secure," fix immediately.

### 2. Mobile-Responsive Design (1 point)

**Check:** Does your site work well on mobile phones?

**Test:**
- Open your site on mobile phone
- Check if text is readable without zooming
- Verify buttons are tap-friendly
- Ensure no horizontal scrolling

**Why it matters:**
70% of searches happen on mobile. AI engines prioritize mobile-friendly sites.

**How to fix:**
- Use responsive design (CSS media queries)
- Test on multiple devices (iPhone, Android, tablet)
- Use Google's Mobile-Friendly Test

### 3. Page Speed < 3 Seconds (1 point)

**Check:** How fast does your site load?

**Test:** Google PageSpeed Insights (pagespeed.web.dev)

**Target scores:**
- Mobile: 70+ score
- Desktop: 80+ score
- Load time: < 3 seconds

**Why it matters:**
AI engines deprioritize slow sites. Users abandon slow-loading pages.

**How to fix:**
- Compress images (WebP format)
- Minify CSS, JavaScript
- Use CDN (Content Delivery Network)
- Upgrade hosting if needed

### 4. Clean URL Structure (1 point)

**Check:** Are your URLs readable?

**Bad:**
```
example.com/page?id=123&cat=seo
```

**Good:**
```
example.com/seo-services-malaysia
```

**Why it matters:**
Clean URLs help AI engines understand page content. Users trust readable URLs.

**How to fix:**
- Use descriptive URLs with keywords
- Separate words with hyphens (-)
- Avoid special characters (?, =, &)
- Keep URLs under 60 characters

### 5. XML Sitemap (1 point)

**Check:** Do you have an XML sitemap submitted?

**Test:** `example.com/sitemap.xml`

**Why it matters:**
Sitemaps help AI engines discover and index all your pages.

**How to fix:**
- Generate sitemap (Yoast SEO, Screaming Frog)
- Submit to Google Search Console
- Submit to Bing Webmaster Tools
- Update when adding new pages

## **Content Requirements**

### 6. Direct Answers to Questions (1 point)

**Check:** Do you answer common questions directly?

**Test:** Search your brand + "how much does [service] cost"

**Bad:**
```
Welcome to our pricing page. We offer various packages...
```

**Good:**
```
SEO costs RM1,200-3,000 per month in Malaysia (2026).
Pricing depends on keyword difficulty, competition, and scope.
```

**Why it matters:**
AI engines extract direct answers for user questions.

**How to fix:**
- Identify top 10 customer questions
- Write direct answers (40-60 words each)
- Place answers prominently on page
- Add FAQ schema markup

### 7. Comprehensive Content (1 point)

**Check:** Do you cover topics thoroughly?

**Test:** Review your main service pages

**Minimum requirements:**
- 800-1,500 words per page
- Covers what, why, how, pricing, process
- Includes examples and data
- Has H2/H3 subheadings
- Uses bullet points for readability

**Why it matters:**
AI engines cite comprehensive, authoritative sources.

**How to fix:**
- Expand thin pages (add sections, examples)
- Add case studies and testimonials
- Include relevant statistics
- Update with 2026 data

### 8. Regular Content Updates (1 point)

**Check:** When was your last blog post?

**Ideal:** Publish 2-4 posts per month

**Why it matters:**
Fresh content signals active, authoritative site to AI engines.

**How to fix:**
- Create content calendar
- Write about industry topics
- Answer customer questions
- Update old posts with new data

### 9. Multimedia Content (1 point)

**Check:** Does your content include images, videos?

**Minimum:**
- 1 image per 500 words
- 1 video per major topic
- Alt text for all images
- Optimized file sizes

**Why it matters:**
Multimedia signals quality. AI engines value diverse content types.

**How to fix:**
- Add relevant images (stock or custom)
- Create explainer videos
- Compress images (WebP format)
- Add descriptive alt text

### 10. Internal Linking (1 point)

**Check:** Do you link between related pages?

**Test:** Navigate your site. Can you reach important pages in 2-3 clicks?

**Why it matters:**
Internal links help AI engines understand site structure and relationships.

**How to fix:**
- Link from blog posts to service pages
- Link from service pages to related services
- Use descriptive anchor text
- Fix broken links

## **Schema Markup**

### 11. FAQPage Schema (1 point)

**Check:** Do you have FAQ schema markup?

**Test:** Use Google Rich Results Test (search.google.com/test/rich-results)

**Example code:**
```json
{
  "@type": "FAQPage",
  "mainEntity": [{
    "@type": "Question",
    "name": "How much does SEO cost?",
    "acceptedAnswer": {
      "@type": "Answer",
      "text": "RM1,200-3,000 per month in Malaysia."
    }
  }]
}
```

**Why it matters:**
Critical for AI citations. FAQ schema increases citations by 300%.

**How to fix:**
- Create FAQ page
- Add JSON-LD schema
- Validate with testing tools

### 12. Article Schema (1 point)

**Check:** Do blog posts have Article schema?

**Test:** Use Rich Results Test

**Why it matters:**
Helps AI engines recognize your content as authoritative.

**How to fix:**
- Add Article schema to all blog posts
- Include author, publisher, datePublished
- Validate implementation

### 13. LocalBusiness Schema (1 point)

**Check:** Do you have LocalBusiness schema? (Local businesses only)

**Test:** Use Rich Results Test

**Why it matters:**
Critical for "near me" searches and local AI recommendations.

**How to fix:**
- Add LocalBusiness schema
- Include name, address, phone, hours
- Add geo coordinates
- Validate implementation

### 14. Organization Schema (1 point)

**Check:** Does your home page have Organization schema?

**Why it matters:**
Helps AI engines recognize your brand as an entity.

**How to fix:**
- Add Organization schema
- Include logo, description, social links
- Place on home or about page

### 15. Review Schema (1 point)

**Check:** Do you display review ratings with schema?

**Why it matters:**
Social proof signals trust to AI engines.

**How to fix:**
- Aggregate reviews (50+ minimum)
- Add AggregateRating schema
- Display star ratings on site
- Keep data accurate

## **Authority and Trust**

### 16. 50+ Customer Reviews (1 point)

**Check:** How many reviews do you have?

**Target:**
- 50+ reviews minimum
- 4.5+ average rating
- Multiple platforms (Google, Facebook)

**Why it matters:**
Reviews signal trust. AI engines prioritize highly-rated businesses.

**How to fix:**
- Ask every satisfied client for review
- Make review process easy (QR code, direct link)
- Respond to all reviews
- Offer incentives (discount for review)

### 17. Google Business Profile (1 point)

**Check:** Is your Google Business Profile claimed and complete?

**Required:**
- Business name, address, phone
- Business hours
- Service description
- Photos (10+ minimum)
- Posts (weekly updates)

**Why it matters:**
Critical for local AI search ("near me" queries).

**How to fix:**
- Claim profile at business.google.com
- Verify ownership (postcard or video)
- Complete all fields
- Post weekly updates

### 18. Backlinks from Authority Sites (1 point)

**Check:** How many backlinks do you have from authority sites?

**Target:**
- 20+ backlinks minimum
- From sites with DA 40+
- Relevant to your industry

**Why it matters:**
Backlinks signal authority. AI engines cite trusted sources.

**How to fix:**
- Create linkable content (research, guides)
- Guest post on industry sites
- Get featured in news/media
- Use PR for coverage

### 19. Social Media Presence (1 point)

**Check:** Are you active on social media?

**Minimum:**
- Facebook business page
- LinkedIn company page
- Weekly posts
- 100+ followers each

**Why it matters:**
Social signals factor into AI authority assessment.

**How to fix:**
- Create business pages
- Post weekly updates
- Engage with followers
- Cross-link to website

### 20. Brand Mentions (1 point)

**Check:** Are people talking about your brand online?

**Test:** Google "your brand name" and search news

**Target:**
- 10+ brand mentions per month
- In blogs, news, forums
- Positive or neutral sentiment

**Why it matters:**
Brand mentions signal relevance and authority.

**How to fix:**
- Publish press releases
- Get interviewed
- Sponsor events
- Create shareable content
