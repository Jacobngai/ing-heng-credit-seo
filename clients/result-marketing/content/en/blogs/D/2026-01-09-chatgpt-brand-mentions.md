## **How ChatGPT Mentions Brands**

### The Mention Process

**When users ask:** "Recommend [service] in [location]"

## **Strategy 2: Collect Reviews**

### Reviews Are Critical

**ChatGPT prioritizes businesses with:**
- 50+ reviews minimum
- 4.5+ rating
- Recent reviews (last 3 months)
- Detailed reviews (not just "good service")

**Review strategy:**
1. **Ask every satisfied customer**
   - Right after service completion
   - Make it easy (QR code, direct link)
   - Offer incentive (discount on next service)

2. **Respond to every review**
   - Thank positive reviewers
   - Address negative reviews professionally
   - Shows active engagement

3. **Diversify review platforms**
   - Google Reviews (primary)
   - Facebook Reviews
   - Trustpilot
   - Industry-specific platforms

**Target:** 100+ reviews across platforms, 4.8+ average rating.

**Why:** Media coverage signals authority to ChatGPT.

4. Note position (1st mentioned, 2nd, etc.)
