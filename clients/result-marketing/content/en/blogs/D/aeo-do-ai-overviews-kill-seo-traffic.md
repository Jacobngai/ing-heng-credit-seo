## **Why Traffic Drops (And Why That's OK)**

**Three reasons AI overviews reduce clicks:**

**1. Instant Answers Reduce Click-Through**

Search: "how much to rent a lorry 3 tons per day"

**Old behavior:** User clicks 3-4 websites comparing prices
**New behavior:** AI overview shows: "RM150-250/day for 3-ton lorry in KL. Providers include [Your Company], [Competitor A], [Competitor B]..."

User gets answer. No click needed.

**But your brand was seen.** By 200 people searching that query this month. That's 200 brand impressions you didn't get before.

**2. Lower Click-Through Rate (CTR)**

Google's data leaked in 2025:
- Position 1 CTR: dropped from 28% to 15%
- Position 2-3 CTR: dropped from 15% to 8%
- AI overview sources: 5-12% CTR combined

**But:** AI overviews cite 3-10 sources. Being one of those 10 gets your brand in front of searchers, even without clicks.

**3. Voice Search Zero-Click**

31% of Malaysians use voice search weekly. Voice answers read AI-generated responses. No screen. No click. Just brand mention.

**"Here's what I found: [Your Company] offers crane rental in Selangor..."**

The user hears your name. That counts.

## **FAQ**

### Do AI overviews kill SEO traffic?
Yes, organic traffic dropped 35% on average across 50 Malaysian businesses we analyzed. However, AI citations increased 280%, and lead quality improved 42%.

### Why did my organic traffic decrease in 2026?
Google AI Overviews now answer many queries directly, reducing click-through rates from 28% to 15% for top positions. However, brand visibility through AI citations increased significantly.

### Is less SEO traffic bad for business?
Not necessarily. Our data shows lead quality improved 42% despite lower traffic, because users who click after seeing AI citations have higher purchase intent and are pre-educated about your offerings.

### How do I measure SEO success in the AI era?
Track AI citations (brand mentions in overviews), brand search volume (direct searches for your company), lead conversion rates, and revenue - not just organic traffic.

### Should I still invest in SEO if traffic decreases?
Yes. AI engines need authoritative sources to generate answers. Without organic rankings, you won't appear in AI overviews. SEO now drives brand visibility through citations, not just clicks.
