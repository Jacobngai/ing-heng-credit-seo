## **Converting Plumbing Leads**

### Mobile Optimization for Emergency Calls

**78% of plumbing searches happen on mobile**. Someone with a burst pipe isn't opening a laptop.

**Your mobile site must have:**
1. **Click-to-call button** above the fold (large, easy to tap)
2. **Emergency service badge** ("24/7 Emergency Plumbing" if available)
3. **Service areas** clearly listed (suburbs you serve)
4. **Response time guarantee** ("Within 2 hours")
5. **Emergency pricing** (builds trust, reduces abandonment)

### WhatsApp Integration for Plumbers

Malaysians love **WhatsApp for service inquiries**. Many won't call but will message.

**WhatsApp capture benefits:**
- Send photos of the plumbing problem
- Get quick price estimates
- Schedule appointments without phone tag
- Track technician arrival time
- **30% higher conversion rate** than phone-only

### Trust Signals That Convert

Homeowners inviting plumbers into their homes need **trust upfront**. Display these prominently:

**Essential Trust Elements:**
- **License/certification** (Lembaga Jurutera, etc.)
- **Insurance coverage** (protects their property)
- **Uniformed technicians** - show team photos
- **Transparent pricing** - emergency fee + hourly rate
- **Warranty on work** - "90-day workmanship guarantee"
- **Real reviews** with customer names and photos
- **Before/after photos** of your work
