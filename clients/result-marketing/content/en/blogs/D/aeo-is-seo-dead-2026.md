   - FAQ sections (questions AI searches for)

## **FAQ**

### Is SEO dead in 2026?
No. SEO evolved into AI Overview Optimization (AEO). You still need organic rankings to appear in AI-generated answers. The metric shifted from clicks to citations.

### Why did my organic traffic drop in 2026?
Google AI Overviews now answer many queries directly, reducing click-through rates. However, if your business appears in those AI answers, you're gaining brand visibility even without clicks.

### How do I show up in AI overviews?
Rank in the top 10 organically, structure content with FAQ sections and comparison tables, add schema markup, and build authority through reviews and backlinks.

### Should I stop investing in SEO?
No. AI engines need authoritative sources to generate answers. Without SEO, you disappear from both organic rankings AND AI overviews. The strategy changed, not the necessity.

### What metrics should I track in 2026?
Track AI citation appearances (use brand monitoring tools), lead quality (conversion rates from reduced traffic), and brand search volume (people searching your company name after seeing AI citations).
