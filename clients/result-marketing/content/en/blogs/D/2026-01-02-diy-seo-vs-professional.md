## DIY SEO: What's Involved

**Time commitment: 15-25 hours/month**

**Monthly tasks:**

**Keyword Research (3-5 hours):**
- Learning tools (Ahrefs, SEMrush, Google Keyword Planner)
- Finding opportunities
- Analyzing competition
- Selecting targets

**Content Creation (8-12 hours):**
- Writing 4 blog posts (500-1000 words each)
- Optimizing service pages
- Creating location pages
- Editing and proofreading

**Technical SEO (2-4 hours):**
- Site speed checks
- Mobile optimization
- Broken link fixes
- Schema markup
- Google Search Console monitoring

**Link Building (2-4 hours):**
- Finding outreach opportunities
- Contacting webmasters
- Guest post pitches
- Directory submissions

**Total monthly hours: 15-25**

**Your time value:**
- If your time worth RM50/hour: RM750-1,250/month
- If your time worth RM100/hour: RM1,500-2,500/month

**PLUS tool costs:**
- Ahrefs/SEMrush: RM300-600/month
- AI writing tools: RM50-200/month
- **Total DIY cost: RM800-3,100/month** (including your time)
