- Click-throughs from AI Overviews

## **FAQ: AEO Revenue Attribution**

### **How accurate is AEO revenue attribution?**

70-80% accuracy is achievable with proper tracking. The remaining 20-30% represents truly unknown touchpoints, but that's far better than traditional analytics which miss 100% of zero-click AI traffic.

### **What if I don't have call tracking?**

You'll lose 40-60% of attribution data. Minimum viable setup: CRM lead source tracking + manual "How did you hear about us?" questions + branded search monitoring in GA4.

### **How long until AEO shows attributed revenue?**

First direct conversions appear in 30-45 days. Full attribution picture (including assisted revenue) emerges by month 3-4. Don't judge AEO ROI before 90 days.

### **Should I count branded searches as AEO revenue?**

Only the lift above baseline. If you normally get 500 branded searches/month and AEO drives it to 800, attribute 300 searches (60% lift) to AEO efforts, not the full 800.

### **What's a good AEO attribution rate?**

Top performers achieve 40-50% of total organic revenue attributed to AEO within 6 months. Average businesses see 20-30%. B2B with longer sales cycles see lower initial attribution (15-20%) but higher deal sizes.
