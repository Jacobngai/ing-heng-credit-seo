## **Converting Cleaning Leads**

### Trust Signals for Cleaning Services

**You're hiring strangers to enter your home/office.** Trust is critical.

**Essential Trust Elements:**
- **Background-checked cleaners** - highlight this prominently
- **Insured/bonded** - protects their property
- **Uniformed team** - professionalism
- **Own transport** - reliability
- **Eco-friendly products** - health-conscious customers
- **Before/after photos** - prove quality
- **Real reviews** with customer names
- **Satisfaction guarantee** - "free re-clean if not happy"

### Pricing Transparency Strategy

**Cleaning customers want pricing upfront.** Display rates to increase inquiries 3x.

**Example Pricing Table:**
```
Residential Cleaning:
- Weekly: RM400-600/month (4 visits)
- Bi-weekly: RM300-450/month (2 visits)
- Monthly: RM250-350/month (1 visit)
- One-time deep cleaning: RM350-600 (depending on house size)

Office Cleaning:
- Small office: RM1,500-2,500/month (daily/3x week)
- Medium office: RM2,500-5,000/month (daily)
- Large office: RM5,000-12,000/month (daily)

Specialized Cleaning:
- Move in/out cleaning: RM400-700
- Post-renovation cleaning: RM500-1,000
- Spring cleaning: RM450-800
```

### WhatsApp Inquiry Management

**65% of cleaning inquiries come via WhatsApp.**

**WhatsApp conversion strategy:**
1. **Auto-reply with pricing table**
2. **Ask qualifying questions:**
   - "Property type? (house/condo/office)"
   - "Size? (sq ft or rooms)"
   - "Frequency? (weekly/monthly/one-time)"
   - "Location? (check if in service area)"
3. **Send before/after photos** of similar jobs
4. **Offer free site visit** (for commercial jobs)
5. **Send simple agreement** via WhatsApp

## **Building Recurring Revenue**

### Contract Conversion Strategy

**Turn one-time jobs into recurring contracts:**

**Step 1:** Deliver exceptional one-time service
- Arrive on time (or early)
- Professional uniform + equipment
- Bring own supplies (convenience)
- Before/after photos
- Follow-up call (ensure satisfaction)

**Step 2:** Make contract offer immediately
- "Save RM100/month with weekly plan"
- "Same cleaner every visit" (consistency)
- "Flexible scheduling" (customer convenience)
- "Pause anytime" (no lock-in fear)

**Step 3:** Easy sign-up
- Monthly auto-debit (most customers prefer)
- WhatsApp agreement (simple)
- No long-term contract (cancel anytime)

**Conversion Rate Impact:**
- One-time jobs only: 10% become contracts
- Proactive contract offer: 35% become contracts
- **250% increase in recurring revenue**

### Commercial Cleaning Strategy

**Commercial = Higher value, longer sales cycle**

**Commercial inquiry handling:**
- Respond within 1 hour (businesses expect speed)
- Ask: "How many sq ft? How many days/week?"
- Send portfolio of similar offices cleaned
- Offer free site visit + quote (industry standard)
- Provide detailed proposal (scope of work, frequency, pricing)
- Highlight insurance + background checks (risk mitigation)

**Commercial Contract Retention:**
- Send quality check photos after each clean
- Monthly performance report
- Responsive communication (WhatsApp/phone)
- Holiday cleaning reminders
- Annual deep cleaning offer (upsell)

## **Frequently Asked Questions**

### How long does cleaning service SEO take?

**2-3 months** for local keywords like "cleaning service [your area]". Commercial cleaning keywords take **3-5 months** (higher competition, higher value). Residential cleaning keywords rank fastest.

### Should I target residential or commercial cleaning?

**Both.** Create separate pages on your website. Commercial has higher value per contract. Residential has more volume. Diversify for stability - commercial as income base, residential for cash flow.

### Can I rank for cleaning keywords in multiple languages?

**Absolutely.** 40% of Malaysians prefer searching in Malay or Chinese. We create content in EN/ZH/MS. Your website automatically serves the right language based on user preference.

### Do I need to show exact pricing on my website?

**Use ranges.** Every property is different size/condition. Show ranges (e.g., "RM400-600/month for weekly house cleaning") to set expectations without over-promising. Exact quotes after site visit or phone assessment.
