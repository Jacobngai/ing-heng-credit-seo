## [Common Success Patterns](#common-success-patterns)

### What Works Across All Industries

**1. Query Targeting (Critical)**
- Focus on queries with AI Overview presence
- Target "how much", "how to", "vs" questions
- Local modifiers ("near me", city names)

**2. Schema Implementation**
- FAQ schema: Highest impact
- Article schema: Content citations
- LocalBusiness schema: Local queries

**3. Content Velocity**
- 4-8 posts/month minimum
- Question-answer format works best
- Update old content, don't just create new

**4. Tracking & Attribution**
- Call tracking essential
- CRM lead source tracking
- Branded search monitoring

**5. Timeline Expectations**
- Month 1-2: Foundation, minimal results
