## **Direct Answer: AEO Consultant vs Agency vs In-House?**

**For most businesses, hire an AEO agency first.** Agencies provide full-service AEO (strategy + content + technical + reporting) for RM1,500-3,000/month. Consultants only provide strategy (you execute yourself) for RM3,000-8,000/project. In-house teams cost RM5,000-10,000/month in salary and require 3-6 months to build. **Choose:** Agency for immediate results, Consultant if you have an in-house team to execute, In-house only when spending RM5,000+/month on agencies.

## **The Three AEO Service Models**

### **1. AEO Agency (Full-Service)**
**What they do:** Everything from strategy to execution to reporting.
**Best for:** Most businesses needing immediate AI Overview visibility.
**Cost:** RM1,500-6,000/month.

### **2. AEO Consultant (Strategy-Only)**
**What they do:** Create the AEO roadmap, you execute it yourself.
**Best for:** Businesses with existing marketing teams to implement.
**Cost:** RM3,000-15,000/project.

### **3. In-House AEO Team (Employee)**
**What they do:** Full-time employee(s) dedicated to AEO.
**Best for:** Large businesses spending RM5,000+/month on agencies.
**Cost:** RM5,000-10,000/month per person + benefits + training.

## **Decision Framework**

### **Choose an AEO Agency If:**
- You need results within 90 days
- You don't have existing marketing team to execute AEO
- You want proven expertise without hiring/training
- Your budget is RM1,500-5,000/month
- You want flexibility (scale up/down as needed)

**This is 90% of businesses.**

### **Choose an AEO Consultant If:**
- You have 2+ marketing team members (content, technical)
- Your team can execute but lacks AEO strategy expertise
- You want to build internal AEO capability
- Your budget is RM3,000-10,000 (one-time)
- You're willing to wait 4-6 months for results

**This is 8% of businesses (usually mid-market companies).**

- You're currently spending RM5,000+/month on agencies
- AEO is critical to your business (core marketing channel)
- You have time to hire and manage employees
- You want long-term cost efficiency (2+ years)
- You can afford the hiring risk (wrong hire = wasted time)

**This is 2% of businesses (enterprise or high-growth companies).**

### **Consultant Red Flags:**

### **In-House Red Flags:**
- Person leaves after 6-12 months (knowledge loss)
- Single point of failure (only one person knows AEO)
- Stale expertise (not learning as AI search evolves)
- Salary creep (costs balloon beyond RM10,000/month)

## **FAQ: AEO Service Models**

### **When should I switch from agency to in-house?**

**Answer:** When spending RM5,000+/month on agencies consistently. Below that, agencies remain more cost-effective when you factor in salary, benefits, hiring time, and management overhead. Most businesses switch after 2-3 years with an agency.

### **Can I hire an agency and a consultant together?**

**Answer:** Yes, but usually redundant. Agencies provide strategy as part of their service. Hire a consultant only if you want a second opinion on your agency's strategy, or if you want to build in-house capability and need a roadmap.

### **How do I know if my team can execute AEO themselves?**

**Answer:** You need: (1) Strong content writer who can create comprehensive guides, (2) Developer or technical person to implement schema markup, (3) Marketer to analyze AI search opportunities. If you lack any of these, hire an agency, not a consultant.

### **What if I hire in-house and it doesn't work out?**

**Answer:** You're out 2-4 months of salary plus recruitment costs. This is why most businesses start with agencies (lower risk, cancel anytime). Only go in-house when you've done agency AEO for 2+ years and know exactly what you need.

### **Do agencies charge less than consultants long-term?**

**Answer:** Yes, initially. Consultants charge RM3,000-15,000 one-time. Agencies charge RM1,500-3,000/month. Break-even is around month 4-6. After that, consultants are cheaper long-termâ€”but only if you can execute effectively yourself. Most can't, which is why agencies provide better ROI.
