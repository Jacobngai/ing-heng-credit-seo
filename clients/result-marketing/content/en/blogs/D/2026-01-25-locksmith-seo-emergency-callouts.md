## **Converting Emergency Call-Outs**

### Mobile Optimization is Critical

**73% of emergency locksmith searches happen on mobile phones**. Someone locked out of their house or car isn't opening a laptop.

**Your mobile site must:**
1. **Click-to-call button** visible above the fold (no scrolling)
2. Load in **under 3 seconds** (every second delays = lost customer)
3. Show your **service area** clearly
4. Display **24/7 availability** prominently
5. Include **emergency pricing** (builds trust, reduces price shock calls)

### WhatsApp Integration for Locksmiths

Malaysians prefer **WhatsApp over phone calls**. Many won't call an unknown number but will message.

**WhatsApp capture strategy:**
- "I'm locked out. Can you come now?" messages
- Send photos of the lock problem
- Get instant price quotes
- Track technician location

**Conversion Rate Impact:**
- Phone-only: 15-20% conversion
- Phone + WhatsApp: 30-40% conversion
- **100% increase in bookings**

### Trust Signals for Emergency Services

When people are locked out at 2 AM, they're **vulnerable and suspicious**. Build trust instantly:

**Essential trust elements:**
- **License number** displayed prominently
- **Response time guarantee** (e.g., "30 minutes or less")
- **Upfront pricing** (emergency service fee + per-lock cost)
- **Real reviews** with photos (Google Business Profile)
- **Before/after photos** of your work
- **Uniformed technicians** - show team photos
