## **General AIEO Writing Rules**

### Content Structure

1. **Answer first** - Direct answer in first paragraph
2. **Use headers** - H1, H2, H3 for structure
3. **Short paragraphs** - 2-3 sentences max
4. **Bullet points** - For readability
5. **Bold key info** - Highlight important details

### Writing Style

- **Conversational** - Write like you're explaining to a friend
- **Direct** - Get to the point, no fluff
- **Specific** - Use numbers, data, examples (not "many" or "lots")
- **Accurate** - Verify all facts and statistics
- **Clear** - Avoid jargon, explain technical terms

### Always Include

- **FAQ section** - Answer common questions
- **Examples** - Real-world applications
- **Data** - Statistics, numbers, metrics
- **CTA** - Clear next step
- **Schema** - Structured data markup
