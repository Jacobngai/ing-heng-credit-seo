## What Are Trust Signals?

Trust signals are visible indicators that tell potential clients: **"This business is legitimate, credible, and safe to work with."**

In SEO specifically, trust signals help:
- **Google** understand your site is authoritative (E-E-A-T)
- **Prospects** feel confident hiring you
- **AI search engines** cite your content as reliable

## Trust Signal Best Practices

### Do's:
‚úÖ Be authentic (real testimonials, real case studies)
‚úÖ Use specific numbers (not vague claims)
‚úÖ Make trust signals visible (don't hide them)
‚úÖ Update regularly (fresh testimonials, new case studies)
‚úÖ Respond to reviews (shows you care)

### Don'ts:
‚ùå Fake testimonials or reviews (easily spotted, destroys trust)
‚ùå Exaggerate credentials (e.g., "Award-winning" when you haven't won awards)
‚ùå Buy reviews or backlinks (violates platform policies)
‚ùå Display outdated information (e.g., old certifications)
‚ùå Over-promise results (be realistic)
