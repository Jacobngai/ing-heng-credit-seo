This research identifies **7 compound healthcare categories** that benefit significantly from SEO in Malaysia. - **3-language strategy** required for Malaysia market

### 1. YMYL (Your Money Your Life) Category

Healthcare websites fall under Google's YMYL category, meaning:
- **Higher quality standards** required for ranking
- **Medical expertise** must be demonstrated

- Medical review of content recommended
- Backlinks from medical authorities valuable

### 2. Medical Certification Keywords (Malaysia-Specific)

**LCP (Letter of Credentialing and Privileging):**
- Required for aesthetic medical practitioners
- KKM (Ministry of Health Malaysia) certification
- Patients search for "LCP certified" clinics
- Important trust signal for aesthetic procedures

**MMC (Malaysian Medical Council):**
- Required for all medical practitioners
- Annual Practicing Certificate (APC)
- Patients verify doctor credentials
- Display registration number on website

**KKM (Kementerian Kesihatan Malaysia):**
- Ministry of Health registration
- Private healthcare facility licenses
- Pharmacy licenses
- Laboratory certifications

**SEO Strategy:**
- Display certifications prominently
- Create "About Us" page with doctor credentials
- Add registration numbers to every page
- Link to official verification websites
- Highlight LCP certification for aesthetic clinics
