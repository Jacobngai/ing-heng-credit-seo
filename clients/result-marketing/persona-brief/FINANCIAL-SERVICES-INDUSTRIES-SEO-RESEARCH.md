**Date:** January 25, 2026

---

## Executive Summary

This research identifies **40+ financial sub-industries** that benefit significantly from SEO in Malaysia. The financial services industry in Malaysia is regulated by **Bank Negara Malaysia (BNM)** and encompasses banking, insurance, investment, and emerging fintech sectors.

**Key Insight:** Financial keywords have **extremely high CPC values** (up to RM76.92 for personal loans), indicating intense advertiser competition and high customer acquisition costs. SEO offers superior ROI compared to paid advertising.
