## Executive Summary

This research identifies **30+ real estate sub-industries** that benefit significantly from SEO in Malaysia. The property market is valued at **RM500+ billion** with **hot market conditions** in urban areas (Kuala Lumpur, Penang, Johor).

**Key Insight:** Real estate searches are **high-intent and location-based**. SEO strategies must focus on **property listings, neighborhood guides, and local SEO dominance**.

- **Bank auctions** (foreclosed properties)

**Auction Process:**
- **View property** (inspect before bidding)
- **Bank valuation** (get loan pre-approval)
- **Pay deposit** (10% of reserved price)
- **Bid at auction** (highest bidder wins)
- **Settle balance** (within 90-120 days)

**SEO Benefits:** **Mandatory service**, legal requirement, commission-based

**Key Keywords:**
- "property agent" - 4,200 searches/month
- "real estate agent" - 3,800 searches/month
- "property negotiator" - 2,100 searches/month

- "real estate negotiator near me" - 2,600 searches/month (mobile)
- "house agent" - 1,900 searches/month
- "property consultant" - 1,400 searches/month
- "licensed estate agent" - 1,100 searches/month

**Customer Types:**
- Property buyers (need representation)
- Property sellers (need marketing)
- Landlords (need tenants)
- Tenants (need rental properties)
- Investors (need advice)

**Commission Structure:**
- **Sale (agency):** 2-3% of property price
  - RM500,000 property = RM10,000-15,000 commission
  - RM1,000,000 property = RM20,000-30,000 commission
- **Rental:** 0.5-1 month rent + 1 month rental
  - RM2,000/month rental = RM2,000-4,000 commission

**License Types:**
- **REN** (Real Estate Negotiator) - Basic license, must work under agency
- **REA** (Real Estate Agent) - Full license, can own agency
- **PEA** (Probationary Estate Agent) - New agents in training

**Why SEO Works:**
- **Mandatory service** (property transactions require agents)
- **Complex transactions** (legal, financial processes)
- **Local expertise** (area knowledge)
- **Negotiation skills** (get best price)
- **Time-saving** (clients save time)

**Malaysia Context:**
- **Board of Valuers, Appraisers, Estate Agents and Property Managers (BOVAEP)** - Regulates REN, REA
- **Negotiator identification tag** (must display tag)
- **Agency registration** - Must register with firm
- **Code of ethics** - Professional conduct required

**Sources:**
- [LPPEH Search](https://search.lppeh.gov.my/) (Search for licensed REN, REA)
- [BOVAEP](https://www.lppeh.gov.my/)
