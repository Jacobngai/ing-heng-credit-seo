## CIDB Malaysia Construction Categories

Based on CIDB Malaysia (https://www.cidb.gov.my/), the construction industry includes:

### CIDB Key Programs & Services:
- **Kad Hijau (Green Card)** - Personnel registration
- **Contractor Registration** - Classification by grades (G1-G7)
- **Facility Management** - Building operations & maintenance
- **IBS (Industrialized Building System)** - Prefabricated construction
- **BIM with AR/VR** - Digital construction technology
- **Construction Training** - Skills & certification programs
- **Takaful** - Insurance for construction personnel
- **SFERA** - Construction innovation hub
- **ADR (Alternative Dispute Resolution)** - Construction dispute resolution
- **Sustainable Construction** - Green building practices
