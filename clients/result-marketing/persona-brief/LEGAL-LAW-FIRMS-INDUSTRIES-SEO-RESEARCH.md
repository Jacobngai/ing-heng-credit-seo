## Executive Summary

This research identifies **7 compound legal categories** that benefit significantly from SEO in Malaysia. The legal industry in Malaysia is governed by the **Malaysian Bar** and encompasses multiple practice areas, from criminal defense to corporate mergers worth millions of ringgit.

**Key Insight:** Legal services have **extremely high customer lifetime value** - a single divorce case can be worth RM10,000-50,000, while corporate M&A can reach RM500,000+. SEO investment yields massive ROI.
