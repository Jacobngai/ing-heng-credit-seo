## Executive Summary

### Content Philosophy
We create content that solves real business problems for equipment financing customers in Malaysia. Every article must pass the **A.R.T. Framework**:

- **A**ttract - Hook them in 3 seconds with specific numbers and pain points
- **R**elevance - Prove we understand their industry, equipment, and situation
- **T**rust - Give confidence with 40+ years experience, real rates, clear CTAs

## Quality Standards

### Every Post Must Have

1. **SEO-Optimized Title**
   - Equipment type + benefit + urgency + year
   - Example: "Excavator Financing 0% Deposit: Get Approved in 48 Hours (2026 Guide)"

2. **Meta Description**
   - Under 160 characters
   - Include primary keyword
   - Clear value proposition
   - Call to action

3. **Content Structure**
   - Hook (3 seconds)
   - Problem agitation (100 words)
   - Solution preview (50 words)
   - Main content (1,000+ words)
   - Ing Heng solution (200 words)
   - Social proof (100 words)
   - FAQ (3-5 questions)
   - Conclusion + CTA

4. **Key Elements**
   - Real numbers (rates, amounts, timeframes)
   - Malaysian context (locations, regulations)
   - Specific equipment examples
   - Clear CTA (WhatsApp + Calculator)

5. **Internal Linking**
   - Link to related equipment posts
   - Link to service pages
   - Link to calculator
   - Link to WhatsApp
