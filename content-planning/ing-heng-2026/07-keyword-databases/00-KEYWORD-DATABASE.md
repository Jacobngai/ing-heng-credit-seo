## Content Keyword Integration

### Blog Post Keyword Formula

```markdown
Each blog post should target:

1 Primary Keyword (Main topic):
- excavator financing

2 Secondary Keywords (Supporting topics):
- 0% deposit, 48-hour approval, bad credit

3 LSI Keywords (Related concepts):
- construction equipment, heavy machinery, hire purchase

4 Long-Tail Keywords (Specific phrases):
- excavator financing for SME contractors Klang Valley

5 Location Keywords (if applicable):
- Selangor, Klang Valley, etc.

6 Brand Keywords (for comparison posts):
- Komatsu vs Caterpillar, etc.

Distribution in content:
- Title: 1 primary + 1 modifier
- Meta description: 1 primary
- H1: 1 primary
- First paragraph: 1 primary
- H2s: 2-3 primary or variations
- Body: 1-2% density for primary, 0.5-1% for secondary
- Internal links: Keyword-rich anchor text
- Image alt: Primary keyword in context
```
