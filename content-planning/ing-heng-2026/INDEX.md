## Quality Standards

### Every Post Must Have

1. **SEO-Optimized Title**
   - Equipment + Benefit + Urgency + Year
   - Under 60 characters
   - Includes primary keyword

2. **Compelling Meta Description**
   - Under 160 characters
   - Includes primary keyword
   - Clear value proposition
   - Call to action

3. **Strong Hook**
   - Grabs attention in 3 seconds
   - Specific number or pain point
   - Relevant to target audience

4. **Problem Agitation**
   - Why this matters
   - What happens without solution
   - Emotional connection

5. **Clear Solution**
   - How Ing HENG helps
   - Specific advantages
   - Real numbers and terms

6. **Social Proof**
   - Case studies or testimonials
   - Results achieved
   - Customer satisfaction

7. **FAQ Section**
   - 3-5 common questions
   - Direct answers
   - Builds trust

8. **Strong CTA**
   - WhatsApp link prominent
   - Calculator link
   - Phone number visible
   - Email included
