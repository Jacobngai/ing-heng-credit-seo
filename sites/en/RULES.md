# CONTENT RULES - Ing Heng Credit

## ❌ FORBIDDEN CONTENT

### 1. NO Personal Loans
**Zero tolerance.** Delete any content mentioning:
- personal loan
- blacklisted loan
- CTOS personal financing
- urgent personal loan
- Any consumer/individual loan content

**Why:** Company does NOT do personal loans. Attracts wrong leads (Malay consumer market). Focus is B2B equipment financing only.

### 2. NO Time-Based Approval Claims
**Absolutely forbidden:**
- "24 hour approval"
- "48 hour approval" 
- "72 hour approval"
- "fast approval"
- "fast loan"
- "quick approval"
- "instant approval"
- "same day approval"

**Acceptable alternatives:**
- "Streamlined processing"
- "Straightforward application"
- "Experienced evaluation team"

**Why:** Cannot make time commitments. Regulatory risk + unrealistic expectations.

### 3. Move Away from "Business Loan" Terminology

**Minimize/avoid:**
- "business loan"
- "SME loan"
- "working capital loan"

**Prefer instead:**
- "Equipment financing"
- "Machinery financing"
- "Hire purchase"
- "Asset financing"
- Specific equipment types (excavator financing, lorry financing, etc.)

**Why:** Focus is equipment-backed financing, not general business lending.
