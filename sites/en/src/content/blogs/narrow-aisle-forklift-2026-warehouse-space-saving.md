## Why Narrow Aisle Forklifts? Maximize Warehouse Space

Narrow aisle (VNA) forklifts are designed for warehouses where every square meter counts. With compact design, specialized mast, and ability to operate in aisles as narrow as 1.6 meters, these forklifts increase storage capacity by 30-50% compared to standard forklifts.

Narrow Aisle Forklift Advantages:
• 30-50% more storage capacity
      • Aisle width: 1.6-2.0 meters (vs. 3.5-4.0 meters standard)
      • High lift height: up to 14 meters
      • Electric operation (indoor friendly)
      • Precision handling (specialized masts)

## VNA Forklift Types: Choose the Right One

### 1. Narrow Aisle Reach Truck

Best For: Warehouses transitioning to narrow aisles

Features:
• Aisle width: 2.0-2.4 meters
      • Lift height: 6-9 meters
      • Reach mechanism: extends forks into rack

Advantages:
• Lower cost than turret trucks
      • Can also work as standard reach truck (flexible)
      • Easier operator training

Limitations:
• Cannot stack as high as VNA
      • Requires wider aisles than turret trucks

### 2. VNA Turret Truck

Best For: High-density storage warehouses

Features:
• Aisle width: 1.6-1.8 meters
      • Lift height: 9-14 meters
      • Turret head: rotates 180 degrees

Advantages:
• Maximum storage density
      • Very high lift capability
      • Operates in extremely narrow aisles

Limitations:
• Highest cost
      • Specialized operator training required
      • Cannot work outside narrow aisles

### 3. Guided VNA (Wire/Rail Guided)

Best For: Fully automated high-bay warehouses

Features:
• Aisle width: 1.6 meters
      • Guidance system: wire in floor or rail
      • Automated operation: minimal operator input

Advantages:
• Highest precision
      • Fastest operation
      • Compatible with warehouse automation

Limitations:
• Most expensive
      • Requires floor installation (guide wire/rail)
      • Fixed to aisles (cannot move between aisles easily)
