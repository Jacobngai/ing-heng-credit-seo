## What is a Pallet Stacker?

A pallet stacker lifts and moves pallets. It is smaller than a forklift. It works well in small warehouses.

Pallet stackers move pallets from floor to shelves. They stack items on top of each other. This saves floor space.

## Why Use Pallet Stackers?

- Perfect for small spaces
- Lower cost than forklifts
- Easy to operate
- No license needed for some models
- Good for light to medium loads

## Types of Pallet Stackers

### Manual Pallet Stacker

Powered by hand. No battery needed. Cheapest option. Good for light use. Hard for all-day work.

### Electric Pallet Stacker

Runs on battery. Easy to use. Good for all-day work. Costs more than manual.

### Walk-Behind Pallet Stacker

Operator walks behind it. Compact size. Good for small aisles.

### Rider Pallet Stacker

Operator stands on platform. Faster work. Good for large areas.

## Choose the Right Pallet Stacker

Ask these questions:

- How much weight? (heavier needs stronger stacker)
- How high to lift? (higher needs bigger stacker)
- How many uses per day? (more uses needs electric)
- How wide are aisles? (narrow needs walk-behind)
- What is your budget? (manual is cheaper)
