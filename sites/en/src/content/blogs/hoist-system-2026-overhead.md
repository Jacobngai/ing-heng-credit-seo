## What is a Hoist System?

A hoist lifts heavy items up. It uses a cable and motor. It hangs from above.

Hoists move items vertically. They lift loads to high places. This makes work safer and easier.

## Why Use Hoist Systems?

- Lift heavy items safely
- Save worker strength
- Prevent back injuries
- Faster lifting
- Move items overhead

## Types of Hoist Systems

### Electric Chain Hoist

Uses chain to lift. Good for light to medium loads. Lower cost. Compact size.

### Wire Rope Hoist

Uses steel cable. Good for heavy loads. Higher cost. Strong and durable.

### Pneumatic Hoist

Uses air pressure. Good for explosive areas. No sparks. Safe for chemicals.

### Manual Chain Hoist

Operated by hand. No power needed. Lowest cost. Slow but reliable.
