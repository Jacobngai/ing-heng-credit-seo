## What to Check When Buying Used

### 1. Service History

- Regular maintenance done?
- Major repairs recorded?
- Oil changes documented?
- Inspection reports available?

### 2. Hours Used

- Under 10,000 hours: Excellent
- 10,000-15,000 hours: Good
- 15,000-20,000 hours: Fair
- Above 20,000 hours: Careful inspection needed

### 3. Physical Condition

- Rust and corrosion check
- Hydraulic system condition
- Wire ropes and cables
- Engine performance
- Tire condition (mobile cranes)

### 4. Certification Status

- DOSH certification current?
- Last inspection date
- Any modifications documented?
- Load testing records
