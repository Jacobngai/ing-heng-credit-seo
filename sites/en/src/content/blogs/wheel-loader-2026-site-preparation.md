## Why Wheel Loaders? Site Preparation Workhorse

Wheel loaders are the versatile workhorses of construction site preparation. With front-mounted bucket, articulating steering, and all-wheel drive, wheel loaders excel at loading trucks, stockpiling materials, grading surfaces, and general site cleanup.

Wheel Loader Advantages:
• Versatile: loading, grading, leveling, stockpiling
      • Fast cycle times: quick truck loading
      • Mobility: travels between job sites quickly
      • All-wheel drive: works in mud, loose soil
      • Various attachments: buckets, forks, brooms
