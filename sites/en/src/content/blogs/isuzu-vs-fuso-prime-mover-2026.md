## Fuso Great: Detailed Analysis

### Engine and Performance

Engine: 6M60 7.5L diesel, 320 HP, 1,569 Nm torque

Performance:
• Adequate power for most loads
  • Struggles on steep hills with full load
  • Slower acceleration
  • Comfortable cruising at 80-90 km/h

### Fuel Economy

Consumption: 40L/100km (loaded highway)

Monthly Fuel Cost (Klang-Penang route, 20 trips):
• Distance: 800 km round trip × 20 = 16,000 km
  • Fuel: 16,000 ÷ 100 × 40L = 6,400L
  • Cost: 6,400L × RM2.55 = RM16,320 per month

### Maintenance Costs

Monthly Average: RM1,800-RM2,500

Includes:
• Oil change: RM400-RM600 (every 10,000 km)
  • Filters: RM150-RM250
  • Tyres: RM700-RM1,000 per month average
  • Minor repairs: RM500-RM700

### Reliability

Strengths:
• Simple mechanics
  • Easy to repair
  • Parts widely available
  • Lower repair costs

Common Issues:
• Clutch replacement at 400,000 km
  • Gasket leaks after 500,000 km

### Resale Value

Depreciation: 10-12% per year

Examples:
• New (RM350,000) → 5 years (RM190,000-RM210,000)
  • 5 years (RM200,000) → 10 years (RM100,000-RM120,000)

## When to Choose Isuzu

### Best For:

- Long-Distance Haulage: Better fuel economy at highway speeds
- Heavy Loads: 380 HP handles steep terrain better
- High-Utilization: Running 25,000+ km/month, fuel savings matter
- Long-Term Investment: Higher resale value when selling
- Driver Comfort: Better cab ergonomics for long trips

## When to Choose Fuso

### Best For:

- Lower Initial Investment: RM30,000 cheaper to purchase
- Short-Medium Distance: Fuel penalty less significant
- Simple Maintenance: Easier and cheaper to repair
- Parts Availability: Widely available, lower cost
- SME Starters: Lower entry price for first truck
