## Why Reach Trucks? High-Rise Storage Essential

Reach trucks are the backbone of modern high-rise warehousing. With extending mast design, ability to lift pallets to 12+ meters, and compact footprint for narrow aisles, reach trucks maximize vertical storage space while minimizing warehouse footprint.

Reach Truck Advantages:
• High lift height: up to 13 meters
      • Extending forks: reach into racks
      • Narrow aisle operation: 2.4-3.0 meters
      • Electric operation: indoor friendly
      • Precision handling: accurate placement
