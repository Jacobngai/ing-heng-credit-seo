## Cash Flow Challenges

### Why Businesses Are Cash-Constrained

- Seasonal revenue fluctuations
- Client payment delays (30-90 days)
- Unexpected expenses
- Growth requires inventory investment
- Economic downturn impact

### What Cash-Constrained Means

- Less than 3 months operating expenses in bank
- Difficulty meeting weekly expenses
- No cash available for down payments
- Living invoice to invoice
- Can't afford equipment deposit
