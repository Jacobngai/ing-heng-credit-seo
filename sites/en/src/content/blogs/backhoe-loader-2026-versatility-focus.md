## Why Backhoe Loaders? Ultimate Versatility

Backhoe loaders are the Swiss Army knives of construction equipment. With excavating rear bucket and loading front bucket, plus versatile attachments, backhoes handle trenching, excavation, loading, grading, and more—all in one machine that travels on roads between job sites.

Backhoe Loader Advantages:
• Two machines in one: excavator + wheel loader
      • Road travel: drives between sites (no trailer needed)
      • Versatile attachments: hammers, augers, forks, brooms
      • Compact size: works in confined urban spaces
      • Lower cost: cheaper than separate excavator + loader
