## Why Tower Crane Business? High-Rise Construction Essential

Tower cranes are essential for Malaysia's high-rise construction boom. From condominiums to commercial towers, MRT stations to hospitals, tower cranes provide the lifting height, capacity, and coverage that mobile cranes cannot match for tall buildings.

Tower Crane Business Model:
• Rental income: RM25,000 - RM60,000 monthly per crane
      • Long-term contracts: 12-24 months typical
      • High demand: Urban development, infrastructure projects
      • Specialized service: Few competitors, premium rates
