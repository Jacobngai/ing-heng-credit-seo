## Why Mobile Cranes? Construction Lifting Essential

Mobile cranes are indispensable for construction projects requiring heavy lifting. From high-rise building materials to HVAC unit installation, bridge construction to steel erection, mobile cranes provide the lifting capacity and reach that excavators and forklifts cannot match.

Mobile Crane Advantages:
• High lifting capacity: 5-200 tons
      • Long reach: up to 60 meters boom
      • Mobility: quick setup between job sites
      • Versatility: various attachments for different lifts
      • Compact footprint: operates in tight spaces

## Mobile Crane Applications by Project Type

### 1. High-Rise Building Construction

Tasks:
• Lifting steel beams to upper floors
      • Hoisting concrete buckets
      • Installing HVAC units on roof
      • Placing glass panels (curtain walls)

Crane Required:
• 20-30 ton capacity
      • 40-50 meter boom
      • High visibility from operator cab

### 2. Bridge Construction

Tasks:
• Placing bridge beams
      • Lifting concrete deck segments
      • Installing bridge girders

Crane Required:
• 50-80 ton capacity
      • 50-60 meter boom
      • All-terrain capability (rough ground)

### 3. Industrial Plant Installation

Tasks:
• Installing heavy machinery
      • Placing generators, transformers
      • Lifting pressure vessels

Crane Required:
• 30-50 ton capacity
      • Precision controls (accurate placement)
