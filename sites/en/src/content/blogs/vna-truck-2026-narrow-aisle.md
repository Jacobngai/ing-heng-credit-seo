## What is a VNA Truck?

VNA means Very Narrow Aisle. VNA trucks work in very narrow spaces. They help warehouses store more items.

VNA trucks can operate in aisles as narrow as 1.5 meters. Regular forklifts need 3-4 meters.

## Why Use VNA Trucks?

- Maximize storage space
- Work in very narrow aisles
- Store items higher
- Reduce warehouse floor space
- Lower rent costs

## Types of VNA Trucks

### Man-Up VNA Truck

Operator goes up with the load. Can pick items at height. Best for order picking.

### Man-Down VNA Truck

Operator stays on ground. Machine does the lifting. Best for pallet handling.

### Swing Mast VNA

Mast swings left and right. Can reach into racks from aisle. Very flexible.

### Mast Type VNA

Mast moves straight up and down. Simple and strong. Good for heavy loads.
