<h2>What is a Lorry with Crane?</h2>
<p>A lorry with a crane is the ultimate multi-purpose truck for any Malaysian SME. It combines heavy transport with lifting power, meaning you don't need to rent a separate crane for loading. Whether it's a Hiab, Palfinger, self-loader, or boom truck, one vehicle does both jobs: deliver the materials, lift them safely, and move on to the next site. It's a game-changer for construction, logistics, and transport businesses.</p>
<h2>Types of Lorry with Crane</h2>
<h3>Hiab Truck</h3>
<p>Hiab is the most popular crane brand in Malaysia for a reason—Swedish quality that's reliable and strong. With a 3-20 ton capacity, it fits perfectly on Hino, Isuzu, UD, or Fuso chassis. Whether you need a 5-ton or 15-ton model, Hiab is a trusted choice seen on construction sites all over the country.</p>
<h3>Palfinger Crane Truck</h3>
<p>Palfinger is an Austrian brand known for precision and heavy-duty lifting. They are particularly popular for container handling and heavy machinery. If your daily operations involve lifting 10-20 tons with pinpoint accuracy, a Palfinger-mounted truck is a solid investment.</p>
<h3>Self-Loader Lorry</h3>
<p>A self-loader is a smart choice for business owners looking to save on labor. With the crane mounted at the back, a single driver can operate everything alone. You load, transport, and unload without needing an extra worker. It's the ideal setup for moving construction materials like sand, gravel, or bricks.</p>
<h3>Boom Truck</h3>
<p>Need extra reach? A boom truck uses a telescopic crane to get into hard-to-reach places. If you're working on building sites with limited access or overhead lifting requirements, the 5-15 ton boom truck is your best friend.</p>

<p>Lift 20ft containers. Move shipping containers. Transfer between sites. Palfinger cranes excel here. 10-20 ton capacity.</p>
<h3>Machinery Transport</h3>
