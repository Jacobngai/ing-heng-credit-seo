## Types of Warehouse Automation

### Conveyor Automation

Moves items automatically between areas. Reduces walking time.

### Automated Storage

Robots store and retrieve items. Very high density storage.

### Sorting Systems

Automatically sorts items to correct areas. Good for shipping.

### Warehouse Software

Computer tracks everything. Knows where each item is.
