## 2026 Budget Incentives

### Capital Allowance for Transport

- Year 1: 40% initial allowance
- Year 2-5: 20% annual allowance

RM135,000 lorry = RM54,000 Year 1 tax deduction. Reduces taxable income significantly.

### Diesel Subsidy (SKDS 2.0)

Registered transport companies qualify for diesel subsidy. Reduces fuel costs by RM0.50 - RM1.00 per liter.

### Investment Tax Allowance

Transport companies expanding fleet may qualify for ITA. Check LHDN guidelines for 2026.
