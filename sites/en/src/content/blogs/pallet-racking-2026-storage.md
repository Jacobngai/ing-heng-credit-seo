## What is Pallet Racking?

Pallet racking is a storage system. It holds pallets on shelves. It helps warehouses store items vertically.

Racking systems go from floor to ceiling. This maximizes storage space.

## Why Use Pallet Racking?

- Store items high up
- Organized storage
- Easy access
- Safe for heavy loads
- Flexible design

## Types of Pallet Racking

### Selective Racking

Most common type. Every pallet is accessible. Low cost per position. Good for variety.

### Drive-In Racking

Forklift drives into racks. High density. Fewer aisles needed. Good for same items.

### Push Back Racking

Pallets push back on rails. 2-5 pallets deep. Good density with some selectivity.

### Cantilever Racking

For long items. Pipes, lumber, sheets. No front posts. Good for odd-shaped items.
