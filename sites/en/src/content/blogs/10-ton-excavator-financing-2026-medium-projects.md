## Why 10-Ton Excavators? The Medium Project Sweet Spot

The 10-ton excavator occupies the ideal middle ground in Malaysia's construction equipment market. It's powerful enough for medium-scale projects yet compact enough for urban job sites where 20-ton machines can't access.

Typical 10-Ton Excavator Applications:
• Commercial building foundations (2-4 stories)
      • Road and highway construction (sub-base preparation)
      • Drainage and sewer projects (municipal scale)
      • Residential development (cluster houses, condominiums)
      • Industrial earthmoving (factory sites, warehouses)
      • Pipeline installation (medium depth, long distance)
