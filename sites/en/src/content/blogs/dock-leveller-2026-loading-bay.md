## What is a Dock Leveller?

A dock leveller bridges the gap. It connects the loading bay to the truck. It moves up and down.

Trucks have different heights. The leveller adjusts to match. This makes loading safe and fast.

## Why Use Dock Levellers?

- Safe loading
- Fast operations
- Fits any truck height
- Reduces worker injury
- Protects goods

## Types of Dock Levellers

### Mechanical Dock Leveller

Uses springs to operate. Worker pulls a lever to lower. Lower cost. Reliable and simple.

### Hydraulic Dock Leveller

Uses hydraulic power. Push button to operate. Easy to use. Higher cost but very convenient.

### Edge-of-Dock Leveller

Mounted on dock edge. For smaller height differences. Lower cost than pit levellers.

### Pit Type Dock Leveller

Installed in pit floor. Most common type. Largest height range.
