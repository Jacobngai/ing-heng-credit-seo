## Why Diesel Forklifts? Outdoor Powerhouse

Diesel forklifts dominate outdoor and heavy-duty applications in Malaysia. With superior power, higher lifting capacities, all-weather operation, and lower upfront cost compared to electric, diesel forklifts are the practical choice for construction sites, lumber yards, brick manufacturers, and outdoor storage facilities.

Diesel Forklift Advantages:
• High power and torque (heavy loads, steep ramps)
      • All-weather operation (rain or shine)
      • Higher lifting capacities (up to 16 tons)
      • Lower upfront cost (vs. equivalent electric)
      • No charging downtime (continuous operation)
      • Outdoor operation (no emissions concern)

## Common Diesel Forklift Applications

### 1. Construction Sites

Tasks:
• Loading/unloading construction materials
      • Moving pallets of cement, bricks, steel
      • Organizing site materials
      • Loading trucks for delivery

Why Diesel:
• Outdoor operation (no emissions concern)
      • Rough terrain capability (solid tires, high ground clearance)
      • Heavy loads (cement bags, steel beams)
      • Continuous operation (refuel quickly)

### 2. Lumber Yards

Tasks:
• Moving timber bundles
      • Loading delivery trucks
      • Stacking lumber for storage
      • Organizing wood by type, size

Why Diesel:
• Outdoor storage (open to rain)
      • Heavy loads (large timber bundles)
      • All-weather operation

### 3. Brick & Tile Manufacturers

Tasks:
• Loading kiln output onto pallets
      • Moving finished bricks to storage
      • Loading delivery trucks
      • Organizing inventory

Why Diesel:
• Heavy loads (pallets of bricks weigh 1-2 tons)
      • Outdoor operation
      • Dusty environment (diesel more tolerant)

### 4. Steel & Metal Fabrication

Tasks:
• Moving steel coils, beams, plates
      • Loading raw materials
      • Organizing finished products
      • Loading delivery trucks

Why Diesel:
• Heavy loads (steel coils 3-5 tons)
      • Outdoor storage yards
      • High power for lifting capacity
