## What is a Conveyor System?

A conveyor system moves items automatically. It uses belts, rollers, or chains. Items move from one place to another.

Conveyors reduce manual work. Workers don't carry items. The system does the moving.

## Why Use Conveyor Systems?

- Move items automatically
- Reduce worker fatigue
- Faster processing
- Fewer errors
- Lower labor costs

## Types of Conveyor Systems

### Roller Conveyor

Uses rollers to move items. Good for boxes and pallets. Can be gravity or powered.

### Belt Conveyor

Uses a continuous belt. Good for loose items and small packages. Smooth movement.

### Chain Conveyor

Uses chains to move items. Good for heavy loads. Very strong and durable.

### Overhead Conveyor

Runs above the ground. Saves floor space. Good for hanging items.
