## The Real Approval Decision Matrix

### Traditional Bank Approval Factors

**Primary Factors (60% of decision):**

• Debt service coverage ratio (minimum 1.25x)

• Credit history perfection (no late payments)

• Industry risk classification (preferred vs restricted)

• Collateral coverage (120-150% of loan amount)

• Business age and stability (minimum 3 years preferred)

**Secondary Factors (40% of decision):**

• Management experience and qualifications

• Market position and competitive advantages

• Financial trend analysis (growing vs declining)

• Loan purpose and strategic fit

• Relationship banking history

**Typical Approval Rate:** 20-30% for SMEs

### Equipment Financing Approval Factors

**Primary Factors (60% of decision):**

• Equipment value and marketability

• Business cash flow vs equipment payment

• Equipment condition and age (up to 10+ years accepted)

• Industry experience of management

• Personal credit history of directors

**Secondary Factors (40% of decision):**

• Business growth potential

• Equipment utilization plan

• Insurance and maintenance planning

• Supplier relationship and support

• Down payment amount offered

**Typical Approval Rate:** 60-80% for legitimate businesses

### Specialist Lender Approval Factors

**Primary Factors (50% of decision):**

• Current business performance

• Management capability and experience

• Market opportunity and demand

• Asset security and recovery options

• Cash flow generation ability

**Secondary Factors (50% of decision):**

• Growth strategy and planning

• Customer base and diversification

• Operational efficiency and systems

• Financial management sophistication

• Industry knowledge and positioning

**Typical Approval Rate:** 50-70% for viable businesses

## Understanding Lender Risk Assessment

### Risk Categories and Approval Impact

**Low Risk (High Approval Probability):**

• Established businesses (5+ years)

• Stable industries (healthcare, essential services)

• Strong credit history (no defaults)

• Substantial collateral or guarantees

• Conservative financial leverage

**Medium Risk (Moderate Approval Probability):**

• Growing businesses (2-5 years)

• Cyclical industries (construction, retail)

• Acceptable credit with minor issues

• Equipment or partial collateral

• Reasonable debt levels

**High Risk (Challenging Approval):**

• New businesses (under 2 years)

• Volatile industries (technology, trading)

• Credit problems or limited history

• Limited collateral or security

• High existing debt levels

### Industry Risk Classification

**Preferred Industries (Easier Approval):**

**Essential Services:**

• Healthcare and medical services

• Education and training

• Utilities and infrastructure

• Government-related services

• Food production and distribution

**Stable Industries:**

• Professional services (legal, accounting)

• Established manufacturing

• Transportation and logistics

• Automotive services

**Challenging Industries (Harder Approval):**

**Volatile Industries:**

• Technology startups

• Entertainment and events

• Fashion and retail

• Import/export trading

• Speculative investments

**Restricted Industries:**

• Gambling and gaming

• Adult entertainment

• Cryptocurrency and digital assets

• Multi-level marketing

## Common Approval Killers and Solutions

### Approval Killer 1: Inconsistent Information

**The Problem:**

• Different revenue figures across documents

• Inconsistent business addresses or contact information

• Varying equipment specifications in different documents

• Conflicting timeline or project information

**The Solution:**

• Review all documents for consistency before submission

• Prepare single source of truth for all key facts

• Use professional document preparation services

• Implement document version control procedures

### Approval Killer 2: Unrealistic Projections

**The Problem:**

• Revenue projections that exceed industry norms

• Overly optimistic equipment utilization assumptions

• Underestimated operating costs and expenses

• Aggressive market penetration timeline

**The Solution:**

• Research industry benchmarks and standards

• Use conservative assumptions in all projections

• Include detailed supporting research and analysis

• Show sensitivity analysis for different scenarios

### Approval Killer 3: Poor Financial Management Evidence

**The Problem:**

• Irregular banking patterns without explanations

• Lack of financial controls and systems

• Poor record keeping and documentation

• No evidence of financial planning or management

**The Solution:**

• Implement professional accounting systems

• Maintain regular financial reporting

• Show evidence of cash flow management

• Demonstrate financial planning sophistication

### Approval Killer 4: Inadequate Business Substance

**The Problem:**

• Generic business description without specifics

• No evidence of actual business operations

• Lack of customer relationships or contracts

• Missing industry knowledge or expertise

**The Solution:**

• Provide detailed business operations description

• Document actual customer relationships and contracts

• Show evidence of industry involvement and knowledge

• Present professional business image and documentation
