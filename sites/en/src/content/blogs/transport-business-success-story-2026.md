## Lessons Learned

- Start Small: One truck is enough to begin. Prove the model, then scale.
- Preserve Cash: 0% down payment keeps cash for operations and growth.
- Reinvest Everything: Profits buy more trucks. Compound growth is powerful.
- Build Relationships: Good payment history opens doors for more financing.
- Focus on Profit: Revenue matters, but profit builds fleets.
