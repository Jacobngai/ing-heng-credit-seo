## What is an Order Picker?

An order picker lifts the operator to the shelf. The operator picks items by hand. It is used for picking individual items.

Order pickers are different from forklifts. Forklifts move whole pallets. Order pickers move individual items.

## Why E-commerce Needs Order Pickers

E-commerce grows fast in Malaysia. Customers order single items. Warehouses must pick many small orders.

- Pick individual items fast
- Reach high shelves easily
- Reduce operator walking
- Faster order fulfillment
- Fewer picking errors
