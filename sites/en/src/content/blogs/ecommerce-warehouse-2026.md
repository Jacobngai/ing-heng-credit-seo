## Essential E-commerce Equipment

### Order Pickers

Essential for picking individual items. Operator goes up with the platform. Reaches high shelves easily.

### Conveyors

Move items from picking to packing. Reduces walking. Speeds up fulfillment.

### Packing Stations

Designated areas for packing orders. Includes tables, scales, and label printers.

### Bin Shelving

Storage for many small items. Each item has its own bin. Easy to find and pick.
