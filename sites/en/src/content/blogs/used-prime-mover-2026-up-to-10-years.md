## What We Look For

### Inspection Criteria

- Engine Condition: Compression test, oil pressure, smoke levels
- Transmission: Gear shifting smoothness, clutch condition
- Chassis: Rust, structural integrity, accident damage
- Service History: Regular maintenance records
- Tyre Condition: Minimum 50% tread
- Electrical: Lights, dashboard, sensors working
