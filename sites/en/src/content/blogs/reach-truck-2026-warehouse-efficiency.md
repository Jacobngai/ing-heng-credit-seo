## What is a Reach Truck?

A reach truck is a special forklift. It reaches high shelves. It works in narrow spaces. It helps warehouses store more items.

Reach trucks lift things to 10-13 meters high. They extend their forks forward. This lets them reach deep into racks.

## Why Use Reach Trucks in 2026?

Warehouses need more space. Land costs are high. Reach trucks help store items higher. This saves floor space.

Benefits:

- Store items up high
- Work in narrow aisles
- Move heavy items easily
- Save warehouse space
- Faster than manual work

## Types of Reach Trucks

### Electric Reach Truck

Best for indoor use. Quiet and clean. No fumes. Good for food and medicine warehouses.

### LPG Reach Truck

Can work outdoor too. Stronger power. Good for cold storage (works in low temps).

### Stand-Up Reach Truck

Operator stands up. Good for fast moves. Best when getting on and off often.

### Sit-Down Reach Truck

Operator sits down. Good for long shifts. More comfortable for all-day work.

## Which Reach Truck Do You Need?

Ask these questions:

- How high are your shelves? (higher reach needs bigger truck)
- How narrow are your aisles? (narrow aisles need special truck)
- How much weight? (heavier loads need stronger truck)
- Indoor only or outdoor too? (electric is indoor only)
- How many hours per day? (more hours needs better quality)
