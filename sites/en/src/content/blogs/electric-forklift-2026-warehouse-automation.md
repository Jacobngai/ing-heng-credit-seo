## Why Electric Forklifts? Warehouse Automation Future

Electric forklifts are transforming Malaysia's warehouse industry. With zero emissions, lower operating costs, quieter operation, and advanced automation compatibility, electric forklifts are the standard for modern warehouses, logistics centers, and manufacturing facilities.

Electric Forklift Advantages:
• Zero emissions (indoor use without ventilation concerns)
      • Lower operating costs (electricity cheaper than diesel)
      • Quieter operation (warehouse noise reduced)
      • Smoother performance (instant torque, precise control)
      • Lower maintenance (no engine, transmission, exhaust)
      • Automation-ready (compatible with AGV systems)

## Electric Forklift Applications by Industry

### E-commerce Warehousing

Requirements:
• High throughput (1,000+ pallets daily)
      • Multi-shift operation (24/7)
      • Narrow aisle forklifts (space optimization)
      • Fast charging capability (minimal downtime)

Electric Forklift Advantage:
• Zero emissions (indoor operation safe)
      • Quiet (night shift operation without disturbance)
      • Smooth operation (reduced product damage)

### Cold Chain Warehousing

Requirements:
• Operation in chilled/freezer environments
  • Condensation-resistant components
  • Heated cabin for operator comfort

Electric Forklift Advantage:
• No diesel emissions (air quality maintained)
      • Consistent performance in cold (diesel struggles)
      • No engine heat (temperature controlled)

### Food & Beverage Warehousing

Requirements:
• Sanitary operation (no contamination risk)
      • Indoor/outdoor versatility

Electric Forklift Advantage:
• No exhaust fumes (food safety compliance)
      • No oil leaks (clean floor maintained)
      • Quiet operation (reduced worker fatigue)
