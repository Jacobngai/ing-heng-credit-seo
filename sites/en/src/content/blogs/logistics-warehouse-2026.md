## What is a Logistics Warehouse?

A logistics warehouse moves goods in and out fast. It is a transport hub. Trucks come and go all day.

Logistics warehouses are busy. Speed is important. Forklifts and equipment must work hard.

- Move goods fast
- Store many items
- Load and unload trucks
- Sort shipments
- Distribution center

## Types of Logistics Warehouses

### Distribution Center

Sends goods to many locations. Fast turnover. Many loading bays needed.

### Cross-Dock Warehouse

Items move in and out quickly. No long storage. Very fast operations.

### Transload Facility

Items move from truck to truck. Different types of transport used.

### Hub and Spoke

Central warehouse with smaller satellites. Covers large area.
