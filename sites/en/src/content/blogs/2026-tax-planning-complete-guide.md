## Why Equipment Purchase Reduces Tax

When you buy equipment, you get tax deductions:

- Capital Allowance: Deduct equipment cost over time
- Initial Allowance: Big deduction in Year 1
- Annual Allowance: Ongoing deductions each year

These deductions lower your taxable income. Lower taxable income means less tax to pay.

## Capital Allowance: The Tax Benefit

When you buy equipment, you claim capital allowance:

Initial Allowance (Year 1):
• 20% to 40% of equipment cost
      • Reduces taxable income immediately
      • Biggest deduction in first year

Annual Allowance (Year 2+):
• 10% to 20% of remaining value
      • Claimed every year until fully deducted
      • Ongoing tax benefit

Example: RM100,000 Excavator
• Year 1 (40% initial): RM40,000 deduction
      • Year 2 (20%): RM12,000 deduction
      • Year 3 (20%): RM9,600 deduction
      • Year 4 (20%): RM7,680 deduction
      • Year 5 (20%): RM6,144 deduction
      Total Deductions: RM75,422
