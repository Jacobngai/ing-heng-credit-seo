## Types of Cold Storage

### Freezer (-18C to -25C)

For ice cream, frozen food, meat. Most expensive to run. Thickest insulation needed.

### Chiller (0C to +4C)

For fresh meat, dairy, drinks. Less expensive than freezer.

### Cool Room (+10C to +15C)

For fruits, vegetables, wine. Least expensive. Moderate cooling.

### Pharmaceutical Cold Storage (+2C to +8C)

For medicine, vaccines. Strict temperature control. Most precise equipment.
