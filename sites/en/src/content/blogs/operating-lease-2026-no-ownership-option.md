# Operating Lease 2026 - No Ownership Option
Operating Lease offers equipment use without ownership. Here's what Malaysian businesses need to know in 2026.
## What is Operating Lease?
Operating Lease means:
- Rent equipment monthly
- No ownership at end
- Return or upgrade option
- Lower monthly commitment
## Operating Lease vs Hire Purchase 2026
| Feature | Operating Lease | Hire Purchase |
|---------|----------------|---------------|
| **Ownership** | No | Yes (after payment) |
| **Down Payment** | 0% | 0-20% |
| **Monthly Cost** | Lower | Higher |
| **Contract Term** | 1-5 years | 3-7 years |
| **Equipment Age** | New preferred | Any age OK |
| **Tax Benefit** | Full deductible | Interest deductible |
## Real Example: Forklift
**Price:** RM 45,000
**Operating Lease (3 years):**
- Monthly: RM 1,200
- Total paid: RM 43,200
- Ownership: No (return or renew)
**Hire Purchase (5 years):**
- Monthly: RM 900
- Total paid: RM 54,000
- Ownership: Yes after final payment
## Who Should Choose Operating Lease?
**Best for:**
- Short-term projects (1-2 years)
- Seasonal demand businesses
- Testing equipment before buying
- Wanting regular equipment upgrades
- Prefer off-balance-sheet financing
**Industries:**
- Peak season logistics
- Construction tender projects
- Event management companies
