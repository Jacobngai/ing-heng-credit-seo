# Finance Lease 2026 - Flexible Terms Malaysia
Finance Lease offers middle ground between operating lease and hire purchase. Here's what you need to know in 2026.
## What is Finance Lease?
Finance Lease means:
- Lower monthly payments
- Balloon payment at end
- Ownership option available
- Flexible term structure
## Finance Lease vs Other Options 2026
| Feature | Finance Lease | Operating Lease | Hire Purchase |
|---------|--------------|----------------|---------------|
| **Monthly Payment** | Lower | Lowest | Higher |
| **Final Payment** | Balloon | None | None |
| **Ownership** | Optional | No | Yes |
| **Term** | 3-7 years | 1-5 years | 5-7 years |
| **Equipment Age** | Any | New preferred | Any age |
## Real Example: Excavator
**Price:** RM 180,000
**Finance Lease (5 years + 20% balloon):**
- Monthly: RM 2,400
- Balloon (end): RM 36,000
- Total paid: RM 180,000
- Option: Pay balloon to own OR return
**Hire Purchase (5 years, no balloon):**
- Monthly: RM 3,000
- Total paid: RM 180,000
- Ownership: Automatic
## Who Should Choose Finance Lease?
**Best for:**
- Wanting lower monthly payments
- Planning to refinance balloon payment
- Uncertain about long-term need
- Wanting ownership option but not commitment
- Cash flow sensitive businesses
## 2026 Finance Lease Structure
**Standard Balloon Options:**
- 10% balloon = Lower final payment
- 20% balloon = Balanced payment
- 30% balloon = Lowest monthly
**Term Options:**
- 3 years + balloon
- 5 years + balloon
- 7 years + balloon
## Finance Lease End Options
At end of term, choose to:
1. **Pay balloon** - Own equipment
2. **Refinance balloon** - Continue using
3. **Return equipment** - Walk away
4. **Extend lease** - Keep using temporarily
