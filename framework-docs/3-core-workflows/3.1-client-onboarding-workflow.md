# 3.1 Client Onboarding Workflow

## Overview

**What it does:** Transforms a client from first contact to fully operational SEO website with brand identity, content strategy, and deployment infrastructure - all in 3-5 business days.

**Why it matters:** A systematic onboarding process ensures every client receives consistent quality, complete strategic planning, and a strong SEO foundation from day one. This workflow sets up long-term success.

**Key deliverables:**
- Keyword research (3 languages)
- Buyer personas (5-8 personas)
- Strategic brief (comprehensive strategy document)
- Brand identity (colors, typography, voice)
- Multi-language website (deployed to 3 domains)
- Production-ready infrastructure

---

## Time Estimates

| Phase | Duration | Notes |
|-------|----------|-------|
| **Initial Contact to Questionnaire** | 2-3 hours | Client fills out 30-question form |
| **Phase 1: Draft Generation** | 5 minutes | AI-powered, fully automated |
| **Phase 2: Employee Review** | 45-60 minutes | Human quality control |
| **Phase 3: Finalization** | 8-10 minutes | Converting drafts to production |
| **Phase 4: Brand Identity** | 10-15 minutes | Visual identity generation |
| **Phase 5: Website Setup** | 5-8 minutes | Astro website scaffolding |
| **Phase 6: Vercel Deployment** | 15-20 minutes | 3 projects, 3 domains |
| **Total Timeline** | 3-5 business days | From first contact to live website |

## Complete Step-by-Step Process

### STEP 1: Initial Client Contact and Questionnaire

**When:** First day of engagement

**Who:** Sales/Account Manager

**What to do:**

1. **Send client questionnaire** (30 questions covering):
   - Business information
   - Target audience
   - Services/products
   - Competitors (top 5 with websites)
   - SEO goals (keywords, languages, objectives)
   - Brand voice (tone, topics to avoid, content preferences)

2. **Collect supporting materials:**
   - Company logo (PNG, transparent background preferred)
   - Brand guidelines (if available)
   - Existing content samples
   - Competitor websites for analysis
   - Access to Google Analytics/Search Console (if existing)

3. **Schedule kickoff call** (optional but recommended)

**Expected output:**
- Completed questionnaire (Word doc or Google Form)
- Logo files
- Competitor list

**Time:** 2-3 hours (client time to complete)

**Success criteria:**
- All 30 questions answered thoroughly
- At least 3-5 competitor websites identified
- Clear understanding of business goals

**Common mistakes to avoid:**
- Don't skip competitors section (critical for keyword gap analysis)
- Don't accept vague answers - push for specifics
- Don't proceed without clear language preferences

### STEP 3: Phase 2 - Employee Review and Approval

**When:** Day 2, immediately after draft generation

**Who:** SEO Specialist/Employee

**What to do:**

**Review Keyword Research (15-20 minutes):**

1. Open each keyword file in `keyword-research/D/`
2. For each language, verify:
   - Keywords are relevant to client business
   - Keywords are researched independently (not translated)
   - SERP analysis is present
   - Search volume estimates are reasonable
   - LSI keywords are included
   - Content gaps identified

3. **Decision tree:**
   - **Good quality:** Rename folder from `D/` to `A/` (approved)
   - **Needs minor edits:** Edit file, then rename to `A/`
   - **Poor quality:** Rename to `F/` (failed), regenerate

**Review Personas (20-25 minutes):**

1. Open each persona file in `persona-idea/D/`
2. For each persona, verify:
   - Realistic and specific (not generic)
   - Culturally appropriate for language
   - Pain points are specific to industry
   - Buying behavior is well-defined
   - Content preferences are clear
   - Buyer journey is mapped

3. **Decision tree:**
   - **Good persona:** Rename from `D/` to `A/`
   - **Needs edits:** Edit, then rename to `A/`
   - **Generic/poor:** Rename to `F/`, regenerate

**Review Strategic Brief (10-15 minutes):**

1. Open `brief-draft/D/brief.md`
2. Verify all 11 sections are complete:
   - Company Overview
   - Target Audience
   - SEO Strategy
   - Content Strategy
   - Brand Voice
   - Content Quality Standards
   - Conversion & Contact
   - Success Metrics
   - Technical SEO Setup
   - Implementation Timeline
   - Notes & Special Considerations

3. **Decision tree:**
   - **Complete and strategic:** Rename to `A/`
   - **Missing sections:** Edit, complete, rename to `A/`
   - **Incorrect strategy:** Rename to `F/`, regenerate

**Expected output:**
- All good files renamed from `D/` to `A/`
- Poor files renamed to `F/` (if any)
- Client folder ready for finalization

**Time:** 45-60 minutes total

**Success criteria:**
- 80%+ of drafts approved without edits
- All approved files are high quality
- Strategic brief is actionable

**Common mistakes to avoid:**
- Don't approve without reading thoroughly
- Don't skip SERP verification for keywords
- Don't accept generic personas
- Don't approve brief with missing sections

## Common Mistakes and Troubleshooting

### Issue: Draft generation takes too long (>10 minutes)

**Cause:** Large questionnaire, complex industry
**Solution:** Split into smaller batches, optimize prompts

### Issue: Keywords are translated, not researched independently

**Cause:** AI misunderstanding instructions
**Solution:** Regenerate with explicit "no translation" instruction in command

### Issue: Personas are generic and not culturally specific

**Cause:** Insufficient questionnaire details
**Solution:** Request more specific target audience information from client

### Issue: Website build fails with dependency errors

**Cause:** Missing packages, version conflicts
**Solution:**
```bash
rm -rf node_modules package-lock.json
npm install
npm run build
```

### Issue: Vercel deployment fails with environment variable errors

**Cause:** Missing or incorrect environment variables
**Solution:** Verify all 4 required variables are set:
- `PUBLIC_DEFAULT_LOCALE`
- `DEFAULT_LOCALE`
- `PUBLIC_SITE_URL`
- `SITE_URL`

### Issue: Site defaults to wrong language after deployment

**Cause:** Incorrect `PUBLIC_DEFAULT_LOCALE` value
**Solution:** Redeploy with correct environment variable

### Issue: SSL certificate not active after domain configuration

**Cause:** DNS propagation delay
**Solution:** Wait 24-48 hours for DNS propagation, verify DNS settings
